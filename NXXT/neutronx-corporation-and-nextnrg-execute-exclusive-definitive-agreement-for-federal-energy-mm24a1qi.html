Summary

NeutronX Corporation and NextNRG, Inc. (NASDAQ: NXXT) executed a binding two-year exclusive cooperation agreement effective February 18, 2026, designating NextNRG as the exclusive technology and execution partner for government contracts secured by NeutronX. The partnership targets federal energy and defense infrastructure projects across military installations, airports, and critical facilities nationwide. NextNRG will act as lead contractor providing AI-optimized microgrids, battery storage, wireless EV charging, solar, and mobile fueling; NeutronX will serve as prime contractor and contribute AI and federal procurement expertise for predictive maintenance, threat detection, and system optimization. Both parties retain their proprietary technologies. No financial terms, contract values, or guaranteed awards were disclosed.
