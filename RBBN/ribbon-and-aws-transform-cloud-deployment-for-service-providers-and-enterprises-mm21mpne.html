Summary

Ribbon Communications (NASDAQ: RBBN) announced a strategic collaboration agreement with Amazon Web Services to deliver a cloud-native secure voice communications solution available on AWS Marketplace. Ribbon is containerizing its SBC CNe, PSX policy and routing engine, and RAMP centralized management platform for AWS Elastic Kubernetes Service (EKS) to enable lifecycle automation, scalability, and lower OPEX/CAPEX for service providers and enterprises. The collaboration includes joint development and customer engagement, integration with Amazon Bedrock for AI-enabled capabilities, and is reported to be in production with a Fortune 500 customer; Aircall provided a customer testimonial. The release discloses no financial terms or revenue guidance.
