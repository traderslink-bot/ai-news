Summary

Press release: TOP Ships Inc. reports management's estimate of net asset value (NAV) of $289 million as of December 31, 2025, based on third-party broker charter-free vessel value estimates plus debt and cash (including the company's 50% joint venture vessel values). NAV per common share is $58.81 on a basic basis and $45.13 fully diluted (assuming exercise of 1,488,478 outstanding warrants). Management states the company is trading at a 91.2% discount to the fully diluted NAV. Company fleet average age ~4.8 years; time charters with high-quality customers are generating positive cash flow, with earliest charter expiring Q1 2028 and latest expiring Q3 2031.
