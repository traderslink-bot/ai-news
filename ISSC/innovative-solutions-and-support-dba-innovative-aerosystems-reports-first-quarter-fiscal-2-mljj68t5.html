Summary

Press release: Innovative Solutions & Support, Inc. (NASDAQ: ISSC) reported Q1 FY2026 results for the quarter ended December 31, 2025. Revenue was $21.8M, up 36.5% year-over-year; gross profit $11.9M and gross margin 54.5%. Net income was $4.1M ($0.22 diluted); Adjusted Net Income $4.5M ($0.25). EBITDA and Adjusted EBITDA were $7.4M (EBITDA up ~172%, Adjusted EBITDA up ~141%). Operating cash flow was $8.2M and free cash flow $7.0M. New orders for the quarter were ~$19M and backlog was ~$75M. Total debt was $23.8M, cash $8.3M, net debt $15.5M and net debt / trailing 12-month Adjusted EBITDA was 0.5x. Company completed integration of F-16 component production into its Exton facility and cited increased commercial aftermarket demand and expected defense ramp during FY2026. Total available liquidity was $83.3M.

Positives
  • Revenue growth of 36.5% to $21.8M year-over-year
  • Gross margin expanded to 54.5% (from 41.4% prior-year quarter)
  • Net income increased to $4.1M ($0.22 diluted) and Adjusted Net Income to $4.5M ($0.25)
  • EBITDA and Adjusted EBITDA of $7.4M, substantial year-over-year increases
  • Strong operating cash flow of $8.2M and free cash flow of $7.0M
  • Net debt reduced to $15.5M and leverage improved to 0.5x
  • Available liquidity of $83.3M (cash plus credit availability)
  • Backlog of approximately $75M and $19M of new orders in the quarter
  • Completed integration of F-16 production into Exton facility to support future margin improvements
Negatives
  • F-16 platform revenue was lower during the quarter due to transition of manufacturing to the Exton facility and timing of customer shipments
  • Operating expenses increased slightly to $5.6M from $5.3M year-over-year
  • Total debt of $23.8M remains on the balance sheet (offset by cash to yield net debt)
