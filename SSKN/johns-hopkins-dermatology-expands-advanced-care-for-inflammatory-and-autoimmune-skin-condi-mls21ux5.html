Summary

STRATA Skin Sciences (NASDAQ: SSKN) announced that Johns Hopkins Dermatology has added the XTRAC® 308 nm excimer laser to its clinical offerings. The device will be used to treat chronic inflammatory and autoimmune skin diseases (psoriasis, vitiligo, atopic dermatitis) and to support ongoing clinical research and academic initiatives. The release highlights prior adoption of XTRAC by leading academic centers, cites over 300 peer‑reviewed studies supporting excimer therapy, and reiterates STRATA's partnership model (fee‑per‑treatment rather than equipment sale). No financial terms, purchase counts, or revenue impact were disclosed. A Safe Harbor statement notes forward‑looking risks and refers investors to the company's SEC filings.
