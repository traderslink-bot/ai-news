Summary

This is a press release from BioRestorative Therapies, Inc. (NASDAQ: BRTX) announcing that IP Australia has allowed Patent Application No. 2020265664 covering non-naturally occurring three-dimensional brown adipose-derived stem cell (BADSC) aggregates and methods of making them. The allowance provides expanded intellectual property protection for the company's allogeneic, off-the-shelf ThermoStem® technology aimed at treating obesity and metabolic disorders. The claims emphasized include a method of making 3D BADSC aggregates. The release reiterates the company’s metabolic and disc/spine development programs, its commercial BioCosmeceutical platform, and warns that statements are forward-looking and subject to risks.
