Summary

Press release reporting preliminary unaudited FY2025 consolidated results for Jinko Solar Co., Ltd. (Jiangxi Jinko) prepared under PRC GAAP. Key metrics: revenue RMB65.49 billion (down 29.18% YoY); operating loss RMB9.11 billion; net loss attributable to shareholders RMB6.79 billion (vs. net profit RMB98.9 million in 2024); net loss excluding non-recurring items RMB7.64 billion. Total assets RMB119.16 billion and total equity attributable to parent RMB25.46 billion as of Dec 31, 2025. JinkoSolar Holding Co., Ltd. owns ~55.59% of Jiangxi Jinko. Company attributes results primarily to lower selling prices for photovoltaic products and cautions results are preliminary, unaudited, and prepared under PRC GAAP which may differ from JinkoSolar's US GAAP consolidated results.
