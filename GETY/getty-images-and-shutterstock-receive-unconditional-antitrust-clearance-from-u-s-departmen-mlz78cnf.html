Summary

Getty Images (NYSE: GETY) and Shutterstock (NYSE: SSTK) announced the U.S. Department of Justice has completed its Hart-Scott-Rodino review and the waiting period expired without conditions, providing unconditional DOJ antitrust clearance for their proposed merger of equals. The companies said the transaction is expected to deliver substantial SG&A and CAPEX synergies. The U.K. Competition and Markets Authority remains in a Phase 2 review and issued an interim report on Feb. 19; its final decision is due by April 19. The release notes prior SEC filings: a Form S-4 registration statement (declared effective April 30, 2025) and a final prospectus filed April 30, 2025. The release contains standard forward-looking statements and references additional SEC filings for risks and details.
