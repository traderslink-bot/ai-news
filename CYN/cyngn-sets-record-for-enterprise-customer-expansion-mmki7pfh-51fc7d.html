Summary

Cyngn announced a record expansion of DriveMod Tugger deployments among existing enterprise and Fortune 100 customers, adding more vehicles and extending autonomous operations across additional routes, workflows, and facilities. The company reported a 2025 increase in DriveMod Tugger sales (threefold vs 2024), higher autonomous activity across its installed base (total autonomous operating time up 113% in the second half of 2025), and a deployment at a WEG electric motor facility. NVIDIA Isaac Sim supported platform development. Management expects Q1 2026 sales to exceed 2025 annual sales. DriveMod Tugger operates on Motrec MT-160 Tuggers and BYD forklifts, with typical payback under two years.
