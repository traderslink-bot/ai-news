Summary

Lifetime Brands (NASDAQ: LCUT) reported Q4 2025 net sales of $204.1M, down 5.2% year-over-year, and full-year net sales of $647.9M, down 5.1%. Q4 gross margin was $78.8M (38.6%); income from operations for the quarter was $20.0M vs. $15.5M in Q4 2024. Q4 net income was $18.2M ($0.83 diluted), versus $8.9M ($0.41) in Q4 2024; full-year net loss was $26.9M ($1.24) vs. a $15.2M loss in 2024. Adjusted EBITDA for 2025 was $50.8M. The Board declared a quarterly dividend of $0.0425 per share payable May 15, 2026 (record May 1, 2026). The company noted a $33.237M goodwill impairment for 2025 and provided a conference call schedule and intent to issue full-year 2026 guidance with Q1 results in mid-May.
