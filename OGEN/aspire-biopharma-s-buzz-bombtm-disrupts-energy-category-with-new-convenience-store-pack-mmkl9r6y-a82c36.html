Summary

Aspire Biopharma Holdings, Inc. (Nasdaq: ASBP) announced the launch of BUZZ BOMB™ Convenience Store Pack through its Buzz Bomb Caffeine Company, expanding retail presence into convenience stores. The six-count Fruit Blast lineup contains two each of Tropical Fruit, Mixed Berry, and Peach Mango. Each stick pack delivers 50 mg of caffeine and is a dry powder designed to be sprinkled under the tongue. The product will be available in select locations starting in May. BUZZ BOMB™ is currently offered in Tropical Fruit, Mixed Berry, Peach Mango, and Coffee Mocha flavors. The company positions the product as a rapid, portable caffeine option for athletes, professionals, and students.
