Summary

Dermata Therapeutics, Inc. named Kyra Peckaitis as Vice President, Marketing to lead the launch of Tome Skincare. Peckaitis will oversee marketing strategy, brand development, and consumer engagement as Tome prepares its first commercial products for mid-2026. The appointment follows her prior roles at Coterie, HubSpot, and Sweetgreen. Dermata granted Peckaitis a non-qualified stock option to purchase 15,000 shares at $1.33 per share, vesting over one year with monthly installments, approved as a material inducement under Nasdaq Listing Rule 5635(c)(4). Dermata is pivoting to direct-to-consumer skincare solutions and plans to launch Tome products in mid-2026.
