Summary

Energy Vault (NYSE: NRGV) issued unaudited preliminary results for Q4 and full-year 2025 and announced a convertible securities offering. Q4 2025 revenue is expected to be $150.0M–$155.0M (≈+355% YoY) with GAAP gross profit of $28M–$33M and gross margin of 18%–22%. Q4 2025 adjusted EBITDA is expected to be positive $5.0M–$10.0M and Q4 net loss expected to be $22.1M–$9.5M (vs. $61.8M in Q4 2024). Full-year 2025 revenue is expected to be $200.0M–$205.0M (≈+335% YoY) with GAAP gross margin 22%–25% and adjusted EBITDA negative $26.0M to $21.0M. Cash at year-end was ~$103.4M. The company will report final audited results and hold a conference call on March 17, 2026.

Positives
  • Q4 2025 revenue expected at $150.0M–$155.0M, ~355% YoY improvement
  • Q4 2025 adjusted EBITDA expected positive $5.0M–$10.0M (improvement vs. prior year)
  • Significant improvement in GAAP gross margin (Q4 18%–22%; full-year 22%–25%)
  • Year-end cash reported at ~$103.4M, more than 3x YoY and +67% sequentially
  • Full-year 2025 revenue within the company's original 2025 guidance range
Negatives
  • Full-year 2025 adjusted EBITDA remains negative ($26.0M to $21.0M)
  • Q4 2025 net loss still expected between $22.1M and $9.5M
  • Company announced a convertible securities offering (potential for dilution)
  • Results are preliminary and unaudited and subject to change pending final reporting
