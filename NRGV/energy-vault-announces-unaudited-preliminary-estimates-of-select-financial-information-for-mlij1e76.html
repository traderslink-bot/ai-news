Summary

Energy Vault (NYSE: NRGV) issued unaudited preliminary estimates for Q4 and FY2025: Q4 revenue $150.0M–$155.0M (≈+355% YoY), GAAP gross profit $28M–$33M (gross margin 18%–22%), adjusted EBITDA $5M–$10M (positive vs. Q4 2024 loss), and Q4 net loss $9.5M–$22.1M (vs. $61.8M in Q4 2024). Full-year 2025 revenue expected $200M–$205M (≈+335% YoY, within prior guidance) with GAAP gross margin 22%–25% and adjusted EBITDA of roughly −$26M to −$21M. Cash on hand at year-end about $103.4M (≈3x YoY, +67% sequential). The company announced a convertible securities offering and will report final Q4/FY2025 results and host a conference call on March 17, 2026. Results are preliminary and subject to change.

Positives
  • Q4 2025 revenue guidance $150.0M–$155.0M, ~355% YoY growth and ~5x sequential increase from Q3 2025
  • Q4 2025 adjusted EBITDA forecast positive $5M–$10M versus a loss in Q4 2024
  • Full-year 2025 revenue expected $200M–$205M, within prior guidance and ~335% YoY growth
  • Year-end cash ~ $103.4M, ~3x YoY and up 67% sequentially
  • Significant improvement in GAAP gross margin (Q4: 18%–22%, FY: 22%–25%)
Negatives
  • Full-year 2025 adjusted EBITDA still expected negative (~−$26M to −$21M)
  • Company still expects a net loss (Q4 net loss $9.5M–$22.1M; FY net loss remains material)
  • Announcement accompanies a convertible securities offering (potential dilution and financing risk)
  • Results are unaudited and preliminary and may change upon completion of audit and Form 10-K filing
