Summary

Getty Images Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: GETY) and Shutterstock, Inc. (NYSE: SSTK) announced the U.S. Department of Justice concluded its review and the Hart-Scott-Rodino waiting period expired without conditions for their proposed merger of equals. Management said the DOJ clearance is a significant step toward closing; the companies expect substantial SG&A and CAPEX synergies post-close. The UK Competition and Markets Authority is conducting an ongoing Phase 2 review (interim report issued Feb. 19) with a final decision due April 19. Getty filed a Form S-4 (preliminary S-4 filed March 31, 2025; pre-effective amendment Form S-4/A April 28, 2025), which was declared effective April 30, 2025, and a final prospectus was filed April 30, 2025.
