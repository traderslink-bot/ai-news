Summary

Leifras Co., Ltd. (Nasdaq: LFS) announced a school partner agreement with non-profit Blaublitz Akita to acquire and operate the Blaublitz Akita Soccer School Odate Branch, launching the "Blaublitz Akita × Liberta Soccer School" in Odate City starting April 1, 2026. The deal grants Leifras brand usage rights (name and uniforms), transfers full school operations and know-how to Leifras, and includes planned member benefits such as complimentary J.League tickets and periodic technical instruction from professional players/coaches. The alliance marks Leifras' first entry into Akita Prefecture and expands its sports school network to 46 of Japan's 47 prefectures. No financial terms were disclosed; the release contains forward-looking statements and references SEC filings for risk factors.
