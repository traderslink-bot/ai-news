Summary

Check-Cap Ltd. (NASDAQ: MBAI) and MBody AI Corp. have submitted a Nasdaq listing application as part of the merger close. The combined company is targeted to list on Nasdaq Capital Markets in the first half of 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. Check-Cap has regained Nasdaq compliance, and MBody AI’s audited financials for 2024 and the first half of 2025 were furnished. A Form F-1 relating to the equity line of credit facility with ARC Group International was filed. Management stated progress toward closing and public-market readiness, while noting the closing remains subject to regulatory approvals and closing conditions.

Positives
  • Nasdaq listing application submitted for merger close
  • Targeted Nasdaq Capital Markets listing for the combined company in 1H 2026, subject to closing conditions
  • Check-Cap regained Nasdaq compliance
  • Audited financial statements furnished for 2024 year-end and 2025 interim period
  • Form F-1 Registration Statement filed in connection with ARC Group International equity line of credit
Negatives
