Summary

Rubico Inc. has entered into an agreement to acquire 100% of the shares of a Marshall Islands SPV that is party to a shipbuilding contract for a 47,499 dwt MR product/chemical tanker to be delivered in 2029. The SPV will be financed by ABC Financial Leasing Co., Ltd. and has secured a seven-year firm time charter with a major oil trader (with a four-year extension option). The total potential gross revenue backlog is about $75 million. Purchase price for the SPV shares is about $4.2 million. The transaction is a related-party deal approved by Rubico’s independent Transaction Committee, which received a fairness opinion. Closing is subject to customary conditions and financing arrangements.

Positives
  • Firm time charter for seven years with a major oil trader, with a four-year extension option.
  • Potential gross revenue backlog of about $75 million (including optional years).
  • Purchase price for the SPV shares is about $4.2 million.
  • Financing arrangements in place via ABC Financial Leasing Co., Ltd. to cover the majority of the vessel price.
  • Transaction approved by an independent Transaction Committee with a fairness opinion.
Negatives
  • Related-party nature of the acquisition.
  • Closing subject to customary conditions and the completion of financing arrangements.
  • Delivery of the vessel scheduled for 2029; long lead time increases exposure to project risk.
  • Financing relies on the SPV’s execution of guarantees and other contingencies.
