Summary

Rubico Inc. (NASDAQ: RUBI) entered into an agreement to acquire 100% of an SPV that is the shipbuilding contract counterparty for a 47,499 dwt ECO MR product/chemical tanker being built by Guangzhou Shipyard International, scheduled for delivery in 2029. The purchase price for the SPV is approximately $4.2 million. The SPV is finalizing lease financing with ABC Financial Leasing Co., Ltd. to cover the majority of the vessel price; closing is subject to customary conditions and issuance of a refund guarantee and Rubico providing a corporate guarantee. The seller, Central Mare Inc. (an affiliate of Evangelos Pistiolis), has secured a time charter with a major oil trader for a 7-year firm term with a 4-year option, producing a potential gross revenue backlog of about $75 million including options. Because the transaction is related-party, it was approved by a special independent Transaction Committee, which obtained a fairness opinion.
