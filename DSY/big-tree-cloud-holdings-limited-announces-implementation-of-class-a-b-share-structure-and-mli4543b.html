Summary

Big Tree Cloud Holdings Limited (NASDAQ: DSY) announced shareholder approval of a 1-for-20 share consolidation (reverse stock split) and the adoption of a dual-class (Class A/Class B) share structure. The Company's Class A ordinary shares are expected to begin trading on The Nasdaq Capital Market under the symbol DSY with a new CUSIP (G1263B132) at the open on February 12, 2026. The consolidation rounded fractional shares up to whole shares; authorised share capital was reallocated to 20,000,000 Class A and 5,000,000 Class B ordinary shares. Existing issued ordinary shares were redesignated (including 3,500,000 issued shares redesignated as Class B held by Ploutos Group), and outstanding options/warrants will be adjusted per their terms. The company said the changes aim to strengthen its market position and provide management stability as it pursues growth and AI initiatives.

Positives
  • Shareholders approved 1-for-20 share consolidation, reducing outstanding ordinary share count via reverse split.
  • Class A ordinary shares to resume trading on Nasdaq under ticker DSY with new CUSIP G1263B132 effective Feb 12, 2026.
  • Authorized capital restructured to 20,000,000 Class A and 5,000,000 Class B ordinary shares, enabling the dual-class framework.
  • Company states the changes are intended to strengthen market position and provide management stability to pursue growth and AI expansion.
  • All outstanding options, warrants and similar securities will be adjusted in accordance with their terms.
Negatives
  • Implementation of a dual-class share structure results in redesignation that concentrates 3,500,000 issued Class B ordinary shares in PLOUTOS GROUP LIMITED.
  • Reverse stock split (1-for-20) materially reduces the number of outstanding shares, which can affect trading metrics and share count.
  • Authorized share capital and share class redesignation alter the company's capital structure and investor rights framework.
