Summary

Foresight Autonomous Holdings Ltd. (Nasdaq and TASE: FRSX) announced it will change its ADS ratio from 1 ADS = 30 Ordinary Shares to 1 ADS = 90 Ordinary Shares, effective February 26, 2026. The exchange will be automatic for uncertificated ADSs in DRS and DTC; holders of certificated ADSs must surrender ADSs to BNY Mellon for cancellation and will receive one new ADS for every three existing ADSs. Ordinary Shares, shareholder ownership percentages and voting power will not change. ADSs will continue to trade on Nasdaq under the symbol FRSX with a new CUSIP. The company will file a post-effective amendment on Form F-6. The company expects the ADS price to increase proportionally but gives no assurance, and fractional entitlements will be aggregated and sold for cash (net of fees, taxes and expenses).

Positives
  • ADS ratio change reduces ADS count by a factor of three (1:30 -> 1:90), consolidating ADSs
  • Company expects ADS price to increase proportionally following the ratio change
  • No change to Ordinary Shares, ownership percentages or voting power (no dilution)
  • ADS will continue trading under ticker FRSX on Nasdaq; company will file Form F-6 amendment
Negatives
  • Company gives no assurance the ADS price will be equal to or greater than proportionate value after the change
  • Fractional new ADSs will not be issued; fractional entitlements will be sold and proceeds distributed net of fees, taxes and expenses
  • Certificated ADS holders are required to surrender ADSs to the depositary (administrative burden)
  • New CUSIP will be issued (administrative/recordkeeping update for holders and brokers)
