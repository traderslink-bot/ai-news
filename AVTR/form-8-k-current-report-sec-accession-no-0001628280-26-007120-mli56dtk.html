Summary

Avantor, Inc. (CIK 1722482) filed a Form 8-K on February 11, 2026 (Items 2.02, 7.01 and 9.01) to report results of operations and financial condition. The filing attaches an earnings release for fourth quarter and full year 2025 (Exhibit 99.1), a document describing a segment realignment (Exhibit 99.2), and XBRL/data and graphic exhibits. The 8-K serves as Regulation FD disclosure of the earnings release and related materials; investors should review Exhibits 99.1 and 99.2 for the full financial details and specifics of the segment changes.

Positives
  • Timely Form 8-K filing disclosing Q4 and full-year 2025 results via an attached earnings release (Exhibit 99.1).
  • Company provided a separate exhibit describing a segment realignment (Exhibit 99.2), increasing transparency on reporting changes.
  • Includes XBRL instance and taxonomy extension files and graphic exhibits for data accessibility.
  • Regulation FD disclosure ensures the results were publicly released and accessible to all investors.
Negatives
  • The body of the 8-K does not include detailed financial figures or metrics; investors must open Exhibit 99.1 for numbers and analysis.
  • Segment realignment described in Exhibit 99.2 could affect comparability of historical results and future segment reporting; details require review in the exhibit.
