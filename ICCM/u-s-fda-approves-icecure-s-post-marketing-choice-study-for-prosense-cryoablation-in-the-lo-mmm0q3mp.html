Summary

IceCure Medical (Nasdaq: ICCM) announced the FDA has approved the study design for its ChoICE post-market study of the ProSense® cryoablation system for low-risk breast cancer. Patient enrollment is expected to begin H2 2026 with at least 80 patients in year one and approximately 400 patients across ~30 U.S. clinical sites within 36 months. Sites may provide ProSense® commercially to non-study patients, the Study will use cryoprobes sold exclusively by IceCure, and procedures are eligible under an established CPT Category III code (facility fee ~ $4,000). The FDA previously granted marketing authorization in Oct 2025 for ProSense® for patients aged 70+ with biologically low-risk tumors ≤1.5 cm treated with adjuvant endocrine therapy. Dr. Robert Ward and Dr. Nathalie Johnson will serve as Co-Principal Investigators.
