Summary

Perpetuals.com announced the launch of Ledgera, a cross-chain settlement layer, and PerpetualPay.Net, a non-custodial crypto payment gateway. The platforms offer quantum-resilient security and claim up to 100x lower transaction costs compared with certain traditional blockchain operations. Ledgera supports Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, and stablecoins (USDC, USDT) with institutional-grade performance and rapid settlements; PerpetualPay.Net enables non-custodial crypto payments to users’ wallets with multi-chain support, automated compliance, and white-label options, built atop Ledgera. Both integrate with Perpetuals Vault for self-custody and asset protection, and are commercially available via perpetuals.com.

  • Quantum-resilient security across both platforms.

  • White-label ready for EMIs, PSPs, brokers, and platforms; optional DEX swaps and licensed fiat on/off-ramps.
  • Integration with Perpetuals Vault (quantum-resilient custody) with warm/cold storage and multi-sig.
