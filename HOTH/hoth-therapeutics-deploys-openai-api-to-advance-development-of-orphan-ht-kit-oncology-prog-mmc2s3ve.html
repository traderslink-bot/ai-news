Summary

Press release: Hoth Therapeutics (NASDAQ: HOTH) has integrated the OpenAI API into its HT-KIT development workflow to support IND-enabling activities, including preclinical data analysis, molecular modeling, and regulatory documentation in advance of IND submission. Reported preclinical highlights: >80% reduction in KIT mRNA/protein across in vitro and in vivo models (systemic mastocytosis and GIST), no dose-limiting toxicities observed in reported preclinical studies, statistically significant tumor-volume reductions in xenograft models by Day 8, and completion of GLP-validated bioanalytical methods to support PK, biodistribution, and exposure-response analyses. HT-KIT has Orphan Drug Designation and is advancing toward IND submission and Phase 1 clinical evaluation.
