Summary

Hoth Therapeutics announced deployment of the OpenAI API to support IND-enabling development of HT-KIT, its orphan-designated therapy targeting KIT-driven cancers. The OpenAI-powered platform is integrated into the HT-KIT development workflow to assist preclinical data analysis, molecular modeling of KIT pathways, and regulatory documentation ahead of IND submission. Preclinical results show >80% reduction of KIT mRNA/protein in vitro and in vivo (systemic mastocytosis and GIST), favorable tolerability with no dose-limiting toxicities observed, and rapid anti-tumor activity in xenograft models with tumor-volume reduction by Day 8. GLP-validated bioanalytical methods have been completed to support PK, biodistribution, and exposure-response analyses for IND. HT-KIT is advancing toward IND submission and Phase 1; Orphan Drug Designation has been granted.
