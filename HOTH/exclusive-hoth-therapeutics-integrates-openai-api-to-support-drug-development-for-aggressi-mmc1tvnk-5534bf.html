Summary

Hoth Therapeutics deployed an OpenAI API platform to support IND-enabling development of HT-KIT for KIT-driven cancers. Preclinical data show over 80% reduction of KIT mRNA/protein in vitro and in vivo models of systemic mastocytosis and gastrointestinal stromal tumors, with no dose-limiting toxicities observed. In xenograft models, tumor-volume reduction by Day 8 was observed with apoptotic signaling consistent with KIT pathway knockdown. GLP-validated bioanalytical methods support pharmacokinetic, biodistribution, and exposure–response analyses ahead of IND submission. HT-KIT is advancing toward IND submission and Phase 1, and has been designated Orphan Drug. Additional preclinical data include a GDNF study for obesity and metabolic-associated steatotic liver disease; interim PK data from the CLEER-001 trial show 100% of evaluable cancer patients achieved clinical response by Week 6 with about 50% reduction in disease severity.
