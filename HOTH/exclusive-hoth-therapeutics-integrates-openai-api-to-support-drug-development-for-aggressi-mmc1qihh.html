Summary

Hoth Therapeutics announced deployment of an OpenAI-powered API into the HT-KIT development workflow to support IND-enabling preclinical data analysis, molecular modeling and regulatory documentation. HT-KIT preclinical data reportedly show >80% reduction of KIT mRNA/protein across in vitro and in vivo models, statistically significant tumor-volume reduction in xenografts by Day 8, and no dose-limiting toxicities to date. GLP-validated bioanalytical methods for PK, biodistribution and exposure-response analyses are completed. HT-KIT is progressing toward IND submission and Phase 1 evaluation and has Orphan Drug Designation. Separately, interim data from the CLEER-001 HT-001 PK cohort showed 100% of evaluable patients achieved clinical response by Week 6 with ~50% reduction in investigator-assessed disease severity. Article notes Hoth stock closed near $1.00.
