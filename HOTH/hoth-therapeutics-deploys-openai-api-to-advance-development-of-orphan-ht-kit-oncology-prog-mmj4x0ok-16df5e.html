Summary

Hoth Therapeutics deployed the OpenAI API to support IND-enabling development of HT-KIT, an orphan-designated therapy targeting KIT-driven cancers. The integration aids preclinical data analysis, molecular modeling of KIT pathways, and regulatory documentation ahead of IND submission. Preclinical results show KIT mRNA/protein reductions >80% across in vitro and in vivo models (systemic mastocytosis and GIST), favorable tolerability with no dose-limiting toxicities, and rapid anti-tumor activity with significant tumor-volume reduction by Day 8 in xenograft models. GLP-validated bioanalytical methods are complete to support PK, biodistribution, and exposure–response analyses. HT-KIT is advancing toward IND submission and Phase 1, and has received Orphan Drug Designation.
