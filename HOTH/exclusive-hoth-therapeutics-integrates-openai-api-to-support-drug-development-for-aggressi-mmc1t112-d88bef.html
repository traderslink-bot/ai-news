Summary

Hoth Therapeutics announced deployment of the OpenAI API to support IND-enabling development of HT-KIT for KIT-driven cancers, integrating AI into preclinical data analysis, molecular modeling, and regulatory documentation ahead of IND submission. HT-KIT showed over 80% reduction in KIT mRNA/protein in preclinical models of systemic mastocytosis and GIST; no dose-limiting toxicities reported; xenograft models showed tumor-volume reduction by Day 8 with apoptotic signaling consistent with KIT knockdown. GLP-validated methods support PK, biodistribution, and exposure–response analyses for IND. HT-KIT advancing toward IND submission and Phase 1, with Orphan Drug Designation. Additional preclinical data referenced include a GDNF obesity/metabolic liver disease study and interim results showing 100% clinical response by Week 6 in evaluable patients. HOTH stock closed at $0.997 on Tuesday.
