Summary

Hoth Therapeutics, Inc. (NASDAQ: HOTH) announced expansion of its CLEER-001 Phase 2a trial with the addition of Regis Clinical Research in Miami to accelerate enrollment. Management cites rising patient demand driven by positive interim HT-001 data: 100% of open-label cohort patients met the primary ARIGA rash severity endpoint (≤1) by week six, zero patients required EGFR inhibitor dose reductions or discontinuations while on HT-001, over 65% reported meaningful reductions in pain and itching, and no dose-limiting toxicities observed to date. A case study presented at the American Academy of Dermatology showed complete lesion and symptom resolution after one week of twice-daily application. The company says the new site will speed data collection and regulatory progress. The release includes customary forward-looking statement warnings.
