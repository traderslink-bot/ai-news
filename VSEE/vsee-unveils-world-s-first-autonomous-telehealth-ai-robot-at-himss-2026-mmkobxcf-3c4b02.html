Summary

VSee announced the launch of the VSee AI Robot, a fully autonomous telehealth robot designed for hospital deployment. It navigates to bedside without staff escort, enables remote rounds and specialist coverage, supports telestroke and rapid response, and delivers medications and supplies. The robot runs on the VSee AI Workflow Engine with no-code/low-code AI modules and a Building Blocks architecture for rapid configuration. Demonstrations and live demos were planned for HIMSS 2026, and the platform is claimed to deliver immediate ROI through improved staffing efficiency and expanded service lines. The release also notes forward-looking statements and Nasdaq listing risk.

  • Remote physician rounding and multisite specialist coverage (ED, ICU, med-surg)
  • Telestroke and rapid response with autonomous bedside dispatch
  • Programmable, secure medication and supply delivery
  • HIPAA-compliant, role-based access
  • VSee AI Workflow Engine enables 100+ clinical AI capabilities with no-code/low-code activation
