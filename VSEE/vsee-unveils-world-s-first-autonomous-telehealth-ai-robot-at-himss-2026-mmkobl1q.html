Summary

Press release: VSee (NASDAQ: VSEE) announced the VSee AI Robot, described as the world's first fully autonomous telehealth AI robot, launched at HIMSS 2026. The robot uses LiDAR navigation and 30x optical/IR night vision to travel autonomously to patient bedsides and supports remote physician rounding, telestroke rapid response, triage, and programmable secure medication/supply delivery. It is powered by VSee's no-code/low-code AI Workflow Engine, which the company says can activate 100+ clinical AI capabilities and integrate without rebuilding IT infrastructure. VSee cites platform scale (over 1.5 million video encounters/month; capacity 1,800+ appointments/day), claims immediate operational and financial ROI, and will demonstrate the robot at its HIMSS booth. The release includes standard forward-looking statements and notes potential risks including Nasdaq listing compliance.
