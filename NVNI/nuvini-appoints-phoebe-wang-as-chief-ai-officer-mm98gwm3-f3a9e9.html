Summary

Nuvini Group Limited (NASDAQ: NVNI) announced the appointment of Phoebe Wang as Chief Artificial Intelligence Officer, effective March 2, 2026. Wang transitions from the Board of Directors to lead enterprise-wide AI strategy, investments, and implementation, overseeing AI developments across the portfolio and aligning with the company's M&A strategy. The role builds on a partnership with Oracle and aims to standardize AI tools across portfolio companies, including Claude Code. Wang's background includes former Amazon Investment Partner and advisory work for Andrew Ng's AI Fund, with academic ties to UC Berkeley Haas School of Business. Wang has stepped down from the Board, and a replacement director will be evaluated in due course.

  • Leadership to develop and deploy AI capabilities across operations, aiming for efficiencies, product innovation, and cross-portfolio synergies
