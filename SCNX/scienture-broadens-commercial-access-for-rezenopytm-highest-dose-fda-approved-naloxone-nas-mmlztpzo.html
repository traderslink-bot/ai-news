Summary

Scienture Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: SCNX) announced multiple Group Purchasing Organization (GPO) agreements providing REZENOPY™ (naloxone HCl) Nasal Spray 10 mg access to more than 5,000 U.S. healthcare institutions — representing potential penetration of roughly 60% of the U.S. institutional market and expanded reach to EMS providers and rehabilitation centers. REZENOPY, the highest-dose FDA‑approved naloxone nasal spray (FDA approval April 19, 2024), targets a U.S. naloxone market estimated at $141 million and 9.4 million prescription units annually (IQVIA, MAT Jan 2026). Scienture LLC holds exclusive U.S. commercialization rights pursuant to a March 2025 agreement with Summit Biosciences (a Kindeva subsidiary).
