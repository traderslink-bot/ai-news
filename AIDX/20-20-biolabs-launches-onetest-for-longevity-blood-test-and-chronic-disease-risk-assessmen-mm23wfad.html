Summary

20/20 BioLabs (NASDAQ: AIDX) announced the commercial launch of OneTest for Longevity, an AI-enabled blood test and risk-assessment platform that integrates inflammatory biomarkers, dietary/lifestyle inputs, and curated scientific research to estimate individual risk for chronic diseases (e.g., diabetes, dementia, cardiovascular disease) and provide evidence-based dietary recommendations. The solution was developed with DAISource and leverages IBM watsonx.ai (Granite 4.0 foundation models) and the Docling toolkit for literature analysis. The test is offered as a laboratory-developed test (LDT) run in 20/20 BioLabs’ CLIA-licensed, CAP-accredited lab (not FDA-approved). The company noted the test is now available for purchase and referenced its recent Nasdaq listing on Feb 19, 2026 under ticker AIDX.
