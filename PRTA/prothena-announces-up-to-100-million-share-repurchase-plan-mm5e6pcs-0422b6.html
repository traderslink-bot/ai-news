Summary

Board authorized a share repurchase program allowing repurchases of up to $100 million of outstanding ordinary shares. Prothena had cash, cash equivalents and restricted cash of $308.4 million and no debt as of December 31, 2025, and expects approximately $255 million in cash at year-end 2026 excluding repurchases. The plan may be executed in open market transactions and may utilize Rule 10b5-1 plans; it expires December 31, 2026 and is not a commitment to repurchase. Potential aggregate clinical milestone payments of up to $105 million from Novo Nordisk and Bristol Myers Squibb in 2026 are noted. Forward-looking statements disclaimer applies.
