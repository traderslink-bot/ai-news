Summary

Prothena Corporation plc (NASDAQ: PRTA) announced its Board authorized a share repurchase program to buy up to $100.0 million of its ordinary shares through Dec 31, 2026. As of Dec 31, 2025 the company reported $308.4 million in cash, cash equivalents and restricted cash and no debt; excluding potential repurchases it expects to end the year with ~ $255 million in cash, cash equivalents and restricted cash. The company noted potential to earn up to $105 million of aggregate clinical milestone payments in 2026 from partners Novo Nordisk (coramitug) and Bristol Myers Squibb (PRX019). Repurchases may occur in open market transactions, potentially under Rule 10b-18 or via Rule 10b5-1 plans, may be suspended or discontinued at any time, and do not obligate Prothena to acquire any shares.
