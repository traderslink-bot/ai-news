Summary

Interactive Strength Inc. (NASDAQ: TRNR) completed its acquisition of Ergatta, acquiring 100% of the company. The base transaction value is $8.8 million (comprised of $1.8M cash at close, $1.8M in debt, and $5.3M in equity locked until May 2027) with maximum earn-out‑adjusted enterprise value of $19.5M. TRNR received $6.4M from Sportstech earlier and used cash on hand to fund the initial cash consideration. TRNR confirmed increased 2026 pro forma revenue guidance of more than $30M; Ergatta is expected to generate >$10M revenue in 2026 with roughly 70% recurring subscription revenue and ~30% EBITDA margin, and reported ~98.3% monthly retention. Ergatta founders and key management are expected to remain; TRNR cites expected near‑term accretion, operational cashflow generation from Ergatta, and potential synergies across TRNR brands.
