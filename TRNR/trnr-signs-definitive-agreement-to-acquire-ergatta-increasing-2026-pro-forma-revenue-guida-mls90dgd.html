Summary

Interactive Strength Inc. (NASDAQ: TRNR) signed a definitive agreement to acquire Ergatta, a Brooklyn-based game-based connected fitness company. TRNR says Ergatta is expected to generate >$10M revenue in 2026 with ~30% EBITDA margin and ~70% recurring subscription revenue (monthly retention ~98%). The deal consideration has a base enterprise value of $8.8M ( $1.8M cash at close, $1.8M debt, $5.3M equity locked until May 2027) with up to $10.8M of contingent earn-outs (maximum EV $19.5M) tied to 2026/2027 EBITDA targets; TRNR expects the transaction to close in Q1 2026 and to increase 2026 pro forma revenue guidance to >$30M.

Positives
  • Acquisition expected to raise 2026 pro forma revenue by ~50% to >$30M
  • Ergatta projected >$10M revenue in 2026 with approximately 70% recurring subscription revenue
  • High reported customer retention (~98.3% monthly) implying strong subscription stability
  • Ergatta expected ~30% EBITDA margin and asset-light, cash-generating model
  • Earn-out structure links significant portion of price to future performance (protects downside)
  • Founders and key management to remain with the business post-acquisition
Negatives
  • Majority of potential transaction value is contingent on future EBITDA targets (earn-outs)
  • Only $1.8M cash at close (less than 10% of transaction value funded at closing)
  • Significant portion of consideration ($5.3M + potential earn-out equity) issued as equity locked until May 2027 (potential dilution)
  • Transaction subject to customary closing conditions and not yet closed
