Summary

Gelteq and Healthy Extracts signed a Memorandum of Understanding to commercialize Gelteq's proprietary gel-based nutraceutical formulations by leveraging Healthy Extracts' U.S. manufacturing, sales and distribution capabilities. Under the MoU Healthy Extracts will manufacture and commercially sell gel-based products (including potential white-labeling) using Gelteq's technical transfer while Gelteq retains IP. The partnership targets scalable U.S. production and global distribution (including potential expansion into China and other markets). Companies intend to finalize a Manufacturing & Royalty Agreement and commercial terms; no financial terms or timelines were disclosed.

Positives
  • Establishes pathway to U.S.-based large-scale manufacturing and global distribution for Gelteq's gel delivery technology
  • Healthy Extracts will handle manufacturing, sales and distribution, enabling scalable production and supply-chain reliability
  • Gelteq retains ownership of its intellectual property while providing technical transfer
  • Partnership opens potential expansion into new product categories and international markets (e.g., China)
  • Collaboration leverages complementary strengths: Gelteq's formulation technology and Healthy Extracts' operational and commercial capabilities
Negatives
  • Announcement is an MoU; definitive Manufacturing & Royalty Agreement and commercial terms have not been finalized
  • No financial terms, timelines, production volumes or revenue/royalty structure disclosed
  • Execution risks and commercial/ regulatory challenges for new nutraceutical formats remain (not quantified)
  • Forward-looking statements highlight potential for outcomes to differ materially from expectations
