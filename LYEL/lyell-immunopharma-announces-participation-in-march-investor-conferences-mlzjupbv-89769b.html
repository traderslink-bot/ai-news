Summary

Lyell Immunopharma, Inc. (NASDAQ: LYEL), a clinical-stage CAR T-cell therapy company, announced that senior management will present and participate in three March 2026 investor conferences: TD Cowen 46th Annual Health Care Conference in Boston on March 2 at 9:10 a.m. ET; Leerink Annual Global Healthcare Conference in Miami on March 9 at 10:00 a.m. ET; and Citizens Life Science Conference in Miami on March 10 at 11:55 a.m. ET. Live webcasts will be available via the company’s Investors site, with a replay available on the site after each presentation.
