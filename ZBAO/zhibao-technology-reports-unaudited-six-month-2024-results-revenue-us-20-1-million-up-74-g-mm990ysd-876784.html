Summary

Zhibao Technology Inc. released unaudited financial results for the six months ended December 31, 2024, reporting revenue of US$20.1 million, gross profit of US$5.8 million, and net income of negative US$0.2 million. The company notes growth supported by its 2B2C embedded digital insurance solutions, partnerships with over 2,000 business channels, and serving more than 20 million customers.

Positives

  • Partnerships with over 2,000 business channels and more than 20 million customers served.
  • Proprietary PaaS-based digital insurance brokerage platform; described as the first in the 2B2C embedded digital insurance sector in China.
  • Unaudited financial results presented for the period.
