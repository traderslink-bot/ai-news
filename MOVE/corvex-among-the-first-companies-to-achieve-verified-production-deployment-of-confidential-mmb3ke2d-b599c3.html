Summary

Corvex announced the completion and verification of production deployment of confidential computing for AI on NVIDIA HGX B200 systems, enabling encrypted GPU-to-GPU communication across NVIDIA NVSwitch and NVLink fabrics with Intel Trust Domain Extensions and NVIDIA Confidential Computing, providing end-to-end runtime security with CPU and GPU remote attestation using Intel Trust Authority. The deployment aims to support secure, compliant, and multi-tenant AI workloads with near-native performance. It also notes a previously announced all-stock merger with Movano Inc. (MOVE).
