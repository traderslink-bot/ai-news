Summary

Corvex announced the completion and verification of a production deployment of confidential computing for AI on NVIDIA HGX B200 systems, enabling encrypted GPU-to-GPU communication across NVIDIA NVSwitch and NVLink fabrics with Intel Trust Domain Extensions and NVIDIA Confidential Computing to provide end-to-end runtime security including CPU and GPU remote attestation using Intel Trust Authority. The deployment supports secure multi-tenant, enterprise, regulated, and sovereign AI workloads with near-native performance. Corvex previously announced a definitive agreement with Movano Inc. to merge in an all-stock transaction.
