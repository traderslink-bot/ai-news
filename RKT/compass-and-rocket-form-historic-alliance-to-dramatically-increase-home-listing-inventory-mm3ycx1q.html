Summary

Rocket Companies (NYSE: RKT) and Compass International Holdings (NYSE: COMP) announced a three-year strategic alliance to surface Compass' 'Coming Soon' and 'Private Exclusive' listings on Redfin, potentially adding more than 500,000 listings to the Redfin platform. The partnership will integrate home search, agent connections and Rocket Mortgage financing, provide Compass real estate professionals access to over 1 million Redfin buyer inquiries, and expose listings to Redfin’s projected ~2 billion visits in 2026. Rocket Mortgage will offer preferred pricing to Compass clients (a one-percentage-point interest-rate reduction for the first year or up to $6,000 lender credit) and its mortgage offerings will be embedded into Compass’ platform.
