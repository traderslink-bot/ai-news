Summary

OneMedNet announced the next phase of its commercial launch for the iRWD platform powered by Palantir Foundry. The company is converting customer evaluations into commercial subscriptions, with multi-year deals expected to contribute millions in incremental annual recurring revenue. The iRWD network includes over 2,130 healthcare sites and expanding multimodal data. Subscriptions offer near real-time, regulatory-grade RWD with AI-powered search across clinical and imaging data, enabling faster cohort feasibility and RWE study design. The company states the approach supports high-margin recurring revenue and expanded deal sizes as adoption grows across life sciences and AI Health Innovators.

  • iRWD network includes over 2,130 healthcare sites with expanding multimodal data.

  • AI-powered search across clinical and imaging data enables faster cohort feasibility and discovery.
