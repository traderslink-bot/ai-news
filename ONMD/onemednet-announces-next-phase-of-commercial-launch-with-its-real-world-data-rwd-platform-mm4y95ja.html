Summary

OneMedNet (NASDAQ: ONMD) announced the next phase of its commercial launch for its iRWD™ Real-World Data platform powered by Palantir Foundry. The company says it is converting customer evaluations into commercial, multi-year subscriptions expected to represent "millions" in incremental annual recurring revenue (ARR). The platform reportedly covers data from over 2,130 healthcare sites, offers real-time, continuously connected multimodal RWD (clinical, imaging, longitudinal), AI-driven search, faster cohort feasibility, and faster onboarding of data partners. Management positions the launch as advancing a high-margin recurring revenue model and cites early customer feedback on performance, speed, and analytical depth. The release includes standard forward-looking cautionary language and references risks and uncertainties.
