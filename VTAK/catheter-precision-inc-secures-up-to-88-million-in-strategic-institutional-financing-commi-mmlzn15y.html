Summary

Catheter Precision, Inc. (NYSE: VTAK) announced institutional capital commitments of up to $88 million to support its transformation and growth, including funding for the recently acquired subsidiary Flyte and the Company’s medical device operations. Catheter Precision now owns 100% of Flyte, a regional air mobility platform operating Cirrus Vision Jets through subsidiary Ponderosa Air, LLC, and intends to use the financing to expand fleet and market operations, scale Flyte’s platform, and simplify its balance sheet. The transaction and financing are subject to customary closing conditions, including shareholder approval; the company referenced a Form 8-K filed March 9, 2026 for additional details.
