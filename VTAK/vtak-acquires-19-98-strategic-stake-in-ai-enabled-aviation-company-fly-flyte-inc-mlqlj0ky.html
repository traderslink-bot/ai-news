Summary

Catheter Precision, Inc. (NYSE: VTAK) announced a strategic minority investment acquiring a 19.98% equity stake in Fly Flyte, Inc. (operating flight operations through subsidiary Ponderosa Air, LLC, FAA Air Carrier Certificate No. 1POA402M) which operates FAA Part 135 regional services with a fleet of Cirrus Vision Jets equipped with whole-aircraft parachute and Safe Return™ autonomous landing tech. Flyte is revenue-generating today and uses AI-enabled optimization for routing, pricing, dispatch and utilization. The transaction gives VTAK immediate exposure to an asset-backed, scalable regional air mobility business while preserving Flyte’s operating control and creating optionality for increased ownership or future monetization. The deal is subject to customary closing conditions, including shareholder approval; additional details are referenced in VTAK’s Form 8-K filed Feb 6, 2026.

Positives
  • Acquires 19.98% equity stake providing immediate exposure to operating aviation business
  • Flyte is revenue-generating today (not pre-revenue)
  • Flyte is FAA Part 135 certified through Ponderosa Air, LLC
  • Asset-backed fleet of Cirrus Vision Jets with integrated safety systems (parachute; Safe Return™)
  • AI-enabled operations for routing, pricing, dispatch and utilization support operating efficiency
  • Scalable fleet expansion model and potential long-term equity upside for VTAK
Negatives
  • Transaction is subject to customary closing conditions, including shareholder approval
  • 19.98% is a minority stake and preserves Flyte's strategic control (limited influence for VTAK)
  • Forward-looking statements noted; actual results may differ due to risks and uncertainties
