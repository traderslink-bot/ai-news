Summary

RenX Enterprises Corp. (NASDAQ: RENX) issued a letter to shareholders detailing operational focus, capital positioning, and strategic direction. Key items include delivery of the Microtec UTM 1200 Turbo Mill expected in March 2026 to shift biomass overages into higher-value engineered soils, ongoing optimization of operations across Resource Group and Zimmer Equipment, and real estate portfolio rationalization to simplify the balance sheet. The company completed a private placement financing, remains within the Nasdaq minimum bid price compliance period with a July deadline, and is evaluating options including a potential reverse stock split to protect shareholder value.
