Summary

J-Star Holding Co., Ltd. (NASDAQ: YMAT) announced an exclusive global distribution agreement with Patriot Green Energy Technology (PSSB) to distribute PSSB's solid-state battery products for Electric Assist Bicycles and Electric Motorcycles, with non-exclusive rights for other PSSB products. The agreement leverages J-Star's materials and composites capabilities and ITRI-backed PSSB technology. No financial terms or closing date disclosed. Forward-looking statements caution applies.
