Summary

Abits Group Inc (NASDAQ: ABTS) announced the closing of a registered direct offering of approximately $2.1 million in ordinary shares and pre-funded warrants at $2.65 per share, with 792,452 shares or pre-funded warrants sold. Aggregate gross proceeds are approximately $2.1 million. Proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes and working capital. Aegis Capital Corp. acted as exclusive placement agent. The offering was made pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form F-3 No. 333-284387.
