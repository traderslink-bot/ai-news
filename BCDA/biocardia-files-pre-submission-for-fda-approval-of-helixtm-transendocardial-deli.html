BioCardia Files Pre-Submission for FDA Approval of Helix™ Transendocardial Delivery Catheter for Therapeutic and Diagnostic Agent Delivery to the Heart

BioCardia submitted a pre‑submission to the FDA seeking feedback on the Helix™ transendocardial delivery catheter as a device for delivering therapeutic and diagnostic agents to the heart. This is an early regulatory step that could clarify requirements for future filings and trials; for a small‑cap company with a sub‑5M float, FDA feedback and subsequent study or approval milestones would be primary catalysts and potential volatility drivers.
