Summary

C4 Therapeutics reports first patient dosed in Phase 2 MOMENTUM trial evaluating cemsidomide with dexamethasone for relapsed/refractory multiple myeloma. MOMENTUM is open-label, single-arm, multicenter and will enroll approximately 100 patients at the 100 µg dose (14 days on, 14 days off) with dexamethasone weekly; primary endpoint is overall response rate per IMWG criteria. Enrollment is expected to complete in Q1 2027. A Phase 1b trial of cemsidomide with elranatamab is expected to initiate in Q2 2026, with further data analysis from the Phase 1 trial expected in mid-2026. More information is available on clinicaltrials.gov (NCT07284758).

Positives
  • First patient dosed in Phase 2 MOMENTUM trial evaluating cemsidomide in RRMM.
  • Phase 2 MOMENTUM is open-label, single-arm, multicenter, enrolling ~100 patients at 100 µg.
  • Primary endpoint is overall response rate per IMWG criteria, assessed by independent review committee.
  • Phase 1b trial of cemsidomide in combination with elranatamab expected to initiate in Q2 2026.
  • Enrollment completion for MOMENTUM expected in Q1 2027.
Negatives
  • Phase 2 MOMENTUM is a single-arm study with no comparator, which may limit interpretability of efficacy signals.
  • Enrollment completion is several years out (Q1 2027), indicating a lengthy development timeline.
  • Volatility in early-stage results and safety/tolerability profiles remain to be established for cemsidomide.
