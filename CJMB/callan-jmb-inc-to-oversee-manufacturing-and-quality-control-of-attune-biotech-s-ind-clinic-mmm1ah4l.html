Summary

Press release: Callan JMB Inc. (NASDAQ: CJMB) agreed with Biostax Corp d/b/a Attune Biotech to provide independent manufacturing oversight, quality assurance and control, and deployment operations for Attune's Investigational New Drug (IND) application No. 181314 for JKB-122 (lodonal™) targeting Post-Acute Sequelae of SARS-CoV-2 infection (Long COVID). Callan JMB's responsibilities include CMO qualification/validation, batch record review, supply chain verification, surge manufacturing capacity assessment, and distribution pathway validation. The FDA has received and assigned IND 181314; Attune plans a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled, multi-center Phase 2b/3 trial assessing safety, tolerability, and efficacy. JKB-122 is investigational, not FDA-approved, and Attune's IP extends through 2041. The release notes an estimated ~20 million Americans with Long COVID and cites forward-looking statements and related risks.
