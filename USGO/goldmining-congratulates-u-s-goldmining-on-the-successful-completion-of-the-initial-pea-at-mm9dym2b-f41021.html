Summary

GoldMining Inc. announces that its majority-owned subsidiary U.S. GoldMining Inc. has released a positive initial economic assessment (PEA) for the Whistler Gold-Copper Project in Alaska. The project is 100% owned by U.S. GoldMining and located about 105 miles northwest of Anchorage. The PEA is preliminary and based on various assumptions; it outlines potential development options and is subject to risks and uncertainties. GoldMining indirectly holds more than 74% of U.S. GoldMining. Tim Smith, P.Geo., supervised the technical information and is a Qualified Person under NI 43-101. Forward-looking statements carry risks, including metal prices, permitting, financing, and costs.
