Summary

Klarna announced its app now reaches more than 55 million monthly active users and about 9 million daily active users, with daily engagement up ~53% year-over-year. The release links growth to recent product launches (debit card, membership tiers, cashback, mobile phone plans, peer-to-peer payments in Europe). Klarna also states it has over 118 million global active users, 3.4 million transactions per day, and lists notable retail partners. Company is listed on the NYSE (KLAR).
