Summary

FiscalNote Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: NOTE) announced a strategic expansion into political prediction markets. The company launched a preview site (PoliticalPredictions.com), entered a non-binding Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) with prediction market technology firm 365Prediction, and appointed Dr. Laila Mintas as Strategic Advisor to support market design, regulatory engagement, and go-to-market execution. FiscalNote plans to leverage its policy datasets, AI capabilities, and customer base to build prediction market offerings and will host a live discussion on February 18, 2026. The release includes a standard Safe Harbor statement outlining forward-looking risks.

Positives
  • Launched preview site (PoliticalPredictions.com) to engage users and refine product approach
  • Entered strategic, collaborative MOU with 365Prediction to explore technology and product integration
  • Engaged industry expert Dr. Laila Mintas as Strategic Advisor with relevant market-design and regulatory experience
  • Positions company to leverage existing policy datasets, AI capabilities, and customer relationships into a growing market
Negatives
  • Partnership with 365Prediction is under a non-binding MOU (no firm commitments disclosed)
  • No financial terms, timelines, or revenue projections provided for the initiative
  • Company highlights regulatory, execution, and other forward-looking risks in the Safe Harbor statement
