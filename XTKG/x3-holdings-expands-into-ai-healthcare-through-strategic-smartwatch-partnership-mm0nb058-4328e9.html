Summary

X3 Holdings Co., Ltd. (NASDAQ: XTKG) announced a strategic agreement with an AI healthcare technology partner to commercialize an AI-powered healthcare smartwatch globally. The device integrates a non-invasive brainwave sensing chip with digital frequency modulation and operates on a cloud-based AI analytics platform utilizing 7 million user records and 25,000 physiological indicators. X3 Holdings will support international commercialization, deployment, and distribution, marking its entry into the AI healthcare sector. The first service contract under the collaboration is expected to generate revenue beginning in March 2026, contingent on the execution of commercial agreements and rollout timelines.
