Summary

X3 Holdings Co., Ltd. (Nasdaq: XTKG) announced a 30-for-1 consolidation of its Class A ordinary shares approved by the board. Post-consolidation trading is expected to begin March 5, 2026. Each 30 pre-consolidation Class A shares will be combined into one Class A share; par value will change from $0.00003 to $0.0009. Issued and outstanding Class A shares will be reduced from 54,238,270 to approximately 1,807,943. The Class A shares will continue to trade on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the symbol XTKG with a new CUSIP G72007159. No fractional shares will be issued; holders entitled to fractional shares will receive an additional whole share. Transhare Corporation will act as exchange agent. The company states the consolidation is intended to increase the market price per share to maintain Nasdaq listing and to help regain compliance with the minimum bid price requirement.
