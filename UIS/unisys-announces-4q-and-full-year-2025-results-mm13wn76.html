Summary

Press release reporting Unisys (NYSE: UIS) fourth-quarter and full-year 2025 results. Q4 revenue $574.5M, up 5.3% YoY (2.7% constant currency); operating profit margin 13.3% (up 440 bps YoY) and non-GAAP operating profit margin 18.0% (up 640 bps YoY). Q4 cash provided by operations $104.9M; pre-pension and postretirement free cash flow $112.9M. Full-year 2025 revenue $1.95B, down 2.9% YoY; GAAP net loss attributable to Unisys $339.8M (includes a $227.7M net-of-tax pension settlement loss and $55.0M goodwill impairment). Cash and cash equivalents $413.9M at year-end; defined benefit pension funding deficit $448.5M (improved $301.7M YoY, reflecting ~$343.7M in cash contributions including a $250M discretionary contribution). Backlog $3.16B. Company issued full-year 2026 guidance: constant-currency revenue decline (6.5)% to (4.5)% and non-GAAP operating profit margin 9.0%–11.0%. Conference call scheduled Feb. 25, 2026.
