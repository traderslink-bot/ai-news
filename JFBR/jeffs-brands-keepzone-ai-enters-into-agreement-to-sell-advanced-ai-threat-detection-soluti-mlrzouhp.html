Summary

Press release: Jeffs' Brands Ltd (NASDAQ: JFBR, JFBRW) announced that its wholly-owned subsidiary KeepZone AI entered a non-exclusive reseller agreement with SeeTrue, Inc. to market, demonstrate and resell SeeTrue's AI-based automated threat detection solution in Mexico. The agreement targets critical infrastructure, urban security, military/defense segments and identifies the FIFA World Cup 2026 in Mexico City as a potential program opportunity, subject to definitive commercial agreements with end users. SeeTrue's APIDS for CT screening is ECAC-approved; no financial terms were disclosed. The release contains standard forward-looking statement disclaimers and describes the transaction as part of Jeffs' Brands' expansion into the homeland security sector.

Positives
  • Grants KeepZone AI rights to market, demonstrate and resell SeeTrue's AI threat detection solution in Mexico
  • Targets multiple high-value segments: critical infrastructure, urban security, military/defense
  • Identifies FIFA World Cup 2026 in Mexico City as a potential deployment opportunity
  • SeeTrue's APIDS for CT screening is ECAC-approved (regulatory recognition for CT screening)
Negatives
