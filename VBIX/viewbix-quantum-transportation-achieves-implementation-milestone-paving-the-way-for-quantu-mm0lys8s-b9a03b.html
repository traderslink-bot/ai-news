Summary

Viewbix Inc. says Quantum Transportation Ltd., a minority-owned subsidiary, has deployed its transformer-based neural decoder on AWS, following Milestone 1 and securing an IP license from Ramot at Tel Aviv University, enabling scalable processing of quantum data for NISQ devices. Viewbix also signed a definitive agreement to acquire up to 100% (not less than 85%) of Quantum X Labs, with closing expected within 90 days from December 15, 2025, subject to customary conditions.

Positives
  • Transformer-based neural decoder deployed on AWS cloud, establishing infrastructure to process complex quantum data at scale.
  • Initial prototype validated and Milestone 1 completed.
  • Key IP license secured from Ramot at Tel Aviv University.
  • Positioning to engage with quantum hardware design partners and test on real quantum hardware across architectures.
  • Definitive agreement to acquire up to 100% (not less than 85%) of Quantum X Labs, expanding quantum error correction portfolio.
Negatives
  • Acquisition has not closed yet and is subject to customary closing conditions.
  • No disclosed financial terms for the acquisition or IP licenses.
  • Forward-looking statements involve risks and uncertainties related to closing and execution of plans.
