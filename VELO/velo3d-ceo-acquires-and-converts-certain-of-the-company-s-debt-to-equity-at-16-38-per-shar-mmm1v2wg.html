Summary

This is a company press release from Velo3D, Inc. (NASDAQ: VELO). CEO Dr. Arun Jeldi acquired a $5 million promissory note and converted it into common stock at $16.38 per share. Separately, director Ken Thieneman converted a $10 million promissory note at $10.50 per share. Together these conversions reduced the company's outstanding debt by approximately 60%, leaving roughly $10 million of debt outstanding. The company said the actions materially deleveraged the balance sheet ahead of fiscal 2026.
