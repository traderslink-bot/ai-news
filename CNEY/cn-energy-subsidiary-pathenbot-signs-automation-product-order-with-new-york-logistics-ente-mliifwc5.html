Summary

CN Energy Group Inc. (NASDAQ: CNEY) announced that its wholly owned subsidiary Pathenbot Group Inc. entered an equipment sales agreement with a New York State warehousing and logistics company for customized commercial logistics automation equipment, including an electric printing and labeling machine. The contract became effective February 6, 2026; Pathenbot is proceeding with equipment delivery and performance per the schedule. The company says the technology will help the client move from manual operations to intelligent workflows and improve standardization and efficiency in sorting and packaging.
