Summary

Press release reporting unaudited Q4 and full-year 2025 results for C3is Inc. (NASDAQ: CISS). Q4 2025 voyage revenues were $10.6M with a daily TCE of $19,469 (up 24% vs Q4 2024) and fleet operational utilization of 93.5%. Q4 net income was $5.2M and EBITDA was $6.7M. For the twelve months ended Dec 31, 2025, revenues were $34.8M, net income was $10.5M, EBITDA was $17.0M and basic EPS was $46.50. Cash and time deposits totaled $14.9M as of Dec 31, 2025. The company completed equity offerings in Oct and Dec 2025 (raising $2.0M and $9.0M), effected a 1-for-20 reverse stock split on Jan 26, 2026, and entered agreements to acquire two product tankers for $39.8M (deliveries in 2026) financed by one-year interest-free loans. The fleet is reported as unencumbered and averages four vessels.

Positives
  • Q4 2025 voyage revenues $10.6M and daily TCE $19,469, a 24% increase vs Q4 2024
  • Fleet operational utilization improved to 93.5% in Q4 2025 (from 90.2% in Q4 2024)
  • Q4 net income $5.2M and Q4 EBITDA $6.7M; FY 2025 net income $10.5M and EBITDA $17.0M
  • Cash and time deposits balance of $14.9M as of Dec 31, 2025
  • Completed follow-on offerings in 2025 raising aggregate proceeds ($2.0M and $9.0M)
  • Entered agreements to acquire two product tankers (total $39.8M) with one-year interest-free loan financing
  • Company reports fleet is unencumbered
Negatives
  • Twelve-month 2025 voyage revenues declined to $34.8M from $42.3M in 2024
  • Full-year fleet operational utilization decreased to 82.7% in 2025 from 90.2% in 2024 (idle days and drydocking impacted utilization)
  • Drydocking costs of $1.9M recorded in 2025 (none in 2024)
  • Adjusted EBITDA for 2025 ($8.2M) decreased vs 2024 ($16.4M)
  • Significant non-cash volatility from warrant fair value adjustments (gain/loss swings reported)
