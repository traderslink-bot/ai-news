Summary

Bolt Biotherapeutics (BOLT) announced management will participate in two March investor conferences: TD Cowen 46th Annual Health Care Conference on March 2, 2026 at 9:50 a.m. EST and Leerink Partners Global Healthcare Conference with 1x1 meetings on March 11, 2026. The company is developing BDC-4182, a Boltbody ISAC targeting claudin 18.2, currently in a Phase 1 dose-escalation trial for gastric/gastroesophageal cancer, with collaborations with Genmab and Toray. Bolt is also seeking a partner for BDC-3042 (Dectin-2 agonist) and has preclinical ISAC programs targeting CEA and PD-L1.

Positives
