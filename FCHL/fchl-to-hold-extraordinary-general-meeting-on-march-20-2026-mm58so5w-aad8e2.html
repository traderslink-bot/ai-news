Fitness Champs Holdings Limited (FCHL) will hold an Extraordinary General Meeting of shareholders on March 20, 2026, at 10:00 a.m. Singapore Time at 7030 Ang Mo Kio Street, Avenue 5, #04-48, North Star@AMK, Singapore. The Notice of the EGM, Proxy Statement and Proxy Card outlining the proposed resolutions are available on the company's investor relations website and on the SEC website. Fitness Champs describes itself as an aquatic sports education provider in Singapore and notes plans to expand its offerings to include other sports such as pickleball.
