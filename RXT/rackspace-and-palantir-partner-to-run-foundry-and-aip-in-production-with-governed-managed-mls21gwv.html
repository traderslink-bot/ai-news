Summary

Rackspace Technology (NASDAQ: RXT) and Palantir Technologies (NASDAQ: PLTR) announced a strategic partnership to deploy and operate Palantir's Foundry and Artificial Intelligence Platform (AIP) in production with Rackspace providing a governed operating model, security, compliance and managed operations. The collaboration includes running Palantir software in Rackspace Private Cloud and UK Sovereign data centers to serve regulated and data-sensitive customers. Rackspace currently has 30 Palantir-trained engineers and plans to scale to over 250 within 12 months. The offering targets turnkey services — data migration, hosting, implementation and ongoing managed ops — to accelerate time-to-value; no financial terms were disclosed.
