Summary

Datasea announced initial technical service revenue from its collaboration with Yizhimei for acoustic + AI head-care robots. The collaboration expanded to joint research and development and accelerated product commercialization and broader industrial deployment. Deployment for Chinese markets covers more than 50 cities and about 300 service locations, with international activity in South Korea, Hong Kong SAR, Taiwan, Singapore, Indonesia, and Malaysia. A chain-based franchise model is being promoted to enable large-scale deployment, aiming for more than 3,000 locations in China and roughly 1,500 locations internationally. The company intends to expand AI-enabled health care robots globally and seek additional collaborations. Market research indicates significant growth potential in China's beauty, wellness, and smart health services sector.
