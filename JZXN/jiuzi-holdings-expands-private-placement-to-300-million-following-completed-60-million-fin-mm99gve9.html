Summary

Jiuzi Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: JZXN) announced a follow-on expansion of a private financing to an aggregate $300 million after completing an initial $60 million private placement. Participating institutional investors have expressed interest in additional subscriptions and new investors are conducting due diligence. The expanded financing would be funded in equivalent cryptocurrency assets and is intended to grow the company's digital asset treasury, optimize asset allocation, accelerate on-chain product deployment, and strengthen treasury governance and custody. Terms (including lock-ups and asset delivery) remain under negotiation. The offering is being conducted under exemptions to Securities Act registration, including Section 4(a)(2) and Regulation S.
