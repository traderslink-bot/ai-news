Summary

Jiuzi Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: JZXN) announced a definitive Securities Purchase Agreement with multiple (unnamed) crypto-focused institutional investors to subscribe for 40,000,000 ordinary shares at $1.50 per share, for aggregate proceeds of approximately $60.0 million to be injected in the form of equivalent crypto assets. The amount represents an increase from an initial indication of $40.0 million (a 50% increase). The company says proceeds will accelerate its Digital Asset Treasury (DAT) strategy into scaled implementation, expanding digital-asset reserves, deploying multi-chain interfaces/on-chain applications, accessing global liquidity networks, and establishing treasury governance and institutional infrastructure. The SPA was announced February 12, 2026; the release includes standard forward-looking statement cautions.

Positives
  • Committed institutional capital of ~$60.0 million via SPA (40,000,000 shares at $1.50 each)
  • Amount increased from initial $40.0M indication to $60.0M, indicating stronger-than-expected investor interest
  • Funding provided in crypto assets intended to expand digital-asset reserves and enable scaled implementation of the DAT strategy
  • Company highlights planned operational upgrades: multi-chain integration, improved liquidity routing, and formalized treasury governance
Negatives
  • Issuance of 40,000,000 ordinary shares (potential dilution to existing shareholders)
  • Capital to be injected as crypto assets (exposes proceeds to crypto market volatility and custody/operational risks)
  • Investors are not named in the release (no disclosure of participating entities or their ownership post-transaction)
  • Announcement contains forward-looking statements and cautions that actual results may differ materially
