Summary

Jiuzi Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: JZXN) announced that after completing a $60 million private placement, it is expanding the private financing to an aggregate $300 million. The expansion involves continued participation by existing investors and new institutions, with terms under discussion for expanded scale, lock-up periods, and asset delivery methods. Proceeds are planned to be used to increase digital asset reserves through crypto assets, improve treasury governance and asset allocation, accelerate on-chain application deployment, and pursue strategic acquisitions within the digital asset storage ecosystem. The offering is being conducted pursuant to exemptions from the Securities Act of 1933 (Section 4(a)(2) and Regulation S). The CEO stated that the funds will support secure crypto custody infrastructure and related initiatives.
