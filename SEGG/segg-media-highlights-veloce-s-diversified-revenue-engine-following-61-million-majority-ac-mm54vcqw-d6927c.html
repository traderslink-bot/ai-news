Summary

SEGG Media completed a $61 million majority acquisition of Veloce Media Group and reports 2026 revenue for Veloce and Quadrant expected to exceed $20 million, based on current operating performance and contracted partnerships. Veloce's revenue model spans five verticals: digital advertising and media; creator representation and brand partnerships; esports and sim racing services; sustainable motorsport operations; and direct-to-consumer commerce via Quadrant. In 2025, Veloce's digital advertising revenue was $2.17 million, with hundreds of millions of views; its creator agency, launched in 2025, generated $620,000 since inception with 15 creators and partnerships with Audi, Ferrari, Mercedes-Benz, Jaguar, Rolex, Peroni and Lego. Quadrant, acquired July 2025, delivered 1 billion brand impressions and $2.45 million in partnership and digital revenue in 2025 (portion owned by Veloce), plus $675,000 in merchandise and B2B activations in H2 2025 including T-Mobile collaborations at the Las Vegas Grand Prix. Esports, sim racing and sustainable motorsport produced $3.36 million in fee-for-service and sponsorship revenue in 2025 and $2.27 million in sponsorship revenue in 2024. Veloce has a confirmed entry into the FIA Hydrogen World Cup in 2026. The combination enhances SEGG Media's consolidated revenue base and supports growth across international sponsorships, creator monetization, and direct-to-consumer commerce.
