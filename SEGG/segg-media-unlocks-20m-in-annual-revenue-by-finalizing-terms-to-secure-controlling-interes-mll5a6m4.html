Summary

SEGG Media (NASDAQ: SEGG, LTRYW) announced binding terms to acquire at least a majority, controlling interest in Veloce Media Group, with completion targeted for February 17, 2026. The transaction values Veloce at approximately $61 million (£45 million) and is projected to contribute in excess of $20 million in additional annual revenue beginning to be reported in Q1 2026. Consideration will be a blend of cash and SEGG common shares priced at $10 per share. Veloce reported $17.5 million (£12.8 million) in revenue for its latest reported period and owns assets including Quadrant (co‑founded by Formula 1 driver Lando Norris) and partnerships with global brands. Closing is subject to final legal review, definitive documentation and customary closing conditions. The release includes standard forward‑looking risk disclosures.
