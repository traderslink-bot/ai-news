Summary

Freight Technologies, Inc. (NASDAQ: FRGT) announced a two-year agreement to license its Fleet Rocket Enterprise TMS to Marksman XBF Holding Group SAPI de CV, a cross-border freight agency focused on U.S.–Mexico shipments. Marksman will deploy Fleet Rocket modules including an AI tendering bot, Zayren.ai pricing, real-time GPS tracking, ETA alerts, mobile access, integrated chat/WhatsApp, analytics, and ERP integration. The implementation will integrate with Fr8App marketplace to access carrier capacity and is presented as supporting recurring software revenue growth and expanded adoption across the USMCA region. The press release does not disclose financial terms or projected revenue impact and includes standard forward-looking statement risk disclosures.

Positives
  • Two-year commercial license for Fleet Rocket Enterprise with Marksman, a cross-border freight agency
  • Deployment includes AI-enabled features (AI tendering bot, Zayren.ai), real-time tracking, analytics, and ERP integration
  • Integration with Fr8App marketplace provides direct carrier capacity access to accelerate tendering and operations
  • Management frames the deal as supporting recurring software revenue growth and expanded regional adoption
Negatives
  • Financial terms, fees and expected revenue contribution from the agreement are not disclosed
  • Agreement duration is limited to two years (no long-term financial commitments disclosed)
  • Press release contains forward-looking statements and risk disclosures, including potential Nasdaq listing and regulatory/competitive risks
