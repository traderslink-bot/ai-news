Summary

VerifyMe, Inc. (NASDAQ: VRME) and Open World Ltd. executed an Agreement and Plan of Merger to combine operations, aiming to create an infrastructure provider for regulated digital-asset services and real-world asset (RWA) tokenization. The combined company is expected to focus on token listings, regulated digital asset infrastructure, enterprise-grade compliance, and institutional RWA tokenization across multiple jurisdictions. The boards of both companies unanimously approved the signing. Closing is subject to customary conditions, including SEC and Nasdaq filings, listing approval, and VerifyMe shareholder approval; regulatory filings and shareholder approvals are anticipated by Q2 2026. VerifyMe’s board approved termination of its at-the-market equity program. Advisors named include Maxim Group LLC, Latham & Watkins LLP and Harter Secrest & Emery LLP. The announcement contains standard representations, covenants and forward-looking statements.
