Summary

VerifyMe, Inc. (NASDAQ: VRME) and Open World Ltd. executed an Agreement and Plan of Merger to combine into a Nasdaq-listed company focused on regulated digital-asset infrastructure and institutional real-world-asset (RWA) tokenization. The boards of both companies unanimously approved the agreement. Closing is subject to customary conditions, including VerifyMe shareholder approval, SEC and Nasdaq filings and approvals; regulatory filings and shareholder approvals are anticipated by Q2 2026. VerifyMe will file a registration statement on Form S-4 to register shares to be issued in the transaction and will include a proxy statement/prospectus. VerifyMe terminated its at-the-market equity program. No transaction consideration or detailed deal economics were disclosed. Advisors named include Maxim Group LLC (financial advisor to Open World), Latham & Watkins LLP (counsel to Open World) and Harter Secrest & Emery LLP (counsel to VerifyMe).

Positives
  • Boards of both companies unanimously approved the merger agreement
  • Combined company positioned to be Nasdaq-listed and focused on institutional-grade RWA tokenization and regulated digital-asset infrastructure
  • VerifyMe to file Form S-4 and proxy/prospectus, providing planned disclosure to shareholders
  • Open World brings prior RWA initiatives and an established advisory network and partnerships
Negatives
  • No financial terms, share exchange ratio or consideration disclosed in the press release
  • Transaction is subject to multiple approvals (VerifyMe shareholders, SEC, Nasdaq) and may not close
  • VerifyMe terminated its at-the-market (ATM) equity program, reducing near-term capital flexibility
