FBS Global Limited (NASDAQ: FBGL) has regained compliance with Nasdaq Listing Rule 5550(a)(2), resolving the minimum bid price deficiency. The company closed at or above $1.00 for ten consecutive trading days from February 12 to February 26, 2026. Shares will continue to trade on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the ticker FBGL. CEO Kelvin Ang said the development supports market confidence and the company’s focus on secured projects and value creation.
