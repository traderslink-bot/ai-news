Summary

Aspire Biopharma Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: ASBP) announced a strategic collaboration with Microsize to develop a novel sublingual powder formulation of alprazolam intended to deliver faster, more predictable relief for anxiety disorders. Phase 1 clinical trial of the patent-pending formulation is planned for mid-2026. The collaboration leverages Microsize's particle engineering capabilities and Pace's analytical/testing services. Alprazolam was among the most prescribed medications in the US in 2023, and the global alprazolam powder market is projected to reach $55.84 billion by 2032.

Positives
  • Aims to create the first sublingual alprazolam powder with faster onset
  • Phase 1 clinical trial planned for mid-2026
  • Utilizes Microsize's particle engineering and Pace's analytical capabilities
  • Aligned with a large existing market for alprazolam prescriptions
Negatives
