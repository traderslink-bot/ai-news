Summary

LiveRamp announced its Board increased the company's share repurchase authorization by $200 million to $1.5 billion and extended the program’s expiration to December 31, 2027. Following the expansion, LiveRamp has approximately $337 million available for repurchases through the end of calendar 2027. Management cited confidence in long-term growth and the company's role in an AI-driven ecosystem; repurchases will be executed based on market conditions, price and capital availability, and the company may modify or terminate the program at any time.

Positives
  • Board increased repurchase authorization by $200 million to $1.5 billion
  • Program expiration extended to December 31, 2027
  • Approximately $337 million currently available for repurchases
  • Management states strong balance sheet and durable cash flow to support repurchases
Negatives
  • Repurchases are discretionary and subject to market conditions, price and capital availability
  • Company reserves the right to modify or terminate the repurchase program
