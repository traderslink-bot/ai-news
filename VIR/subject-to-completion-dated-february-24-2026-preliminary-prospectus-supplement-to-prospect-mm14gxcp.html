Summary

SEC filing (Form 424B5) — preliminary prospectus supplement for Vir Biotechnology, Inc. offering $200,000,000 of common stock (Nasdaq: VIR). Issuer: Vir Biotechnology, Inc. Underwriters have a 30-day option to purchase up to $30,000,000 additional shares. Use of proceeds: fund Vir's portion of global clinical development and potential U.S. launch costs for VIR-5500 under a recently announced collaboration with Astellas, advance other PRO-XTEN TCE programs, and general corporate purposes. Reporting/transaction counterparties: Astellas US LLC (Astellas) — Vir entered a Collaboration and License Agreement and a Stock Purchase Agreement (Astellas SPA) under which Astellas agreed to purchase 7,239,382 shares for ~$75 million at $10.36/share (50% premium to 30-day VWAP as of Feb 17, 2026); the collaboration contemplates $240 million cash upfront + a $20 million near-term milestone and up to $1.37 billion in future milestones, plus tiered royalties; profit share in U.S. to be 50/50 (if approved). Ownership facts: Vir had 139,474,954 shares outstanding as of Dec 31, 2025; Astellas’ 7,239,382-share purchase represents approximately 5.2% of that outstanding base (subject to closing and subsequent changes). Other capitalization details disclosed: 8,634,511 options outstanding (as of 12/31/25), 5,690,008 RSUs outstanding (as of 12/31/25), additional options/RSUs granted in 2026 (2,783,800 options and 3,468,026 RSUs), ~27.3M shares reserved under equity plans, ~7.35M reserved for ESPP, and an at-the-market program capacity up to $300.0M. Material clinical update included: positive Phase 1 VIR-5500 monotherapy dose-escalation data (safety/tolerability and dose-dependent activity, n=58; detailed cohort efficacy metrics provided). Closing of Astellas transactions subject to Hart-Scott-Rodino clearance and other conditions.
