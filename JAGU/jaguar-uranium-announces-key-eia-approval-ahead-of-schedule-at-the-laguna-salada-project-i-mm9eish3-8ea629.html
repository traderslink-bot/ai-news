Summary

Jaguar Uranium Corp has received the Environmental Impact Assessment permit from the Ministry of Environment for the Guanaco concession at Laguna Salada in Argentina, ahead of schedule. The approval enables major exploration activities and accelerates the fully funded Phase 1 exploration campaign following a $25 million IPO earlier this year. Laguna Salada is described as one of the largest and most prospective near-surface uranium projects in Argentina.
