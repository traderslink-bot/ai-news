Summary

Jaguar Uranium Corp. and its wholly-owned subsidiary 2847312 Ontario Inc. entered into a non-exclusive framework collaboration agreement with the Mendoza Province Ministry of Energy and Environment to cooperate on Jaguar's properties in Mendoza, including geological, environmental, legal, engineering, economic and financial studies; information exchange; training; and environmental, social and community practices related to mining. The initial term is one year and may be renewed for one additional year by mutual consent.

Positives
  • Establishes a formal cooperation framework with the Mendoza energy ministry
  • Potential access to technical, environmental, and community development support
  • Initial one-year term with potential renewal provides staged commitment
Negatives
  • No exploration results, resources or reserves are disclosed in this release
  • Agreement is non-exclusive, which may limit exclusivity of collaboration
