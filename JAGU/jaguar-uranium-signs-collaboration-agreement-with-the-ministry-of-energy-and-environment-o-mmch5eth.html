Summary

Jaguar Uranium Corp. (NYSE American: JAGU) announced that its wholly‑owned subsidiary, 2847312 Ontario Inc., entered a non‑exclusive one‑year (renewable once) framework collaboration agreement with the Ministry of Energy and Environment of Mendoza. The Agreement provides a cooperation framework on the company's Mendoza properties covering technical and professional assistance (geological, environmental, legal, engineering, economic and financial studies), structured information exchange (with confidentiality), training and professional development, and coordination of environmental, social and community practices. The release states the collaboration is intended to support responsible development and facilitate exploration activity, and notes no new scientific or technical results are being reported.
