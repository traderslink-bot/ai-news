Summary

SEC filing (Form 424B5) — U.S. Energy Corp. filed a prospectus supplement for a registered underwritten offering of 8,800,000 shares of common stock at $1.00 per share. Underwriter: Roth Capital Partners, LLC. Underwriting discount: 5.75% ($0.05750/share). Gross proceeds: $8.8 million; estimated net proceeds to company: ~$8.294 million (after underwriting). Shares outstanding before offering: 44,269,192; after offering: 53,069,192. Use of proceeds: development of Kevin Dome (Montana) industrial gas assets, general corporate purposes and working capital. Common stock trades on Nasdaq under ticker USEG (last reported sale $1.15 on March 6, 2026). Delivery expected on or about March 10, 2026. Filing discloses prior Common Stock Purchase Agreement with Roth Principal Investments (commitment up to $25.0M); to date 8,548,382 shares have been sold to Roth Principal Investments for aggregate gross proceeds of $9.3M. SEC risk/disclosure details include immediate dilution to new investors (~$0.25/share), lock-up agreements (60 days), and extensive risk factors related to project execution, liquidity and market volatility.
