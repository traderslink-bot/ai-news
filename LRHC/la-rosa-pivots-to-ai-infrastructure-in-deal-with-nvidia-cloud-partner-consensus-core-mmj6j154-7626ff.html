Summary

La Rosa Holdings Corp. (LRHC) announced a non-binding letter of intent to acquire 100% of Consensus Core Technologies via an all-equity exchange with no cash consideration. Consensus is an Nvidia Cloud Partner providing AI infrastructure solutions and targets more than 2 gigawatts of capacity across North America. Post-closing ownership would be about 3.10% for La Rosa stockholders and about 96.90% for Consensus holders, with Consensus designating the post-transaction CEO and Chairman and La Rosa designating two directors. Maxim Group LLC is advising La Rosa; the LOI is non-binding and subject to definitive agreements. LRHC has recently acquired the remaining 49% stake in La Rosa Realty Lakeland LLC and plans a Tier III AI data center in Osceola County, Florida. LRHC shares were down 2.13% in premarket trading.
