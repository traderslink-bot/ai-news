Summary

La Rosa Holdings announced a non-binding LOI to acquire 100% of Consensus Core Technologies in an all-equity exchange with no cash. Post-closing, La Rosa stockholders would own about 3.10% of the combined company, while Consensus holders would own about 96.90%. Consensus will designate the post-transaction CEO and Chairman, and the board will be 5–7 directors with La Rosa appointing two. Maxim Group is the exclusive financial advisor to La Rosa; legal counsel are DuMoulin Black LLP (Consensus) and Sichenzia Ross Ference Carmel LLP (La Rosa). The company has also pursued related AI infrastructure and real estate moves, including acquiring the remaining stake in La Rosa Realty Lakeland LLC and planning a 10,000-square-foot Tier III AI data center. LRHC stock was down 2.13% in premarket trading to $0.98, near a 52-week low.
