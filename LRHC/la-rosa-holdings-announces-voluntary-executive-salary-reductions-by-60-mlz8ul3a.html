Summary

La Rosa Holdings Corp. (NASDAQ: LRHC) announced that its Chief Executive Officer and Chief Operating Officer have voluntarily reduced their base salaries by 60%, effective March 15, 2026, as part of a broader initiative to streamline operations, reduce overhead, and reallocate capital toward higher-impact initiatives to support profitability. The company is reviewing operations, restructuring or exiting underperforming subsidiaries, and intends to provide additional details in its Form 8-K filed with the SEC.
