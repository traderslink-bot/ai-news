Summary

Jaguar Health, Inc. (NASDAQ:JAGX) strengthened its balance sheet by restructuring and reducing royalty obligations and debt held by affiliates of Chicago Venture Partners L.P., including extinguishment of 48,212 warrants. The restructuring reduced royalty obligations and debt by about 10% (roughly $3 million). It extended the maturity date by three years for a secured revolving line of credit for about $7 million established in November 2025, and ensures no debt maturities before July 2026 for any debt held by Chicago Venture Partners L.P. The company plans to pursue additional opportunities to further restructure debt. Jaguar continues its crofelemer program for rare-disease intestinal failure indications, with ongoing business development discussions that could bring in non-dilutive funds from potential licensee partners.
