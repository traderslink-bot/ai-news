Summary

Motorsport Games reported Q4 2025 revenue of $3.8 million, up from $2.0 million in the prior year. Q4 gross profit was $3.3 million with an 85.0% gross margin. For the full year 2025, revenue was $11.3 million, gross profit $9.2 million, and net income $6.8 million ($1.43 per share). Adjusted EBITDA for 2025 was $7.3 million. The company generated $4.1 million in operating cash flow in 2025 and had cash and cash equivalents of about $5.0 million at year-end, rising to $6.0 million by February 28, 2026. A $3 million revolving credit facility with Citibank was secured. Le Mans Ultimate Version 1.2 was released, including the Paul Ricard circuit and Ginetta LMP3 car, with DLC sales contributing to higher 2025 results. Esports sponsorship revenue was affected by the absence of Le Mans Virtual Series events in 2025 and 2024.
