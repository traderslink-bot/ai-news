Summary

Dyadic Applied BioSolutions and Inzymes ApS announced plans to commercialize recombinant non-animal bovine chymosin in 2026 after completing final development activities under their collaboration agreement. The milestone triggered a $200,000 payment to Dyadic, and royalties on future commercial sales remain possible. Chymosin is the primary milk-clotting enzyme used in cheese production. The global dairy processing enzymes market is estimated at $1.5–$2.0 billion annually. The partners will pursue commercialization by combining Dyadic's microbial expression platforms with Inzymes' scale-up and commercialization expertise, while additional non-animal dairy enzyme candidates progress under the collaboration.
