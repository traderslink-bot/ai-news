Summary

GD Culture Group Limited (NASDAQ: GDC) announced a board-authorized share repurchase program permitting the company to repurchase up to US$100 million of its common stock over the next six months, through August 17, 2026. Repurchases may be made in open-market or privately negotiated transactions in compliance with applicable laws and the company's insider trading policy. The number and timing of repurchases are at management's discretion and depend on factors including the CME CF Bitcoin Reference Rate (New York Variant), share price, trading volume, working capital needs, market conditions and legal requirements. The board will periodically review and may adjust the program.
