Summary

Zura Bio Limited (NASDAQ: ZURA) announced it has commenced an underwritten public offering of its Class A ordinary shares or, for certain investors, pre-funded warrants to purchase Class A ordinary shares. The company expects to grant underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 15% of the aggregate number of shares and shares underlying the pre-funded warrants at the public offering price, less underwriting discounts and commissions. All securities in the offering will be sold by Zura pursuant to an effective shelf registration statement on Form S-3 (Reg. No. 333-281905). Leerink Partners, Piper Sandler and Cantor are acting as joint bookrunning managers and Wedbush PacGrow as lead manager. The offering is subject to market and other conditions and a preliminary prospectus supplement will be filed with the SEC.
