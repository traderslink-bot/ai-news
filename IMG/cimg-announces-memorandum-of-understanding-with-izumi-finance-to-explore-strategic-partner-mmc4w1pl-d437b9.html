Summary

CIMG Inc. announced a memorandum of understanding with iZUMi Finance to explore a broader strategic partnership focused on digital asset infrastructure and on-chain liquidity services. Under the MOU, CIMG and iZUMi plan to explore a potential transaction under which CIMG may acquire certain assets, patents, and technology from iZUMi, and they will pursue broader cooperation including the potential purchase by CIMG of certain services and IZI tokens from iZUMi. The parties also intend to evaluate additional ways to manage and utilize CIMG's digital assets holdings, including Bitcoin. Any transaction would be subject to further negotiations and the execution of definitive agreements. CIMG is Nasdaq-listed (IMG) and operates in digital health and sales development with a cryptocurrency-focused strategy.
