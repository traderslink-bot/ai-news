Summary

Press release: CIMG Inc. (Nasdaq: IMG) reported audited results for year ended Sept. 30, 2025. Revenue was approximately $10.3 million, up ~433% year-over-year, driven by new computing power service contracts and digital health. Net loss declined ~45.5% year-over-year. The company held ~500 BTC valued at ~$57.05 million as of Sept. 30, 2025. Total assets rose ~1,227% year-over-year. Book value per share was ~$2.99. CIMG completed high-performance computing server transactions via a subsidiary and said it will continue focusing on AI computing infrastructure, digital health, and a cryptocurrency-focused strategy, with potential future Bitcoin purchases.

Positives
  • Revenue of approximately $10.3 million, up ~433% year-over-year
  • Net loss reduced by ~45.5% year-over-year
  • Holds ~500 Bitcoin valued at approximately $57.05 million (as of Sept. 30, 2025)
  • Total assets increased ~1,227% year-over-year
  • Book value per share approximately $2.99
Negatives
  • Company remains unprofitable (reported a net loss for FY2025 despite reduction)
  • Significant exposure to Bitcoin and digital-asset price fluctuations (500 BTC on balance sheet)
  • Forward-looking risks cited, including ability to execute contracts, raise capital, Nasdaq listing compliance, and regulatory/legal/currency transfer risks related to operating in China
