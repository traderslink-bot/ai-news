Summary

SEC filing: Form 424B5 prospectus supplement by REalloys Inc. for an underwritten offering of common stock (Nasdaq: ALOY). The supplement outlines an offering of shares of common stock (exact share count and price fields are redacted/subject to completion), with Clear Street LLC and Needham & Company as joint book-running managers and a 30-day underwriter option to purchase additional shares. REalloys (formerly Blackboxstocks Inc.) describes its business as a North American integrated rare earth to NdFeB magnet materials and magnet company and discloses recent M&A: acquisition of Strategic Metals Development Inc. (Hoidas Lake project) and PMT Critical Metals Inc. (Euclid Magnet Facility). Post-merger capitalization and recent corporate actions: as of the Merger Closing there were ~57.11 million shares outstanding and former Private REalloys stockholders owned ~92.2% of the Company (former pre-Merger stockholders ~7.8%). As of March 3, 2026 the prospectus uses 57,128,001 shares outstanding as the base for the Offering. Convertible and other dilutive instruments disclosed include: 5,126 shares of Series C Convertible Preferred Stock (convertible into 1,372,542 shares at $11.205 conversion price), 3,269,998 shares of Series A Convertible Preferred Stock (convertible into 654,000 shares), warrants to purchase up to 100,245 shares (weighted-average exercise $13.19), stock options reserved/exercisable (figures disclosed in filing), 8,500,000 shares reserved under 2025 LTIP, and CVRs issued one per outstanding share at record date. Use of proceeds: working capital and general corporate purposes. The filing notes material risk disclosures including potential dilution, market volatility, Nasdaq continued listing risks, and that certain Private REalloys interim financial statements (period ended Sep 30, 2025) were unaudited/unreviewed by independent auditors.
