Summary

REAlloys Inc., a merger target of Blackboxstocks Inc. (NASDAQ: BLBX), entered into a partnership with the Saskatchewan Research Council to expand heavy rare earth refining at SRC’s facility in Saskatoon, enabling North America’s first fully funded and permitted heavy rare earth processing pathway with a long-term offtake securing the majority of annual production. The project includes a US$21 million expansion increasing heavy REE capacity by about 300% and NdPr capacity by about 50%, with targets of up to 30 t/year Dy oxide, 15 t/year Tb oxide, and 400 t/year NdPr metal (600 t/year after full expansion). A precursor commercial facility in Saskatoon is planned for roughly 200 t Dy, 85 t Tb, and 2,700 t NdPr per year. The expanded facility is expected to be operational in early 2027 and aligned with U.S. defense procurement rules under Title 50 and DFARS, ensuring a zero-Chinese nexus. REAlloys’ downstream operations are in Euclid, Ohio, and the partnership anchors North America’s midstream rare earth capability to support defense, clean energy, and high-performance manufacturing supply chains.
