Summary

Orange 142, a division of Direct Digital Holdings (Nasdaq: DRCT), announced the launch of Ignition+, a unified, AI-powered programmatic platform designed for enterprise advertisers. The platform combines activation, supply access, AI-driven optimization, and measurement in a single stack to centralize media buying, aiming to improve transparency, reduce waste, and lower costs. It offers a flat-fee managed-services model with no hidden platform fees, and provides on-demand support to in-house teams or outsourced teams. Beta testing in late 2025 reportedly demonstrated improved cost efficiency and higher performance for early adopters. Ignition+ targets Fortune 1,000 brands and large independent agencies.

Positives
  • Unified platform integrating activation, supply access, optimization, and measurement
  • Improves transparency and reduces waste with a flat upfront fee and no hidden platform fees
  • Direct access to premium inventory through a single integration point
  • AI-driven optimization features: inventory quality scoring, predictive clearing price optimization, closed-loop audience learning
  • On-demand managed services that can supplement in-house or outsourced teams
