Summary

Megan Holdings Limited (NASDAQ: MGN) priced a follow-on offering of 20,750,000 Class A ordinary shares at $0.40 per share, for expected gross proceeds of $8.3 million before placement agent fees and expenses. The offering is being conducted on a reasonable best-efforts basis with D. Boral Capital LLC as exclusive placement agent and is expected to close on or about February 27, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. The securities are offered under an effective Form F-1 (File No. 333-292850). The company intends to use net proceeds for business ventures and potential acquisitions, development of new products including its Smart Farming System, and general working capital and corporate purposes.
