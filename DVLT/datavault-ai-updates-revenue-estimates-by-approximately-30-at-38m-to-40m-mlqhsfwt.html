Summary

Press release: Datavault AI (NASDAQ: DVLT) updated its preliminary, unaudited fiscal 2025 revenue estimate to $38M–$40M (midpoint $39M), up from a prior estimate of $30M (≈+27% to +33%; +30% at midpoint). The company attributed the increase to tech-licensing fees and tokenization/monetization services. Datavault reaffirmed its FY2026 revenue target of $200M (projected +400%+ YoY vs. FY2025). The results are preliminary and unaudited; audited FY2025 financials are expected to be filed with the SEC next month.

Positives
  • Preliminary FY2025 revenue estimate raised to $38M–$40M, midpoint $39M (+30% vs prior estimate)
  • Company attributes growth to tech-licensing fees and tokenization/monetization services
  • FY2026 revenue target of $200M reaffirmed, implying substantial projected growth
Negatives
  • Updated FY2025 figures are preliminary and unaudited
  • Independent auditor has not reviewed these preliminary estimated financial results
  • Company notes actual results may differ materially from preliminary estimates
