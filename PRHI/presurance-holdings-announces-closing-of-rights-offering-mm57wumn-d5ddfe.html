Summary

Presurance Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: PRHI) announced the closing of its rights offering. A total of 4,284,640 shares were purchased at $1.00 per share at closing on February 27, 2026. Under a Backstop Agreement, Clarkston Companies, Inc. and its assignee purchased 9,715,360 unsubscribed shares at $1.00 per share. The company redeemed its Series B Preferred Stock and paid accrued dividends. Gross proceeds totaled $14,000,000. Proceeds will be used to redeem the Series B Preferred Stock and for general corporate purposes. Rights not exercised by 5:00 p.m. ET on February 24, 2026 expired; the offering terminated for shares not issued at closing.
