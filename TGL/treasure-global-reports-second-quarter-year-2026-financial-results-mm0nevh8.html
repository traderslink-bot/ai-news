Summary

Press release: Treasure Global (NASDAQ: TGL) reported results for the quarter ended December 31, 2025. Revenue was USD 1.08 million (up from USD 0.30 million in Q2 FY2025). Net loss was USD 3.11 million versus a net loss of USD 0.23 million in Q2 FY2025; loss per share was USD (3.47) compared with USD (11.32) (retroactively adjusted for stock splits). The quarter included USD 4.63 million of favorable fair-value adjustments of derivative liabilities. Cash and cash equivalents increased to USD 5.45 million from USD 0.24 million as of June 30, 2025, supported by financing activities and warrant exercises. Operationally, the company highlighted growth in E-voucher transactions on its ZCITY platform (2.7 million registered users as of June 2025) and the early launch of its OXI Wallet in December 2025 aimed at expanding fintech capabilities.
