Summary

Treasure Global Inc. reported Q2 FY2026 results for the quarter ended December 31, 2025. Revenue was USD 1.08 million, up from USD 0.30 million in Q2 FY2025. Net loss was USD 3.11 million, compared with USD 0.23 million in Q2 FY2025, with USD 4.63 million in favorable fair-value adjustments of derivative liabilities. Loss per share was USD (3.47), versus USD (11.32) in Q2 FY2025. Cash and cash equivalents rose to USD 5.45 million from USD 0.24 million. The company highlighted financing activities and warrant exercises supporting liquidity, the OXI Wallet launched in December 2025, and ongoing expansion of the ZCITY platform and merchant partnerships.

  • Revenue increased to USD 1.08 million in Q2 FY2026 from USD 0.30 million in Q2 FY2025
  • Loss per share improved to USD (3.47) from USD (11.32)

  • USD 4.63 million in favorable fair-value adjustments of derivative liabilities

  • ZCITY platform expansion and increased merchant partnerships highlighted
  • ZCITY user base noted (over 2.7 million registered users as of June 2025)
Negatives
