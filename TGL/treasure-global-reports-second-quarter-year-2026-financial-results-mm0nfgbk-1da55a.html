Summary

Treasure Global, NASDAQ: TGL, reported Q2 FY2026 results for the quarter ended December 31, 2025. Revenue 1.08 million USD, up from 0.30 million USD in Q2 FY2025. Net loss 3.11 million USD; offset in part by 4.63 million USD in favorable fair-value adjustments of derivative liabilities. Loss per share 3.47 USD, improved from 11.32 USD in Q2 FY2025 (retroactively adjusted). Cash and cash equivalents 5.45 million USD, up from 0.24 million USD as of June 30, 2025. The company cited progress on its ZCITY platform and OXI Wallet initiative, along with ongoing platform modernization and expanded merchant partnerships.

Positives
  • Revenue for the quarter increased to USD 1.08 million from USD 0.30 million in the prior-year quarter.
  • Loss per share improved to USD (3.47) from USD (11.32) in the prior-year quarter (retroactively adjusted).
  • Cash and cash equivalents increased to USD 5.45 million from USD 0.24 million as of June 30, 2025.
  • USD 4.63 million in favorable fair-value adjustments of derivative liabilities partially offset the net loss.
  • Management highlighted progress on ZCITY platform enhancements, OXI Wallet initiative, and expanded merchant partnerships.
Negatives
  • Net loss for the quarter remained significant at USD 3.11 million.
  • Revenue, while up year over year, remains modest at USD 1.08 million.
  • Net results include substantial non-cash fair-value adjustments, indicating that some improvements are driven by accounting adjustments rather than operating performance.
