Summary

RenX Enterprises Corporation (NASDAQ: RENX) announced a PIPE financing of approximately $6.0 million consisting of Senior Convertible Notes and attached warrants. The Notes total $6.0M principal, bear 12% annual interest, mature in 13 months, and are convertible at the holder's option at a fixed conversion price of $0.281 per share. The Company will also issue warrants equal to 100% of the face value of the Notes, exercisable for six years at $0.15594 per share. Dawson James is the sole placement agent; the offering is expected to close on or about February 13, 2026, and is being conducted as a private placement under Section 4(a)(2) and/or Regulation D. The Company agreed to customary registration rights for the resale of shares underlying the Notes and Warrants and intends to use net proceeds primarily for working capital.

Positives
  • Raises approximately $6.0 million in gross proceeds
  • Registration rights agreed to permit resale of shares issuable upon conversion/exercise
  • Dawson James serving as sole placement agent; closing expected promptly (on or about Feb 13, 2026)
  • Financing structured as an at-market transaction per Nasdaq Listing Rule 5635(d)(1)(A)
Negatives
  • Notes accrue 12% interest and mature in 13 months, creating near-term refinancing/repayment risk
  • Conversion price ($0.281) and warrants (exercise $0.15594) likely to be dilutive to existing shareholders
  • Warrants equal to 100% of note face value increase potential share overhang
  • Securities are unregistered and were sold in a private placement (restricted resale until registration)
