Summary

Slide Insurance (NASDAQ: SLDE) reported strong Q4 and FY2025 results. Q4 gross premiums written were $618.5M, up 56.7% YoY; total revenue was $347.0M (up 45.5%); net income was $170.4M (up 126.9%) and diluted EPS was $1.23. Q4 combined ratio improved to 38.0% (loss ratio 8.3%). Policies in force at December 31, 2025 were 493,532, driven in part by assumed policies from Citizens. Full-year 2025 gross premiums written were $1.796B (up 34.6%), total revenue $1.156B (up 36.5%), net income $444.0M and diluted EPS $3.36; full-year combined ratio improved to 52.1%. Slide initiated 2026 guidance: expected gross written premiums $1.85B–$1.95B and net income $455M–$470M. Management cites growth from organic expansion and Citizens policy assumptions and highlights lower catastrophe losses (no named-storm incurred losses in the reported periods).
