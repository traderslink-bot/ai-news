Summary

NexGel, Inc. (NASDAQ: NXGL) will license and acquire a portfolio of commercial-stage regenerative biomaterial products from Celularity, Inc. The portfolio includes six products focused on tendon repair, skin grafts, and bone growth with over a decade of clinical use and established insurance reimbursement pathways. NexGel expects annual revenue to nearly triple to about $35 million in 2026 and to be profitable immediately. The deal is supported by approximately $1.8 million in previously announced financing and an expected approximately $14.9 million in additional financing in the first quarter or early in the second quarter of 2026. Three additional 510(k) filings are planned for 2026, 2027, and 2028, creating a visible pipeline for growth. NexGel also aims to scale biomaterials and hydrogel technologies across multiple sectors.
