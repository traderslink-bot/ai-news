Summary

Avalon GloboCare Corp. closed a private placement priced at-the-market under Nasdaq rules for 6,372,550 shares of common stock (or pre-funded warrants), plus Series A-1 warrants to purchase up to 6,372,550 shares and Series A-2 warrants to purchase up to 6,372,550 shares, at $0.51 per share. Aggregate gross proceeds to date are approximately $3.25 million; potential additional gross proceeds up to approximately $6.5 million from full exercise of the warrants. Proceeds are to be used for debt repayment and for working capital and general corporate purposes. H.C. Wainwright & Co. acted as exclusive placement agent; Roth Capital Partners as financial advisor. Securities are offered to accredited investors under Section 4(a)(2) of the Securities Act with registration rights to cover resale. Warrants may be exercisable only after stockholder approval, with Series A-1 expiring five years from approval and Series A-2 expiring eighteen months from approval.
