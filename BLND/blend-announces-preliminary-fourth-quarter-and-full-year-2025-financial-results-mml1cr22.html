Summary

Blend Labs (NYSE: BLND) issued preliminary Q4 and full-year 2025 results and authorized a share repurchase program up to $50 million. Q4 revenue was $32.4 million, up 7% year-over-year; software platform revenue was $30.3 million (+10% YoY). Q4 non-GAAP operating income was $5.4 million (vs. $3.7M prior-year). Full-year 2025 revenue was $123.5 million, up 7% YoY, and non-GAAP operating income was $15.1 million (versus a $12.8M non-GAAP loss in 2024). GAAP operating loss improved to $21.8 million for the year (from $48.8M in 2024). Blend repurchased 5.1 million shares in Q4 for >$15 million and repurchased $25 million YTD; board authorized up to $50 million additional repurchases. Company provided Q1 2026 guidance: revenue $28.5M–$30.0M and non-GAAP operating income $2.0M–$3.0M, and noted mortgage market unit assumptions (HMDA 1.10–1.20M units for Q1). Blend highlighted deployment of its AI agent, Blend Autopilot, with large customers. Results are preliminary and subject to closing procedures and audit review; final results will be filed in the Form 10-K.
