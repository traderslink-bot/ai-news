Summary

Zentek Ltd. (TSXV: ZEN; Nasdaq: ZTEK) announced Nasdaq has granted a 180-calendar-day extension to regain compliance with the $1.00 minimum bid price requirement under Nasdaq Listing Rule 5550(a)(2). The company’s initial 180-day cure period expired on February 23, 2026; the new deadline to demonstrate compliance (a closing bid of at least $1.00 for ten consecutive business days) is August 24, 2026. There is no immediate effect on listing or trading, and management says it will actively monitor the share price and consider options to regain compliance.
