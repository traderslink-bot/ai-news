Summary

This Form 6-K, filed February 23, 2026 by Guardforce AI Co., Limited, reports two press releases attached as Exhibits 99.1 and 99.2. On February 20, 2026 the company announced board approval of a share repurchase program authorizing repurchases of up to $5.0 million of outstanding ordinary shares. On February 9, 2026 the company announced it entered into a non-binding letter of intent to acquire 100% of the issued and outstanding share capital of MGAI Limited, an AI-driven speech therapy and development/rehabilitation services company. The report is incorporated by reference into multiple Form F-3/F-1 registration statements and prospectuses previously declared effective by the SEC.
