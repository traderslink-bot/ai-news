Summary

Trust Stamp (IDAI) announces a contract with IDetect to provide access to the AAMVA Driver's License Data Verification service, enabling real-time verification of driver’s license data against DMV records across IDetect’s ID scanning solutions. The integration aims to enhance security and prevent forgeries, with a projected capability to process millions of verifications per month at full scale. IDetect serves customers across hospitality, government, and other sectors, including Marriott, Harrah’s Casinos, and the FBI. Financial terms were not disclosed.

Positives
  • Contract with IDetect to provide DMV data verification via AAMVA DLDV service
  • Real-time verification capability across IDetect’s hardware and software solutions with a one-second process
  • Potential to process millions of verifications per month at full scale
  • Expansion of Trust Stamp’s government-backed identity verification offerings
  • Leverages IDetect’s established customer base across multiple industries
Negatives
  • No financial terms or contract value disclosed
  • Success depends on ongoing access to AAMVA DLDV and DMV data; subject to regulatory/data-access risks
  • Projected volumes are contingent and not guaranteed
  • Forward-looking statements and related risk factors are present in the release
