Summary

Press release: Trust Stamp (NASDAQ: IDAI) announced it will provide IDetect with access to the AAMVA Driver's License Data Verification (DLDV) service, enabling real-time verification of driver's license data against issuing agency (DMV) records. The integration will be available across IDetect's hardware and software, claims a one-second verification process, and is expected by the companies to scale to millions of verifications per month. No financial terms were disclosed.
