Summary

Press release: Intuitive Machines, Inc. (NASDAQ: LUNR) announced a $175 million strategic equity investment led by global institutional investors to be received in exchange for the Company's Class A common stock under a definitive securities purchase agreement. The company says proceeds will support revenue expansion following its January acquisition of Lanteris Space Systems, accelerate development of its Near Space Network Services (NSNS), expand use of the Lanteris 1300 series for GEO, lunar and Mars capabilities, and fund growth in high-power on-orbit data processing/edge computing and space-based data centers. Transaction expected to close February 27, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. Cantor is the exclusive placement agent; legal advisors include DLA Piper and Simpson Thacher. The release contains standard forward-looking statement and risk disclosures.
