Summary

Intuitive Machines (NASDAQ: LUNR) announced that its wholly owned subsidiary, Lanteris Space Systems, was selected by L3Harris Technologies (NYSE: LHX) to design, build and deliver 18 spacecraft platforms for the Space Development Agency (SDA) Tranche 3 Tracking Layer. The work supports SDA’s proliferated warfighter space architecture to provide persistent, real-time tracking of advanced missile threats, including hypersonic and ballistic systems. The press release contains standard forward-looking statement disclosures and lists program- and company-level risks but does not disclose contract value or timing.
