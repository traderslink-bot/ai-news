Summary

Criteo becomes the first advertising technology partner to integrate with OpenAI's advertising pilot in ChatGPT Free and Go in the United States. The collaboration aims to enable Criteo's advertising platform to participate in conversational AI experiences, with early data indicating referrals from LLM platforms convert at about 1.5x the rate of other referral channels. The rollout will begin in the coming weeks as part of the ongoing ChatGPT advertising pilot. Criteo reports annual media spend of over $4 billion across more than 17,000 advertisers worldwide.

Positives
  • First advertising technology partner integrating with OpenAI's ChatGPT advertising pilot in the US.
  • Potential to enable advertising within conversational AI experiences and support discovery/consideration.
  • Observed higher conversion rate for referrals from LLM platforms (about 1.5x) in a sample of U.S. retailers.
  • Criteo has a large scale with over $4 billion in annual media spend and more than 17,000 advertisers.
Negatives
  • Forward-looking statements and associated risks; actual results may differ.
  • Risks related to data privacy, access to advertising inventory, and regulatory developments.
  • Uncertainty around the rollout pace and effectiveness of advertising within ChatGPT.
  • Macro-economic conditions (inflation, interest rates) could impact business.
  • Risks related to the planned corporate domicile conversion (France to Luxembourg) and related execution.
