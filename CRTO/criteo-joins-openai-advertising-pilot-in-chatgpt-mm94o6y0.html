Summary

Criteo (NASDAQ: CRTO) announced it is the first advertising technology partner integrating with OpenAI's advertising pilot in ChatGPT Free and Go in the United States; the integration will begin rolling out in coming weeks. Criteo cited aggregated insights from a sample of 500 U.S. retailers showing LLM-referred users convert at ~1.5x the rate of other referral channels. The release highlights Criteo's scale (activates >$4 billion in annual media spend, works with ~17,000 advertisers) and includes forward-looking risk disclosures, including a planned legal domicile transfer (the "Conversion"), macroeconomic impacts, and data/privacy and regulatory uncertainties.
