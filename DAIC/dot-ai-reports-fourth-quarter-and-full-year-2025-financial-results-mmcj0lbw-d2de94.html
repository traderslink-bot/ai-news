Summary

Dot Ai reported results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2025. For 2025, revenue was $5.8 million, gross profit was $2.7 million, and total operating expenses were $13.1 million, resulting in a net loss of $36.7 thousand. For Q4 2025, revenue was $4.5 million, gross profit was $2.0 million, operating expenses were $4.2 million, and net loss was $2.4 thousand. Adjusted EBITDA for the year was $(9.1) thousand and $(2.2) thousand for Q4. The company provided 2026 revenue guidance of $6.0–$7.5 million. Highlights include completion of generation 3.0 SaaS platform with multi-tenant architecture, expanded manufacturing capacity at Barceloneta, Puerto Rico, and strategic partnerships with CanTech Group (Australia), Wiliot (ambient IoT), and Würth Industry North America. Dot Ai showcased Gen 3.0 at Manifest 2026, and management noted progress toward converting pipeline to recurring software subscriptions. The company completed its business combination and began trading on Nasdaq (DAIC).

Positives
  • 2026 revenue guidance of $6.0–$7.5 million.
  • Completed generation 3.0 SaaS platform with multi-tenant architecture and real-time asset visibility.
  • Expanded manufacturing capacity at Barceloneta, Puerto Rico.
  • Strategic partnerships with CanTech Group (Australia), Wiliot (ambient IoT), and Würth Industry North America.
  • Showcased Gen 3.0 platform at Manifest 2026 with CEO participation.
  • Finished business combination and began trading on Nasdaq (DAIC).
Negatives
  • 2025 GAAP net loss of $36.7 thousand and 2024 net loss of $21.5 thousand.
  • 2025 total operating expenses of $13.1 million, up from $7.2 million in 2024.
  • Adjusted EBITDA for 2025 was $(9.1) thousand; negative Adjusted EBITDA also in Q4.
  • Higher operating costs cited as costs of being a public company and the transition to commercial operations.
