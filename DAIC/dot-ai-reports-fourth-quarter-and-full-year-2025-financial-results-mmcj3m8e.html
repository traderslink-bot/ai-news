Summary

Dot Ai (Nasdaq: DAIC) reported Q4 2025 revenue of $4.5M and full-year 2025 revenue of $5.8M, with Q4 gross profit of $2.0M (43.7% gross margin) and full-year gross profit of $2.7M. Operating expenses were $4.2M in Q4 and $13.1M for the year, driving a Q4 net loss of $2.4M ($0.08 per share) and a full-year net loss of $36.7M ($1.78 per share). Adjusted EBITDA was negative $2.2M for Q4 and negative $9.1M for the year. Management provided 2026 revenue guidance of $6.0M to $7.5M, highlighted completion of its generation 3.0 multi-tenant SaaS platform, expansion of manufacturing in Barceloneta, Puerto Rico, and announced strategic distribution/technology partnerships with CanTech, Wiliot, and Würth Industry North America.
