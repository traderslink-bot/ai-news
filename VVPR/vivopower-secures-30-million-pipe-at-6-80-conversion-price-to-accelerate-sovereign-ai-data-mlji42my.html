Summary

VivoPower International PLC (NASDAQ: VVPR) completed a $30 million private investment in public equity (PIPE) via convertible preference shares priced to convert at $6.80 per share with a 6% annual PIK coupon. Investors include New York-based Blue Sky Capital, sovereign family offices from the GCC, and VivoPower Chairman Kevin Chin. Proceeds will be primarily used to scale VivoPower's AI data center portfolio and for general working capital. The release includes standard forward-looking statement disclosures.
