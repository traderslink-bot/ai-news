Summary

Aveda announced a global partnership with AeroFlexx to commercialize AeroFlexx's curbside-recyclable refill packaging technology for prestige beauty. The AeroFlexx format is resealable, enables controlled refilling, and its integrated airframe structure uses up to 70% less plastic than two 250ml Aveda bottles. The refill will debut on select Aveda best-sellers beginning early next year. AeroFlexx is part of the Innventure (NASDAQ: INV) family of companies.
