Summary

Franklin Street Properties Corp. (NYSE: FSP) announced it closed a $320 million secured credit facility with an affiliate of TPG Credit. In an initial drawdown of $258.5 million (net of an original issue discount of $16.5 million), the Company repaid in full its $248.9 million aggregate principal amount of outstanding indebtedness. The Facility includes up to $45 million of delayed draw term loans to fund tenant improvements, leasing commissions, building improvements and other lender-approved uses. Key terms: $320 million aggregate, original stated maturity February 26, 2029 (company option to extend up to one year subject to conditions), initial coupon 9.0%, exit fee 4.0% of funded amount due upon repayment, and a first-priority lien on substantially all assets. Alter Domus (US) LLC will act as administrative agent. FSP said addressing near-term maturities reduces uncertainty and it will continue to review strategic alternatives.
