Summary

GEE Group Inc. announced it has engaged Roth Capital Partners, LLC as its financial advisor to assist in reviewing unsolicited expressions of interest and evaluating strategic alternatives to maximize shareholder value. Roth will help the Board and its M&A Committee evaluate responses and other strategic options; there is no assurance that the review will result in a transaction.
