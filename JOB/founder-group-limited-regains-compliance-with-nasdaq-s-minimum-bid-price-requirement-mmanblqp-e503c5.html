Founder Group Limited (NASDAQ: FGL) announced that on February 25, 2026 Nasdaq's Listing Qualifications Department confirmed the company regained compliance with Nasdaq's minimum bid price requirement under Listing Rule 5550(a)(2) after its Class A ordinary shares closed at US$1.00 per share or higher. The company is a pure-play EPCC solutions provider for solar PV facilities in Malaysia, focusing on large-scale solar projects and commercial and industrial solar projects. The announcement is dated March 3, 2026, from Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
