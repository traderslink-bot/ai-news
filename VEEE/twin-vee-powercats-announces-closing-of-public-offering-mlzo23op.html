Summary

Twin Vee PowerCats Co. (Nasdaq: VEEE) closed a public offering of 6,383,000 shares of common stock at $0.47 per share for gross proceeds of approximately $3.0 million before placement agent fees and offering expenses. ThinkEquity acted as sole placement agent. A registration statement on Form S-1 (File No. 333-292661) became effective January 30, 2026, with a post-effective amendment on February 13, 2026; the offering was made by prospectus and the final prospectus is on the SEC website. The company intends to use net proceeds primarily for working capital and general corporate purposes. The release includes standard forward-looking statement disclaimers.
