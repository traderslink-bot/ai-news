Summary

SEC Form 424B4 prospectus for a best-efforts public offering of 6,383,000 shares of Twin Vee PowerCats Co. common stock at $0.47 per share. Gross offering price around $3.0 million; placement agent ThinkEquity LLC will receive approximately $210,001 in fees ($0.0329 per share). Net proceeds to the company are expected to about $2.5 million after expenses; funds to be used for working capital and general corporate purposes, with potential investment in short-term securities. Post-offering shares outstanding expected to be about 8,620,299; public trading symbol is VEEE on Nasdaq. The offering is being made with no minimum, no escrow, and delivery is anticipated around February 23, 2026. The company is an emerging growth company and will have reduced reporting requirements; the prospectus includes extensive risk factors including immediate dilution to investors and going-concern considerations.
