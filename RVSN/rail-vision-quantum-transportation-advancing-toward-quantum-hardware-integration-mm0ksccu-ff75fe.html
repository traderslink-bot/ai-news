Summary

Rail Vision Ltd. announced its majority-owned Quantum Transportation Ltd. has implemented a transformer-based neural decoder on the AWS cloud, advancing toward testing on physical quantum hardware. Rail Vision completed a 51% stake acquisition in Quantum Transportation on January 14, 2026. Quantum Transportation holds an exclusive sub-license for rail technologies from Ramot and is developing a Transformer-based Universal Decoder (DQECCT) intended to outperform classical decoders. Cloud deployment provides scalable infrastructure to process quantum data with potential applications in railway safety, predictive maintenance, and autonomous operations.
