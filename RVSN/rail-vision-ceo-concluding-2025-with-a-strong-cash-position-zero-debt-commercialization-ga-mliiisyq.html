Summary

Rail Vision (NASDAQ: RVSN) issued a CEO corporate update summarizing 2025 progress and 2026 priorities. Key items: the company reports a strong cash position and zero debt; secured a purchase order (MainLine) from a major Central American freight rail operator now in advanced trials; received a $335,000 follow-on order from a major Latin American mining company; deepened partnership with Israel Railways with MainLine operational and plans to deploy ShuntingYard in cargo; signed an MOU with Sujan Industries and launched a high-profile proof-of-concept in India; completed acquisition of a 51% stake in Quantum Transportation, whose transformer-based neural decoder showed simulated outperformance vs. classical quantum error correction algorithms. Stated 2026 focus: convert pilots/POCs to revenue and fleet deployments, scale sales/marketing, expand geographically through partners/distributors, and integrate quantum-AI capabilities. The release contains forward-looking statements and references SEC filings for risk factors.
