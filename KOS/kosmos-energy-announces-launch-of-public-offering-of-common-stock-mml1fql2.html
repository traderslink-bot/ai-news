Summary

Kosmos Energy Ltd. (NYSE/LSE: KOS) announced a registered underwritten public offering of $175,000,000 of common stock, with a 30-day option for underwriters to purchase up to an additional $26,250,000 of shares. Net proceeds are intended to repay outstanding borrowings under its commercial debt facility and additional outstanding debt. Barclays and Stifel are joint book-running managers. The Offering is being made under an effective shelf registration statement filed with the SEC on June 20, 2024; the offering will be made by means of a preliminary prospectus supplement and accompanying prospectus.
