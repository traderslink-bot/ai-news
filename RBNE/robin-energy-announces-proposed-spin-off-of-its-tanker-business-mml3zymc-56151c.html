Summary

Robin Energy Ltd. announced that its board, following a recommendation by its independent directors, will spin off its tanker business into a new subsidiary, AI OKTO Corp., issuing one AI OKTO share for every 6.5 Robin shares. The tanker entity will be a standalone holding company for the vessel; AI OKTO intends to list on the Nasdaq Capital Market. Robin's chairman and CEO, Petros Panagiotidis, will become chairman and CEO of AI OKTO upon completion. Robin shareholders will not need to take action, and fractional AI OKTO shares will be cashed out. The spin-off is subject to the Registration Statement becoming effective and listing approval, and there is no guarantee of completion or timing.
