Summary

Davis Commodities Limited announced a 20-for-1 reverse stock split of its Class A and Class B ordinary shares. The board approved the split; shareholders previously approved on February 4, 2026. Trading on a split-adjusted basis will commence March 9, 2026. Under the split, 20 Class A shares consolidate into 1, and 20 Class B shares consolidate into 1. Par value increases from US$0.000000430108 to US$0.00000860216. No fractional shares will be issued; fractional entitlements will be rounded up to the nearest whole share. Class A shares will continue trading on Nasdaq Capital Market under ticker DTCK. New CUSIP for Class A is G2677P113. The reverse split aims to help Nasdaq listing compliance and potentially improve market price.
