Summary

Davis Commodities (NASDAQ: DTCK) announced a ~US$20 million commercial pipeline originating from a January 2026 international food trade exhibition, comprised of secured orders and advanced negotiations, which management says will enhance revenue visibility over the next 6–12 months. The pipeline supplements an existing recurring customer base that has generated approximately US$100 million in revenue over the past two years. Company highlights include growing repeat business driven by branded products (Maxwill), newly secured supply arrangements with several Singapore FMCG manufacturers, and expected improvements in revenue predictability, cash flow generation, working capital efficiency, and operating leverage. Executive Chairwoman Li Peng Leck emphasized disciplined execution, focus on branded and value-added products, and prudent risk management.
