Summary

Form 6-K filed by Australian Oilseeds Holdings Limited reporting the closing on March 6, 2026 of a private placement under a Securities Purchase Agreement. The Company issued 2,000,000 Units at US$1.00 per Unit for aggregate gross proceeds of US$2.0 million. Each Unit consists of one Class A ordinary share and one warrant to purchase up to two ordinary shares. An aggregate of 2,000,000 ordinary shares and 2,000,000 warrants were issued. Each warrant is exercisable immediately, has an exercise price of US$2.00 per share, and expires five years from issuance (warrants convertible into up to 4,000,000 additional ordinary shares if fully exercised). Securities were issued in a Regulation S private placement and are not registered under the U.S. Securities Act. The filing does not disclose the identities of the purchasers or any change in control; purchaser intent (passive vs. active) is not stated.
