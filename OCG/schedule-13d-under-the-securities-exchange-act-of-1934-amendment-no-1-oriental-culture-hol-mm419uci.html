Summary

Schedule 13D/A filed by Aimin Kong and Hao Shun Investments Limited reporting beneficial ownership of preferred shares in Oriental Culture Holding LTD (NASDAQ: OCG). Hao Shun owns 12,000,000 preferred shares (each with 15 votes; two preferred convertible into one ordinary) which, if converted to 6,000,000 ordinary shares, represent approximately 50.8% of outstanding ordinary shares (based on 5,814,789 ordinary shares outstanding as of Feb 18, 2026). Due to the preferred share voting multiple, the reporting persons report 180,000,000 votes of sole voting power. Aimin Kong is COO of the issuer and a 70% beneficial owner and director of Hao Shun. The filing notes a 1-for-220 reverse stock split effective Jan 16, 2026, a November 14, 2025 transfer of 490,000 ordinary shares at no cost, and related-party equity transfers of Hao Shun on Feb 3, 2026. The filing does not state a passive intent; it is made on Schedule 13D (Amendment No. 1).
