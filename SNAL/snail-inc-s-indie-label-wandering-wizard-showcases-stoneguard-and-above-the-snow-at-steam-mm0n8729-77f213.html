Summary

Snail, Inc. (NASDAQ: SNAL) announced Wandering Wizard showcased Stoneguard and Above The Snow at Steam Next Fest (Feb. 23–Mar. 2, 2026). Stoneguard will enter Early Access on March 19, 2026 on Steam. Above The Snow will debut its first public demo during Next Fest. Stoneguard is a medieval survival strategy game; Above The Snow is a tycoon strategy and city-building title with awards including Best PC City-Building Game at NYX Game Awards Season 2 2025 and nominations at Taipei Game Show 2026 and Poznań Game Arena 2025. Bellwright Maiden Voyage update boosted daily units sold by 18.5% during the Steam Winter Sale. The release also notes collaborations with retail brands and emphasizes platform visibility as a growth lever. No financial metrics (revenue or earnings) are provided in the release.
