Summary

PEDEVCO Corp. (NYSE: PED) announced a 1-for-20 reverse stock split effective March 13, 2026 at 12:01 a.m. EDT, with shares expected to trade on a split-adjusted basis on the NYSE American at market open that day. The split will reduce outstanding common shares from approximately 266.0 million to approximately 13.3 million (subject to change before the effective date); the trading symbol will remain PED and the CUSIP will change to 70532Y402. The action was approved by majority stockholders via written consent (effective February 27, 2026) and the company filed a Schedule 14C information statement on February 2, 2026; a Form 8-K will be filed on the effective date. All outstanding options and securities exercisable for common stock and equity incentive plan share counts will be adjusted; no fractional shares will be issued (cash paid in lieu). The company states the split is intended to streamline capital structure, increase per-share trading price and improve its capital markets profile following a recent merger.
