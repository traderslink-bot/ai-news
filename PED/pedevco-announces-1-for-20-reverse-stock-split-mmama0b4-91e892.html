Summary

PEDEVCO Corp. (NYSE: PED) announced a 1-for-20 reverse stock split. The split is expected to take effect March 13, 2026 at 12:01 a.m. EDT, with trading on NYSE American on a split-adjusted basis starting at market open that day. The action follows a transformative merger with portfolio companies controlled by Juniper Capital Advisors, L.P., and aims to streamline the capital structure, improve per-share metrics, and broaden investor access. Outstanding shares will decrease from about 266.0 million to about 13.3 million; fractional shares will be cashed out; the par value remains $0.001 per share; the trading ticker remains PED, while the CUSIP will change to 70532Y402.
