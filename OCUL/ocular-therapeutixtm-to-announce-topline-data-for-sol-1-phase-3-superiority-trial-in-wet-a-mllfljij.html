Summary

Ocular Therapeutix (NASDAQ: OCUL) announced it will release topline results from the SOL-1 Phase 3 superiority trial of AXPAXLI (OTX-TKI) for wet age-related macular degeneration on February 17, 2026. A webcast reviewing the topline results will begin at 8:00 AM ET that day; detailed data will be presented at the 49th Macula Society Annual Meeting (Feb 25–28, 2026). AXPAXLI is an axitinib intravitreal hydrogel using the company's ELUTYX formulation technology and is also in Phase 3 for diabetic retinal disease (including NPDR). The company markets DEXTENZA and is evaluating next steps for investigational OTX-TIC.

Positives
  • Topline SOL-1 Phase 3 results scheduled for public release on Feb 17, 2026 (webcast at 8:00 AM ET).
  • Detailed data to be presented at the 49th Macula Society Annual Meeting (Feb 25–28, 2026).
