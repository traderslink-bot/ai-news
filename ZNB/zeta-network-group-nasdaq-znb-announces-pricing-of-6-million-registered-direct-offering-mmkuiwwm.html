Summary

This press release announces that Zeta Network Group (NASDAQ: ZNB) entered a purchase agreement with institutional investors to issue up to $10.0 million of Senior 10% Original Issue Discount (OID) Convertible Promissory Notes (12-month maturity) and warrants. The initial closing will issue $6.0 million principal amount of Notes, producing gross proceeds of $5.4 million after the 10% OID, plus Warrants whose quantity will be determined by dividing $3.0 million by the VWAP of the Company’s Class A ordinary shares on the trading day before closing (adjusted for a previously announced 1-for-100 share consolidation effective March 12, 2026). The Notes bear no interest, are convertible upon issuance (subject to exceptions) at a variable conversion price with a floor; Warrants are exercisable upon issuance (subject to exceptions), expire five years from initial exercise, and have an exercise price equal to the initial conversion price with customary anti-dilution protections. A second closing for the remaining securities may occur no sooner than May 12, 2026. Maxim Group LLC is the sole placement agent. The offering will be made pursuant to an effective Form F-3 shelf registration (File No. 333-292327); a prospectus supplement will be filed with the SEC.
