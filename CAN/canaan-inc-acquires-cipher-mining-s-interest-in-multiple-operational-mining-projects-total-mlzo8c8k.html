Summary

Canaan Inc. (NASDAQ: CAN) announced it acquired Cipher Mining Technologies Inc.'s (NASDAQ: CIFR) 49% equity interest in the joint venture owning Alborz LLC, Bear LLC and Chief Mountain LLC (the "ABC Projects"). The sites provide 120 MW of power capacity and ~4.4 EH/s of operating hashrate with fleet efficiency of ~25.7 J/TH. Canaan also purchased 6,840 Avalon A15Pro mining rigs. Total consideration was approximately US$39.75 million, funded via issuance of 806,439,900 Canaan Class A ordinary shares (equivalent to 53,762,660 ADS) to Cipher at US$0.7394 per ADS, subject to a six-month lock-up. WindHQ retains a 51% stake in the ABC Projects. Canaan characterized the assets as low-cost (sub-3 cents/kWh) and strategic to its U.S. energy and AI-HPC integration plans.
