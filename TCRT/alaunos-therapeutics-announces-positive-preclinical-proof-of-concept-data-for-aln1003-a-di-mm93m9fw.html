Summary

Press release: Alaunos Therapeutics (NASDAQ: TCRT) reported early non-GLP preclinical DIO mouse data for ALN1003, an oral small-molecule, non-hormonal obesity candidate. In two studies ALN1003 produced dose-dependent body weight loss (peak mean -12.9% vs DIO control on Day 34; -10.3% at Day 48 in Study 1), favorable body composition changes driven primarily by fat loss, reductions in liver weight (Study 1: liver weight down ~43%; Study 2 high dose: up to 55% reduction), and improvements in liver enzymes (ALT, AST, ALP) and metabolic biomarkers (glucose, total cholesterol). Drug was generally tolerated; observed effects included mild, transient hypolocomotion and, at the highest dose in Study 2, slight dehydration in two mice. The company notes appetite and water intake reductions that may confound attribution of weight loss to direct drug exposure. Alaunos plans further preclinical, CMC and IND-enabling work and computational chemistry to optimize candidates. As of Sept 30, 2025 cash and equivalents were ~$1.9M with runway into Q2 2026; additional financing is planned.
