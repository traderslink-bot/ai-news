Summary

HCW Biologics Inc. announced that the Nasdaq Hearings Panel determined on February 26, 2026 that the company regained compliance with all continued listing rules of The Nasdaq Capital Market. The company said this allows continued access to public capital to advance its immunotherapy programs, including HCW9302 in a Phase 1 trial for alopecia areata. The pipeline includes preclinical candidates HCW11-018b and HCW11-040, and the company has entered two licensing agreements.
