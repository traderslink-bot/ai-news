Summary

Press release: Domo reported fiscal Q4 (ended Jan 31, 2026) total revenue of $79.6M (+1% YoY), subscription revenue $73.4M (+2%), billings $111.2M (+8%), subscription RPO $437.9M (+8%) and current subscription RPO $227.0M (+1%). Q4 GAAP operating margin was -13% (improved 2pts YoY) and non-GAAP operating margin was 10% (improved 6pts YoY). Q4 GAAP net loss was $8.0M (EPS $0.19); Q4 non-GAAP net income was $1.2M (diluted EPS $0.03). Cash and cash equivalents were ~$43.0M as of Jan 31, 2026. Full-year fiscal 2026 revenue was $318.9M (+1%), billings $318.7M (+3%), GAAP net loss $59.3M (EPS $1.45), non-GAAP net loss $1.3M, and adjusted free cash flow was a negative $0.6M (improved). Domo highlighted highest quarterly billings ever, improved retention, industry recognitions, and scheduled an earnings call.
