Summary

Domo, Inc. (NASDAQ: DOMO) announced its Board has initiated a formal process to evaluate strategic alternatives to maximize shareholder value, which may include a strategic investment, sale, business combination or other transaction. The Board has not set a timetable and made no assurance any transaction will occur. Domo reaffirmed previously provided FY2026 guidance: revenue $317.5M–$318.5M and non-GAAP net loss per share (basic and diluted) $0.07–$0.11 based on 41.0M weighted-average shares. The Board engaged Jefferies LLC as financial advisor and Goodwin Procter LLP as legal advisor. The company reiterated standard forward-looking statements and noted it did not provide a quantitative reconciliation for the non-GAAP guidance.

Positives

  • Reaffirmed non-GAAP net loss per share guidance: $0.07–$0.11 (based on 41.0M shares)

Negatives
