Summary

Allurion Technologies announced a definitive agreement for immediate exercise of outstanding warrants to purchase up to 2,659,565 shares of common stock at a reduced exercise price of $1.15 per share, generating approximately $3.0 million in gross proceeds (before advisory fees). Shares issuable upon exercise of the January 2025 and February 2025 warrants are registered for resale under Form S-3 (File No. 333-286837); shares from the November 2025 warrants are registered under Form S-3 (File No. 333-292660). Exercising holders will receive new privately issued warrants exercisable into up to 5,319,130 shares at $1.15 per share, exercisable beginning upon stockholder approval and with a five-year term. The company will file a resale registration statement for the shares underlying the new warrants. Closing is expected on or about February 25, 2026, subject to customary conditions. Roth Capital Partners is acting as financial advisor.
