Summary

Allurion Technologies, Inc. announces the immediate exercise of outstanding warrants to purchase up to 2,659,565 shares at $1.15 per share, generating approximately $3.0 million in gross proceeds before advisory fees. In connection with the exercise, holders will receive new warrants to purchase up to 5,319,130 shares in a private placement, exercusable after stockholder approval at $1.15 per share for five years. Closing is expected on or about February 25, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. Roth Capital Partners is acting as financial advisor.

Positives
  • Approximately $3.0 million gross proceeds expected from warrant exercise.
  • Immediate exercise of existing January 2025, February 2025 and November 2025 warrants.
  • New warrants to purchase up to 5,319,130 shares issued in a private placement at $1.15 per share for five years after stockholder approval.
  • Shares underlying the January 2025 and February 2025 warrants are registered for resale; shares underlying the November 2025 warrants are registered for resale.
  • Closing is targeted for on or about February 25, 2026 (subject to customary conditions).
  • Roth Capital Partners serving as financial advisor.
Negatives
  • Private placement securities are not registered under the Securities Act of 1933.
  • Sale of the new warrants and underlying shares may be subject to regulatory restrictions and requires stockholder approval for the exercise terms.
  • Closing is contingent on satisfaction of customary closing conditions; no guarantee the transaction will close.
