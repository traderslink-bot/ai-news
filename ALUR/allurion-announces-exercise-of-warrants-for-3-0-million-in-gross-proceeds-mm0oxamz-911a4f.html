Summary

Allurion Technologies, Inc. (NYSE: ALUR) announced immediate exercise of outstanding warrants to purchase up to 2,659,565 shares at $1.15 per share, generating approximately $3.0 million in gross proceeds before fees. Exercising holders will receive new warrants to purchase up to 5,319,130 shares in a private placement at $1.15 per share, exercisable after stockholder approval for five years. Closing is expected on or about February 25, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. Roth Capital Partners is the financial advisor.

Positives
  • Approximately $3.0 million gross proceeds expected from warrant exercise.
  • Existing warrants are registered for resale under Form S-3.
  • New warrants issued in a private placement to purchase up to 5,319,130 shares.
  • Estimated closing date around February 25, 2026.
  • Roth Capital Partners acting as financial advisor.
Negatives
  • New warrants and related securities are not registered under the Securities Act and will require stockholder approval to become exercisable for the five-year term.
  • Sale of the securities in the private placement may be subject to restrictions and may not occur in the United States absent registration or exemptions.
  • Closing is subject to customary closing conditions and may not occur.
  • Timing and outcome of stockholder approval and other regulatory requirements are uncertain.
