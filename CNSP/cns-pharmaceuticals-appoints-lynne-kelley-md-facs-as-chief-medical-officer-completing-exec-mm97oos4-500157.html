Summary

CNS Pharmaceuticals announced the appointment of Lynne Kelley, MD, FACS, as Chief Medical Officer, completing its executive leadership team. Dr. Kelley brings more than two decades of global leadership in clinical development, translational medicine, and regulatory strategy across oncology, rare diseases, and CNS, with experience leading Phase 1-3 programs and obtaining regulatory approvals in the US, Europe, China, and Japan. She has held chief medical officer roles at multiple biotech and medical device companies and has contributed to corporate strategy, fundraising, and M&A. CNS Pharmaceuticals develops treatments for brain and CNS cancers; its drug candidate TPI-287 is an abeotaxane that stabilizes microtubules and has been studied in over 350 patients, including monotherapy and combination regimens.
