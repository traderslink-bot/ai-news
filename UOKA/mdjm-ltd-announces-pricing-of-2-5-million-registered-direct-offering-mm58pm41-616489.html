Summary

MDJM LTD announced a registered direct offering of 24,600,000 Class A ordinary shares at $0.1015 per share, with estimated gross proceeds of approximately $2.5 million before expenses. Closing is expected on or about March 2, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. Maxim Group LLC is the sole placement agent. The offering is being made under a shelf registration on Form F-3 (File No. 333-261347), and a prospectus supplement will be filed. Notices regarding jurisdictions where offers are unlawful and standard forward-looking statements apply.
