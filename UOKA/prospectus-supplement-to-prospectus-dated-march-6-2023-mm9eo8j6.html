Summary

SEC filing (Form 424B5) by MDJM LTD: prospectus supplement for a registered offering of 24,600,000 Class A ordinary shares at $0.1015 per share (aggregate offering price $2,496,900) to certain institutional investors under Registration No. 333-261347. Placement agent: Maxim Group LLC (up to 7% fee). Expected delivery ~March 2, 2026. Proceeds intended for development and operation of the company's new cultural IP ecosystem, working capital and general corporate purposes; estimated net proceeds after placement agent fees and offering expenses approximately $2.16M (document contains slight varying net-proceeds figures). Company is listed on Nasdaq under ticker UOKA. As of the prospectus date the company had 18,479,922 Class A shares and 408,000 Class B shares outstanding. CEO Siping Xu beneficially owns 408,000 Class B and 5,529 Class A shares (20,405,529 votes), representing ~52.48% of voting power prior to this offering; upon completion of this offering his voting power would be ~32.15% (company would no longer be a Nasdaq “controlled company”). The filing discloses prior financings including a Feb 11, 2026 underwritten follow-on (4,280,000 units / ~$6M gross) and a history of inter-company transfers of offering proceeds to UK subsidiary MD UK. The prospectus highlights material risks including small operating revenues, net losses, strategic shift to a cultural IP business, Nasdaq minimum bid/listing compliance history and potential delisting risks, and UK/Europe operational and regulatory risks.
