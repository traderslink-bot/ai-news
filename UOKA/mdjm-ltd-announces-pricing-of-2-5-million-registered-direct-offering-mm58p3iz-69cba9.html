Summary

MDJM LTD announced the pricing of a registered direct offering consisting of 24,600,000 Class A ordinary shares at $0.1015 per share, with estimated gross proceeds of approximately $2.5 million before fees. The offering is expected to close on or about March 2, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. Maxim Group LLC is the sole placement agent. The offering is made pursuant to a shelf registration on Form F-3 (333-261347), declared effective in 2023, with a prospectus supplement to be filed. MDJM describes itself as a global culture innovation company and is pursuing expansion efforts in the UK.
