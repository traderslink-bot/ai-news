Summary

SEC Form 6-K filed by MDJM LTD reporting the closing of a registered direct offering. On March 2, 2026 the Company issued and sold 24,600,000 Class A ordinary shares (par value $0.025) to certain institutional investors at $0.1015 per share, generating $2,496,900 in gross proceeds before placement agent fees and offering expenses. The offering was made pursuant to the Company’s Form F-3 registration statement (File No. 333-261347) and a prospectus supplement filed March 2, 2026. MDJM entered into a Purchase Agreement with the institutional purchasers and a Placement Agency Agreement with Maxim Group LLC as exclusive placement agent; the placement agent fee is 7.0% of gross proceeds and the Company agreed to reimburse up to $60,000 of the agent’s out-of-pocket expenses. Exhibits filed include the Placement Agency Agreement (Exhibit 10.1), form of Securities Purchase Agreement (Exhibit 10.2), legal opinion of BGA Law (Cayman) Limited (Exhibit 5.1), and a press release announcing pricing (Exhibit 99.1). Purchasers are described only as “certain institutional investors”; no purchaser ownership percentages or change-of-control information is provided and the investors’ intent (active vs. passive) is not specified.
