Summary

MDJM LTD said the animated short Journey to the West has formally entered full production, led by a European directing team and produced with Abano Producións S.L., with a targeted completion in October 2026 and plans to showcase at major international film festivals and on streaming platforms.

Positives
  • Animated short Journey to the West formally enters full production.
  • European directing team led by Isabel Herguera, Gianmarco Serra, and Gina Thorstensen.
  • Production overseen by Chelo Loureiro of Abano Producións S.L., with international production expertise.
  • Abano Producións S.L. is involved as production partner.
  • Target completion set for October 2026.
  • Strategic aim to compete at top international film festivals and expand to digital platforms and streaming services.
  • MDJM positions the project as part of a broader European/global animation expansion strategy.
