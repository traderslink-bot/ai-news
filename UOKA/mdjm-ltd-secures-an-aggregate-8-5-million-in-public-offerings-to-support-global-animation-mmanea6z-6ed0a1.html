Summary

MDJM LTD completed two offerings totaling approximately $8.5 million in February and March 2026. An upsized public offering on February 11, 2026 raised about $6 million, and a registered direct offering on March 2, 2026 raised about $2.5 million. Roughly 50% of the combined proceeds will fund international animation collaborations and the development of Fernie Castle in Scotland, including a Fernie Castle Animation Museum and Classical Oriental Garden. Co-productions with H5 and Abano Producións are planned, with design work by KKAA and Simpson & Brown. The initiative aims to create a global cultural development framework integrating animation production, cultural infrastructure, and cross-border collaboration.
