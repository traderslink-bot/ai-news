Summary

MDJM LTD completed two financing transactions totaling approximately USD 8.5 million in February and March 2026, consisting of an upsized public offering (~USD 6.0 million) and a registered direct offering (~USD 2.5 million). About 50% of the combined proceeds will fund international animation collaborations and the Fernie Castle cultural development project in Scotland, including a Fernie Castle Animation Museum and a Classical Oriental Garden, with design by KKAA and Simpson & Brown. Co-productions with H5 and Abano Producións are in active production, targeted for completion by end-2026. The company notes forward-looking statements and related risks.
