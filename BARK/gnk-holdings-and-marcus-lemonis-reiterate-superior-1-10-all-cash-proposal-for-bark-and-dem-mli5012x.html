Summary

GNK Holdings LLC and Marcus Lemonis delivered a letter to the Special Committee of BARK, Inc. (NYSE: BARK) reiterating a non-binding, all-cash take-private indication of interest at $1.10 per share (a 22% premium to a previously announced related-party $0.90 per share CEO proposal). The Group says the Special Committee has not engaged in substantive discussions, criticizes a proposed NDA/standstill from BARK's counsel, refuses to sign it, and demands a response. The Group intends to make the letter public and states its view that its proposal best maximizes shareholder value. The proposal remains non-binding and subject to customary approvals and financing.

Positives
  • Proposed $1.10 per share all-cash offer represents a 22% premium to the CEO's $0.90 related-party proposal
  • Offer is all-cash, potentially providing immediate liquidity to shareholders
  • GNK Holdings and Marcus Lemonis are publicly pressing the Special Committee, which may force engagement or a better process
  • Palladium Capital Group engaged as financial advisor to GNK/Lemonis
Negatives
  • Proposal is a preliminary, non-binding indication of interest and not a committed transaction
  • BARK's Special Committee has, according to the Group, not engaged in substantive discussions
  • BARK (via counsel) proposed an NDA/standstill that GNK/Lemonis consider coercive and are refusing to sign
  • Related-party CEO proposal at $0.90 remains on the table and the board's treatment of that bid is unclear
  • Transaction remains subject to approvals, potential financing risks and other customary contingencies
