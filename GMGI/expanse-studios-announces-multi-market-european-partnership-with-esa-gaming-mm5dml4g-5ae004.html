Summary

Expanse Studios, a subsidiary of Golden Matrix Group Inc. (GMGI), announced a strategic partnership with ESA Gaming to distribute Expanse's proprietary content across ESA Gaming's operator network, enabling multi-jurisdiction European expansion and Malta market access; the collaboration leverages ESA's Game Aggregator System and OpenSlots no-code builder.

Positives
  • Expanse Studios' content will be distributed across ESA Gaming's operator network.
  • Immediate Malta market access and foundation for broader EU market entries.
  • Multi-jurisdictional distribution across Southern Europe markets with regulatory alignment.
  • ESA Gaming's Game Aggregator System provides access to thousands of third-party games via a single integration and OpenSlots no-code builder.
  • Alignment of Expanse's top-performing titles (e.g., Super Heli, Wild Icy Fruits, VASO Psycho, Titan Roulette) with priority expansion markets.
  • Golden Matrix Group's B2B and B2C footprint supports expanded distribution of Expanse titles.

  • None
