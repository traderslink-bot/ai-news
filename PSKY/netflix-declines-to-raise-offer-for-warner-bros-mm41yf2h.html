Summary

Netflix announced it will not raise or match Paramount Skydance's (PSKY) latest proposal for Warner Bros. after Warner Bros. Discovery's (WBD) board determined the PSKY bid qualified as a "Superior Proposal" under WBD's merger agreement with Netflix. Co-CEOs Ted Sarandos and Greg Peters said the price required to match PSKY's offer made the transaction financially unattractive. Netflix reiterated its business is healthy, said it will invest approximately $20 billion in content this year, and will resume its share repurchase program. The release references WBD's definitive proxy statement filed on Schedule 14A (mailed around Feb. 17, 2026) and directs investors to review the proxy and related SEC filings.
