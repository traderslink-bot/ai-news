Summary

Warner Bros. Discovery (WBD) announced a Special Meeting of shareholders to vote on the merger with Netflix on March 20, 2026 (8:00 a.m. ET) and has commenced mailing the definitive proxy statement. WBD's Board unanimously recommends shareholders vote FOR the Netflix merger and recommends rejecting Paramount Skydance's (PSKY) tender offer. Netflix granted WBD a seven-day waiver (through Feb 23, 2026) permitting WBD to engage with PSKY to seek clarity on outstanding deficiencies and solicit PSKY's best and final offer; PSKY reportedly informed WBD it would agree to pay $31 per share pending engagement and that $31 was not its "best and final." Netflix retains its matching rights under the merger agreement. WBD sent PSKY a letter and draft transaction agreements to confirm binding terms; WBD referenced its Schedule 14D-9 amendment and urged shareholders to review the proxy materials.

Positives
  • WBD Board unanimously recommends shareholders vote FOR the Netflix merger
  • Special Meeting set for March 20, 2026 and definitive proxy statement being mailed
  • Netflix granted a limited seven-day waiver allowing WBD to engage PSKY for clarifications and a best-and-final offer
  • Netflix retains matching rights under the existing merger agreement
Negatives
  • WBD views PSKY's tender offer as containing unresolved deficiencies and unfavorable terms
  • PSKY's proposed financing raises material closing uncertainty compared with Netflix's investment-grade financing
  • WBD has not determined PSKY's proposal is reasonably likely to be superior to the Netflix transaction
  • No assurance that engagement with PSKY will produce a definitive, superior transaction
