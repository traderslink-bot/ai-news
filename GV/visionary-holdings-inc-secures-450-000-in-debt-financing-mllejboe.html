Summary

Visionary Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: GV) announced it issued a senior secured convertible promissory note dated December 2025 with a $500,000 principal amount in exchange for $450,000 gross proceeds from an existing institutional investor. The December 2025 Note matures on December 11, 2026, accrues interest at the greater of prime + 4.5% or 9% per annum (payable semi‑annually beginning June 11, 2026), and carries an 18% interest rate upon an event of default. The note is convertible at the investor's option into common stock at $1.44 per share (including principal and accrued amounts), subject to customary adjustments and Nasdaq listing rules. The note was issued in a private placement exempt from registration. The company previously issued a $1,500,000 senior secured convertible note to the same investor on January 31, 2025 (conversion price then $2.25), and states the December note updates economic and conversion terms versus the January note.

Positives
  • Raised $450,000 in gross proceeds from an institutional investor
  • Debt instrument is senior secured
  • Conversion mechanics and updated terms negotiated with an existing investor
Negatives
  • December note issued at a discount (principal $500,000 for $450,000 proceeds)
  • Conversion price of $1.44 per share could be dilutive if converted
  • Interest floor is high (minimum 9% per annum) and default rate increases to 18%
