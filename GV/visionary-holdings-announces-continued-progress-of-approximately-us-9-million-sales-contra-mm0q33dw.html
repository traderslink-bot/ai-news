Summary

Visionary Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: GV) announced that its wholly owned subsidiary, Visionary Health Technology Group Limited (GV Hong Kong), is advancing implementation under a commercial sales agreement signed in September 2025 with a total contract value of approximately US$9 million. Early-stage service activities are underway and the company expects further substantive execution milestones by the end of Q1 2026. The contract covers the Premier Regenerative Complex product series and customized VIP health services. Any revenue recognition and cash collection will follow the agreement terms and applicable accounting standards.
