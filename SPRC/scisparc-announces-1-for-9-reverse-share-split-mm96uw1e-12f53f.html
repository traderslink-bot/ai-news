Summary

SciSparc Ltd. intends to implement a 1-for-9 reverse stock split of its ordinary shares, effective at market open on March 4, 2026. Outstanding ordinary shares will decrease from about 5,089,501 to approximately 565,500; trading will continue on Nasdaq Capital Market under the SPRC ticker on a split-adjusted basis. The new CUSIP is M82618139. The reverse split was approved by shareholders at the AGM held June 26, 2025. No fractional shares will be issued; fractions will be rounded to the nearest whole share; there will be a proportionate adjustment to the exercise price and the number of shares issuable upon exercise of all outstanding options and warrants. No action is required by shareholders; the exchange will be processed automatically by brokers.
