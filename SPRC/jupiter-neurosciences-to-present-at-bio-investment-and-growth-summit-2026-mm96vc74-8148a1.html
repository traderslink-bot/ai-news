Summary

Jupiter Neurosciences announced that management will present at the BIO Investment and Growth Summit 2026 in Miami Beach, March 2–3, 2026. The company is advancing an FDA-cleared Phase IIa Parkinson's program targeting neuroinflammation, with IND clearance in November 2025. The presentation will cover the Phase IIa program, JOTROL bioavailability improvements (nine-fold vs. standard resveratrol formulations), translational proof-of-concept in Parkinson's models, and revenue generation through Nugevia. Jupiter describes a dual-path strategy combining clinical development with commercial revenue, and notes ongoing Nasdaq communications.
