Summary

Tokyo Lifestyle Co., Ltd. announced that its wholly-owned Hong Kong subsidiary, Tokyo Lifestyle Limited (TLS), entered into a five-year HKD 7.5 million (approximately US$0.96 million) loan agreement on February 19, 2026, with Mei Kanayama, Representative Director and Principal Executive Officer, for TLS's working capital and operating needs. The loan runs from February 1, 2026, to January 31, 2031, with a 4.35% annual interest rate and repayment per a mutually agreed schedule; any remaining principal and interest are due at maturity. The funding is intended to support TLS's expansion, omnichannel integration, and market reach into Macau and Mainland China. Hong Kong retail sales are projected to grow about 8% in 2026, with pharmaceuticals and cosmetics growing around 11%. Mei Kanayama emphasized commitment to TLS's growth and strategic initiatives.

Positives
  • Interest rate of 4.35% annually is described as favorable relative to prevailing commercial lending rates in a high-interest-rate environment.
  • Provides TLS with working capital to support ongoing expansion, omnichannel integration, and strategic initiatives.
  • Funding may bolster TLS's market presence and sales network in Hong Kong and adjacent markets such as Macau and Mainland China.
