Summary

SciSparc Ltd. intends to effect a 1-for-9 reverse stock split of its ordinary shares, effective at market open on March 4, 2026. Outstanding shares will be reduced from about 5,089,501 to approximately 565,500, with about 561,533 publicly held. Shares will trade on Nasdaq Capital Market under SPRC on a split-adjusted basis starting March 4, 2026. New CUSIP M82618139. The authorized share capital remains unchanged at 2,000,000,000. The split will uniformly affect all holders; fractional shares will be rounded and options/warrants adjusted accordingly. No action is required by shareholders; the transfer agent is VStock Transfer. Forward-looking statements and associated risks are noted in filings.
