Summary

Scinai Immunotherapeutics Ltd. (Nasdaq: SCNI) executed a Second Amendment to its Binding Option Agreement to acquire PinCell S.r.l., extending the deadline to fulfill option conditions to August 31, 2026 and the option exercise period to September 30, 2026. The company submitted a revised application to the European FENG SMART Path program seeking €12 million in non-dilutive, non-repayable funding (80% of a €15 million integrated R&D program) to advance PC111, a fully human monoclonal antibody targeting soluble Fas Ligand, through non-clinical work and First-in-Human proof-of-concept for pemphigus vulgaris and SJS/TEN. Scinai expects a funding decision in approximately three to four months. The grant, if awarded, would support early clinical development while minimizing shareholder dilution; the release reiterates forward-looking risks including grant non-approval, development and regulatory risks, and potential capital needs.
