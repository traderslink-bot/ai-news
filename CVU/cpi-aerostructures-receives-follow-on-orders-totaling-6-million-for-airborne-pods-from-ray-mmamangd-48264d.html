Summary

CPI Aerostructures announced funded orders totaling $6 million for Airborne Pods from Raytheon's Advanced Products & Solutions unit. The orders have a not-to-exceed ceiling of $12 million and cover design and development of assembly tools, fixturing, monolithic parts, assembly and integration of the pod, and testing. CPI Aero states manufacturing airborne pods is a core competency and notes continued trust from Raytheon and other OEMs.
