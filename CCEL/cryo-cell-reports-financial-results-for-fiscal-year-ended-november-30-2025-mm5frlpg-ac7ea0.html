Summary

Cryo-Cell International, Inc. (NYSE American LLC: CCEL) reported fiscal 2025 results ended November 30, 2025. Revenue totaled $31.6 million, compared with $32.0 million in fiscal 2024, with $31.4 million in processing and storage revenue, $54,000 in product revenue and $130,000 in public banking revenue for 2025 versus $31.6 million, $68,000 and $367,000 respectively in 2024. The company posted a net loss of $2.4 million ($0.30 per basic and diluted share) for 2025, compared with net income of $402,000 ($0.05 per share) in 2024. An impairment charge of $4.4 million was recognized in Q4 2025 to reduce public inventory from cost to net realizable value.

Positives
  • Revenue remained essentially flat year over year (31.6 million in 2025 vs 32.0 million in 2024).
Negatives
  • Net loss of $2.4 million in fiscal 2025 ($0.30 per share).
  • Impairment charge of $4.4 million recognized in Q4 2025 to reduce public inventory to net realizable value.
  • Total revenue declined from $32.0 million in 2024 to $31.6 million in 2025.
  • Product revenue declined from $68,000 to $54,000 and public banking revenue declined from $367,000 to $130,000.
