Summary

Akanda Corp and its subsidiary First Towers & Fiber Corp plan to expand its approximately 700-kilometre dark fiber network in Central Mexico, the largest in the region. The network was constructed under a 20-year lease with Telefónica, which serves as anchor tenant and currently leases two fiber pairs; Marcatel also leases capacity. The company intends to pursue additional dark fiber lease agreements in 2026 to grow recurring revenue with limited incremental capital investment.
