Summary

Adial Pharmaceuticals announced a collaboration framework with Molteni Farmaceutici for exclusive European commercialization of AD04, its lead drug for Alcohol Use Disorder in heavy drinkers. The framework contemplates upfront payments, milestones, and tiered royalties totaling up to about $60 million, contingent on a final definitive agreement. The deal covers clinical, regulatory, manufacturing, and commercial terms. Adial plans a Phase 3 program in Europe and is pursuing U.S. partners for a pivotal Phase 3; FDA End-of-Phase-2 minutes provide input into the Phase 3 design. ONWARD data showed reduced drinking with no overt safety concerns. AD04 may have potential in other addictive disorders. The collaboration represents Adial's first commercial partnership.
