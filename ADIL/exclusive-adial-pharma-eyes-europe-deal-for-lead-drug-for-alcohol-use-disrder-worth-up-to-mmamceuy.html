Summary

Adial Pharmaceuticals (NASDAQ: ADIL) entered a collaboration framework agreement with Molteni Farmaceutici to pursue an exclusive commercialization partnership for AD04 in Europe. The framework, subject to a definitive agreement, covers clinical, regulatory, manufacturing and commercial planning and gives Molteni an exclusivity period to evaluate the program. The proposed deal could include an upfront payment, milestone payments and tiered royalties (high single digits to low double digits), with total potential milestones and royalties estimated at nearly $60 million if AD04 advances and is launched in Europe. AD04 is Adial's lead investigational program for Alcohol Use Disorder (targeting certain genotypes); the company plans a pivotal Phase 3 program and has FDA End-of-Phase-2 meeting minutes informing its Phase 3 design. ADIL shares traded higher in premarket following the news.
