Summary

Adial Pharmaceuticals (NASDAQ: ADIL) entered a collaboration framework with Molteni Farmaceutici for an exclusive European commercialization partnership for AD04, subject to a definitive agreement. Molteni has exclusivity to evaluate feasibility, due diligence and commercialization planning. The anticipated definitive deal would include an upfront payment, development/commercial milestones and tiered royalties (high single digits to low double digits) on European net sales; aggregate potential royalties and milestones are estimated at nearly $60 million over time, contingent on AD04 progressing and being commercialized. AD04 is a genetically targeted serotonin-3 receptor antagonist being developed for Alcohol Use Disorder in patients with a responsive genotype; Adial is preparing a Phase 3 program, has manufacturing agreements with Thermo Fisher and Cambrex, filed a patent that could extend loss of exclusivity to 2045 if granted, and replaced a blood test with a cheek swab genetic screening method. The framework is non-binding until a definitive agreement is executed and outcomes depend on clinical, regulatory and commercial success.
