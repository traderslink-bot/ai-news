Summary

Adial Pharmaceuticals announced a collaboration framework with Molteni Farmaceutici to pursue an exclusive European partnership to commercialize AD04 for Alcohol Use Disorder. The framework outlines clinical, regulatory, manufacturing, and commercial terms and contemplates upfront payments, milestones, and tiered royalties on European AD04 net sales, with a total potential value near $60 million. The definitive agreement is subject to execution of a final agreement. Molteni has exclusivity to evaluate feasibility and plan a European launch. Adial is pursuing a U.S. Phase 3 program for AD04 and has had FDA input on the design. ADIL shares rose in premarket trading.

  • Potential total value up to $60 million in milestones and royalties
  • First commercial partnership for Adial
  • AD04 advancing toward Phase 3 and potential expansion to other addictive disorders
Negatives
  • Agreement not yet final; final definitive agreement required
