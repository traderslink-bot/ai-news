Summary

Envirotech Vehicles, Inc. (NASDAQ: EVTV) announced it accepted delivery of initial high-performance compute systems from AZIO AI for a South Texas site. EVTV plans near-term installation, commissioning, calibration and staged integration into its modular AI infrastructure; monetization of AI workloads or digital asset production would occur only after full integration. The companies remain in due diligence and EVTV is pursuing preliminary discussions (including non-binding LOIs) for up to five additional potential sites. Management emphasizes energy sourcing and phased deployment; no assurance of transaction completion or timing was provided.
