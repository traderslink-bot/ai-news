Summary

NAPC Defense describes itself as a U.S.-based, service-disabled veteran-founded defense contractor registered with the Directorate of Defense Trade Controls. It provides military hardware, munitions, and construction services to the U.S. military, NATO allies, and law enforcement, with capabilities in ammunition, weapons, airframes, and platform lifecycle management. The company positions itself as a DDTC-registered broker handling export licensing, supported by an ISO 9001:2008-based quality system. Focus areas include small arms and ammunition, visual augmentation systems, jamming and countermeasure equipment, NIJ-certified body armor, and Corner Shot systems. It states it will produce Corner Shot USA weapons under an exclusive licensing agreement and brokers munitions worldwide, subject to U.S. State Department approval; inventory is dynamic.
