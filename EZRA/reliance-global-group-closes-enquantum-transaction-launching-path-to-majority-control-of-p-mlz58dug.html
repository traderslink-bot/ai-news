Summary

Reliance Global Group, Inc. (NASDAQ: EZRA) announced completion of an initial strategic acquisition of Enquantum Ltd., a post-quantum cryptography company. Reliance obtained an initial equity position by converting a previously issued secured bridge note and making an additional cash investment. The deal is structured as milestone-based tranche investments intended to increase Reliance's ownership over time to a 51% fully diluted controlling interest and to confer majority governance rights upon achievement and funding of specified milestones. Enquantum develops hardware-accelerated, NIST-aligned post-quantum cryptographic solutions (including a 2025 FPGA-related patent). This transaction is the first platform investment under Reliance's Scale51 operating model and part of its EZRA International Group strategy. The release includes forward-looking statements and enumerates risks related to milestone achievement, funding, integration, commercialization, and market adoption.
