Summary

Maris-Tech Ltd. announced a deal to integrate its Jupiter Drone edge video processing system into loitering munition drones (kamikaze drones) from a leading manufacturer. The order follows a February 19, 2026 production order and signals move from pilots to volume production. The Jupiter platform provides onboard AI video processing for real-time target detection and tracking, ultra-low latency streaming with H.265 compression, and a compact, low-power design suitable for small, autonomous platforms, including operation in disconnected or bandwidth-constrained environments. The loitering munitions market is expanding due to rising defense budgets and demand for autonomous drone capabilities; Maris-Tech's positioning emphasizes AI-enabled video intelligence for defense applications.
