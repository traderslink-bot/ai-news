Summary

Maris-Tech Ltd., an Israeli technology company focused on edge AI video processing for defense, announced a deal to integrate its Jupiter Drone edge video processing system into loitering munitions (kamikaze) drones produced by a leading manufacturer in the sector. The company had announced a February 19, 2026 production order, moving from pilot programs to volume production. The Jupiter Drone platform features H.265 video compression with ultra-low latency streaming, built-in AI acceleration for object detection and tracking, and a compact, low-power design suitable for small, autonomous platforms that can operate in disconnected or bandwidth-limited environments. The loitering munitions market is growing due to rising defense budgets and demand for autonomous, cost-effective weapons systems; Israel is a notable leader in this domain. The development could enable follow-on orders and deeper partnerships with major manufacturers.
