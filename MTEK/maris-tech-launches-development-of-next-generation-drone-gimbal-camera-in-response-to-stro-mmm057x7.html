Summary

Press release: Maris-Tech Ltd. (NASDAQ: MTEK, MTEKW) announced development of an advanced, lightweight drone gimbal camera combining a 4K day camera and thermal sensor with onboard AI-powered edge analytics for real-time video streaming. The device is being developed to meet strict SWaP requirements for defense and surveillance UAVs, builds on Maris‑Tech's existing field‑proven video and AI platforms deployed on thousands of drone systems, and is expected to be completed by the end of 2026. The company cites strong customer demand from drone manufacturers and defense integrators as the driver for this initiative.
