Summary

Flywire (NASDAQ: FLYW) reported Q4 2025 revenue of $157.5M, up 34.0% year-over-year; revenue less ancillary services was $152.7M, up 35.3% YOY. Total payment volume rose 35.6% to $9.3B; the acquired Sertifi contributed $14.2M of Q4 revenue. Q4 GAAP net income was effectively break-even ($0.0M) vs. a $15.9M loss in Q4 2024; FY2025 net income was $13.5M. Adjusted EBITDA for Q4 was $25.4M (16.6% margin), up from $16.7M a year earlier. Flywire repurchased ~0.7M shares for ~$10M in Q4 with ~$182M remaining under the repurchase program and paid down the remaining $15M of debt used to fund the Sertifi acquisition. Cash and cash equivalents were $330.3M at year-end. Flywire provided FY2026 guidance of FX-neutral revenue less ancillary services growth of 15–21% and adjusted EBITDA margin expansion of 150–350 bps; Q1 2026 FX-neutral revenue less ancillary services growth guidance is 26–30% with adjusted EBITDA margin expansion of 100–350 bps.
