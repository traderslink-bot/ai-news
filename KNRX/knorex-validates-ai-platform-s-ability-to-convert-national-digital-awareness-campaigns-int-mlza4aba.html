Summary

KNOREX Ltd. (NYSE: KNRX) announces results from a large-scale automotive campaign using its XPO platform to convert national digital awareness into dealership visits at scale. Campaign ran during the Black Friday period with over 106 million impressions. 6.9+ million video interactions and engagement rates up to 28% were achieved. 54,000+ offline dealership visits were tracked, linking digital engagement to showroom traffic. Display CPMs were below $2.50; video CPMs ranged from $3.40 to $4.20 during peak demand; cost per acquisition for dealership visits was below $4. Automated optimization managed bidding, creative sequencing, and cross-channel budgeting in real time, with KNOREX’s managed services providing strategic oversight. The release notes growing demand in automotive advertising for measurable, scalable outcomes. The document contains forward-looking statements regarding a proposed Offering and its timing, with standard risk disclosures; no revenue or earnings figures are provided for the campaign itself.
