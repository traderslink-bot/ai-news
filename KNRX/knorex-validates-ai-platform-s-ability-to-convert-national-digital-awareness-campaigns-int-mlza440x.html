Summary

KNOREX Ltd. (NYSE: KNRX) issued a press release reporting results from a large-scale automotive Black Friday campaign run on its AI-powered XPO platform for a U.S. dealer network partner. Key metrics: >106 million impressions, 6.9+ million video interactions, engagement rates up to 28%, 54,000+ tracked offline dealership visits, display CPMs below $2.50, video CPMs $3.40–$4.20, and cost-per-acquisition below $4 for dealership visits. KNOREX says XPO's AI optimized bidding, creative sequencing, and cross-channel budget allocation in real time and positions the platform for expanded adoption in the automotive and multi-location retail verticals. The release includes standard forward-looking statement cautions regarding future results and a referenced proposed offering.
