Summary

Protara Therapeutics (NASDAQ: TARA) announced interim Phase 2 ADVANCED-2 results for intravesical TARA-002 in high-risk NMIBC. BCG-Unresponsive cohort: 35 evaluable participants (22 evaluable at 6 months, 15 at 12 months); CR at any time 65.7% (23/35); 6-month CR 68.2% (15/22); 12-month CR 33.3% (5/15); KM-estimated probability of maintaining CR at 6 months 71.1% (95% CI: 46.7–95.5). BCG-Naïve cohort: 29 evaluable (27 at 6 months, 19 at 12 months); CR at any time 72.4% (21/29); 6-month CR 66.7% (18/27); 12-month CR 57.9% (11/19); KM 6-month CR maintenance 73.1% (95% CI: 52.9–93.4). Safety: mostly Grade 1 transient TRAEs, no Grade 3+ treatment-related AEs, no related serious adverse events, and no discontinuations for TRAEs. Company expects to complete enrollment of the BCG-Unresponsive registrational cohort in 2H 2026, enrollment complete in the BCG-Naïve cohort (31 patients), and plans to initiate ADVANCED-3 in 2H 2026.
