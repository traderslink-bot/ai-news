Summary

Fusion Fuel Green PLC (NASDAQ: HTOO) announced a definitive share exchange agreement to acquire a controlling interest (expected 75–100%) in Royal Uranium Inc., giving Fusion Fuel a portfolio of 16 uranium and 3 producing natural gas royalties in the Americas. Consideration is up to 3,750,025 Fusion Fuel shares. The deal is subject to Irish Takeover Panel review, Fusion Fuel shareholder approval at an EGM, and other closing conditions, with closing expected in H1 2026. Company highlights the portfolio’s exposure to uranium (tier-one jurisdictions and partners) and producing Alberta gas royalties for near-term cash flow, and positions the company for royalty-based, capital-light, high-margin revenue participation in uranium and natural gas markets.

Positives
  • Acquisition would add 16 uranium and 3 natural gas royalties, diversifying revenue exposure
  • Includes producing Alberta natural gas royalties expected to generate near-term cash flow
  • Consideration via share exchange (up to 3,750,025 shares) implies no immediate cash outlay
  • Royalty model avoids direct operating risk and capital expenditure obligations
  • Targets assets in tier-one jurisdictions and with established industry partners
Negatives
  • Transaction requires Irish Takeover Panel approval and Fusion Fuel shareholder approval at an EGM
  • Closing subject to additional conditions and third-party consents; not guaranteed
  • Realization of revenue depends on development and performance of third-party operators and commodity prices
  • Forward-looking benefits depend on successful integration and execution and carry typical market and regulatory risks
