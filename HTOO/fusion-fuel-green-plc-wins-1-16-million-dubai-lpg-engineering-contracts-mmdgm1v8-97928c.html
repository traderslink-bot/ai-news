Summary

Fusion Fuel Green PLC (NASDAQ: HTOO) announced two Dubai engineering subcontracts totaling approximately $1.16 million to design, supply, install, test, and commission centralized LPG systems for two residential developments. Work starts immediately; a potential LPG utility operations subcontract could cover about 2,900 apartments and six boiler rooms, creating recurring revenue opportunities. The company will expand its LPG delivery fleet with additional trucks in 2026. In February, Fusion Fuel Green announced a deal to acquire a controlling stake in Royal Uranium Inc., issuing up to 3.75 million shares; the deal would add 16 uranium royalties and 3 natural gas royalties. HTOO shares were up 3.37% in premarket trading at $3.37, near a 52-week low of $2.92.
