Summary

Clarivate reported Q4 2025 revenue of $617.0 million and full-year 2025 revenue of $2,455.2 million, declines versus 2024 driven by divestitures. Organic revenue declined 0.1% for the year; organic ACV grew 1.8%. Recurring revenues accounted for 88% of total in 2025. Net income for the year was a loss of $201.1 million; adjusted net income was $468.1 million. Adjusted EBITDA was $1,001.8 million for the year. Operating cash flow was $628.5 million and free cash flow was $365.3 million, up 7.8% year over year. The company repurchased 56.0 million shares in 2025 (~$224.5 million). As of December 31, 2025, cash and cash equivalents were $329.2 million and total debt was $4,469.9 million. Management provided a 2026 outlook: revenue of $2.30–$2.42 billion; Adjusted EBITDA of $980–$1,040 million; adjusted diluted EPS of $0.70–$0.80; free cash flow of $365–$435 million; organic ACV growth of 2.0%–3.0%. The company is pursuing the sale of its Life Sciences & Healthcare segment with Morgan Stanley advising; no guarantee the sale will occur.
