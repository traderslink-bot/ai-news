Summary

Classover Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: KIDZ, NASDAQ: KIDZW) announced the Board unanimously approved termination of its $400 million Equity Purchase Facility Agreement with Solana Strategic Holdings LLC, ending its Solana-focused digital asset treasury strategy. The Board determined the strategy is not accretive under current market conditions; termination removes the potential for significant share dilution. The company says it will redirect capital toward AI and robotics initiatives, maintains a healthy balance sheet with no imminent liquidity needs, and has not sold existing Solana holdings or staking yields (which may be evaluated for future divestiture).
