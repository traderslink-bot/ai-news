Summary

QuantumNexis Inc., a subsidiary of Healthcare Triangle, launched its Dubai, UAE operations in Meydan Free Zone to deliver clinically validated digital mental health solutions and an AI-powered Hospital Information System to GCC markets. The initiative aims to support national health modernization efforts in the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Oman, and Bahrain, leveraging partnerships with Ezovion Health OS, Ziloy.ai and Better.care, as well as alignment with Microsoft. The GCC digital health market is projected to grow, with the UAE expected to reach USD 1.84 billion by 2030 and Saudi Arabia over USD 11 billion by 2033.
