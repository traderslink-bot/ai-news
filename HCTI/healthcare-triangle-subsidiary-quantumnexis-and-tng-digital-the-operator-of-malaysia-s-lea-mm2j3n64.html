Summary

Healthcare Triangle, Inc. (Nasdaq: HCTI) subsidiary QuantumNexis entered a strategic partnership with TNG Digital to integrate QuantumNexis's Ziloy digital mental health platform into TNG eWallet, providing clinically validated, multilingual mental wellbeing services to TNG's more than 25 million verified Malaysian users. The integration offers in-app discovery, single sign-on, and frictionless payments via TNG eWallet. QuantumNexis expects meaningful adoption in 6–12 months and projects Malaysia-generated transaction revenue in the growing six-figure range over the next two quarters. The company positions the deal as a scalable model for broader Southeast Asia rollout and public health collaboration.
