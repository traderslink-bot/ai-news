Summary

PRF Technologies Ltd. (NASDAQ: PRFX) announced that its DeepSolar unit has entered into a solar analytics services agreement with a special-purpose company of EDF Power Solutions Israel (a subsidiary of EDF Group) to perform a technical due diligence (TDD) engagement on an operating utility-scale solar plant in Israel. DeepSolar has begun intake and analysis of historical and operational plant data using its Smart TDD analytics to assess asset condition and performance. The engagement is described as the first commercial TDD engagement for DeepSolar and is positioned as a repeatable analytics-driven service for use cases including commissioning, warranty expiry review, underperformance diagnosis, and asset acquisition. No financial terms were disclosed.
