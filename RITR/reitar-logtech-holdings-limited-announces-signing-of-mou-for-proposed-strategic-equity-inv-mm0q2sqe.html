Summary

Reitar Logtech Holdings Limited (NASDAQ: RITR) announced a non-binding Memorandum of Understanding with Equator Capital Management SPC (for the Segregated Portfolio "Equator Private Equity Fund SP") for a proposed strategic equity investment of up to US$60.0 million via subscription of newly issued ordinary shares at US$4.00 per share. Proceeds are intended primarily (≥92%) to fund Reitar's capital contribution to a consortium, alongside a large industrial private equity partner, to acquire a controlling interest in an international logistics company (Target). Completion is subject to definitive documentation, due diligence, regulatory and corporate approvals, and the Target achieving audited EBITDA of at least US$8.0 million for its most recently completed fiscal year. The MOU grants the investor 90 days of exclusivity, is non‑binding except for exclusivity, confidentiality, costs/expenses and certain other provisions, and may terminate if definitive documents are not executed within the exclusivity period.
