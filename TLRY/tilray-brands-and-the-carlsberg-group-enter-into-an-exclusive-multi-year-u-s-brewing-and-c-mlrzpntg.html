Summary

Tilray Brands announced an exclusive multi-year U.S. licensing agreement with the Carlsberg Group to produce, market, sell and distribute Carlsberg®, Carlsberg Elephant®, 1664® and Kronenbourg 1664 Blanc® in the United States beginning January 1, 2027. The agreement has an initial five-year term with an automatic five-year renewal subject to performance criteria. Tilray will use its U.S. brewing facilities, sales and marketing teams, and distribution network to locally manufacture and commercialize the Carlsberg portfolio; the company says the deal will drive volume growth, expand shelf presence and accelerate revenue once the contract commences. Financial terms were not disclosed.

Positives
  • Exclusive multi-year U.S. license for several major Carlsberg brands starting Jan 1, 2027
  • Initial five-year term with automatic five-year renewal subject to performance
  • Expected to drive immediate scale and revenue accretion upon contract commencement
  • Expands Tilray's U.S. beverage portfolio and leverages existing brewing and distribution infrastructure
Negatives
  • No financial terms or consideration disclosed
  • Contract does not commence until Jan 1, 2027, so revenue impact is delayed
