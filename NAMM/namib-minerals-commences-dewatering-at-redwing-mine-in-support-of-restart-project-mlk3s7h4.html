Summary

Press release from Namib Minerals (NASDAQ: NAMM) announcing that dewatering at the Redwing Mine in Zimbabwe began on January 29, 2026, per the Company's November 2025 work plan. The dewatering phase will enable access for feasibility work, including underground condition assessments and detailed engineering evaluations to inform the planned restart of Redwing. Company states progress is consistent with planned enabling works and emphasizes a safety-focused approach. The release reiterates Namib Minerals operates the How Mine, is targeting reserve growth and a restart of Redwing, and includes standard forward-looking statements and risks (including the need to raise required capital).
