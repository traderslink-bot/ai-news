Summary

Form 6-K from Namib Minerals (Commission File No. 001-42685) reports that the company received a Nasdaq notice on January 30, 2026 that it did not meet the minimum market value of publicly held shares (MVPHS) requirement of $15,000,000 under Nasdaq Listing Rule 5450(b)(2)(C). On February 18, 2026 Nasdaq notified the company that it met the MVPHS requirement for more than ten consecutive trading days and has therefore regained compliance; the matter is closed. The company issued a related press release dated February 19, 2026 (attached as Exhibit 99.1). The filing indicates the registrant files annual reports on Form 20-F.
