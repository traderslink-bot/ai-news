Summary

3 E Network Technology Group Limited (NASDAQ: MASK) announced on Feb. 13, 2026 that its Mikkeli, Finland project has been designated the firm's "Nordic Compute Gateway." The company says the greenfield site will host AI-native nodes optimized for next‑generation GPU/HPC workloads, leveraging Finland's low‑carbon power (wind and nuclear), natural free‑cooling and fiber backbone connectivity to improve total cost of ownership and enable low‑latency links to European IXPs. The initiative is presented as part of 3 E's global "Gateway Model" to expand its compute scheduling network and digital‑sovereignty positioning; no capacity, timeline, funding or commercial customer commitments were disclosed. The release includes standard forward‑looking statement cautions.

  • Planned use of free cooling and competitive base power could materially improve TCO
  • Connectivity to Nordic fiber backbone and IXPs supports low‑latency, cross‑regional orchestration
