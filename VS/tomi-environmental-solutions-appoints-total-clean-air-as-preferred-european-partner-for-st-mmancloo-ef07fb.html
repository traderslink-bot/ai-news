Summary

TOMI Environmental Solutions, Inc. (NASDAQ: TOMZ) announced that Total Clean Air (TCA) will be the Preferred European Partner for the SteraMist iHP decontamination technology, covering sales, service, and technical support across the United Kingdom and the European Union. TCA will maintain consignment stock and a workshop facility, and will provide annual calibration, routine inspections, and validation support. A demonstration and trial program will be hosted at TCA's UK showroom in Street, Somerset. The partnership targets increased demand for aseptic processing and high-level decontamination in life sciences, pharmaceutical, and controlled environments. UK sterility testing is projected to grow at 14% CAGR through 2035, and UK infection control markets are expected to exceed $6 billion by 2031, with alignment to EU GMP Annex 1 requirements.
