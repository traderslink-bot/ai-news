Summary

MEDIROM Healthcare Technologies (NASDAQ: MRM) announced that a Master Service Agreement with Tools for Humanity and World Foundation became effective February 2, 2026. MEDIROM has installed World "Orb" authentication devices at over 150 locations (mainly its Re.Ra.Ku relaxation studios) with more than 20,000 cumulative authentications. Based on current authentication performance, MEDIROM estimates that expanding Orb deployment to ~3,000 locations in Japan could generate about USD 39 million (approx. JPY 6.10 billion, using USD 1 = JPY 156.36) in income before income taxes over the next two years under the agreement. The release reiterates standard forward-looking disclaimers and lists operational and market risks that could affect outcomes.
