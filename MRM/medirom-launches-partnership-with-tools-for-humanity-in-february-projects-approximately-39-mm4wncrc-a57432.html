Summary

MEDIROM Healthcare Technologies Inc. announced a Master Service Agreement with Tools for Humanity and World Foundation, effective February 2, 2026. If MEDIROM expands to about 3,000 locations nationwide in Japan, it estimates income before income taxes of approximately USD 39 million over the next two years under the agreement. Orbs have been installed at more than 150 locations with cumulative authentications exceeding 20,000. Tools for Humanity is led by Sam Altman and Alex Blania; MEDIROM Group operates around 300 wellness salons under the Re.Ra.Ku brand, and has a portfolio in HealthTech and remote monitoring.
