Summary

Turbo Energy S.A. (NASDAQ: TURB) states its SUNBOX Industry and SUNBOX Industry Max systems integrate large-scale battery storage with AI-driven software to deliver solar-plus-storage solutions for real-time energy management, aiming to reduce exposure to oil and gas price shocks and stabilize operating margins. The company reports $53 million in signed contracts representing 366 MWh of deployed and scheduled capacity across 10 manufacturing facilities. CEO Mariano Soria says the AI-driven renewable electrification platform helps shield margins and strengthen financial resilience in volatile energy markets. Turbo Energy is a subsidiary of Umbrella Global Energy, S.A.
