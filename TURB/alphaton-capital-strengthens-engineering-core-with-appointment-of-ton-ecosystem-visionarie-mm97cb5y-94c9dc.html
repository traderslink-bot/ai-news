Summary

AlphaTON Capital Corp. announced the appointment of Aleksej Paschenko and Andrei Pfau as Senior Engineers to strengthen its engineering core. Paschenko has more than 15 years of engineering experience, including roles at zymFi and Tonkeeper, with expertise in Kubernetes and GPU-enabled infrastructure using NVIDIA B200/B300 chips. Pfau is a prominent TON community figure who authored the Kotlin Multiplatform SDK used by Tonkeeper and created the IntelliJ TON plugin, and will contribute to protocol-level engineering, decentralized identity, and confidential compute. The company emphasizes advancing its Web3 and AI compute agenda for the Telegram ecosystem and notes its Nasdaq listing under the ticker ATON.
