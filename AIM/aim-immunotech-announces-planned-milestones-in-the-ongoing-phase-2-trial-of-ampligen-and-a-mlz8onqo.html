Summary

Press release: AIM ImmunoTech (NYSE: AIM) provided milestone timelines for DURIPANC (ClinicalTrials.gov NCT05927142), an investigator-initiated, exploratory, open-label, single-center Phase 2 study of Ampligen (rintatolimod) combined with AstraZeneca's durvalumab (Imfinzi) in metastatic pancreatic cancer patients with stable disease post-FOLFIRINOX. Eighteen subjects have enrolled to date. Planned milestones: complete enrollment by July 2026; complete Ampligen dosing by August 2026; primary endpoint (clinical benefit rate at 24 weeks) evaluation in December 2026; secondary endpoint evaluations (PFS, OS, immunogenicity, tumor infiltrating immune profile, QoL) by June 2027. AIM highlighted no significant toxicity reported to date, continued promising PFS/OS signals from Phase 1, an updated corporate presentation prioritizing pancreatic cancer and a stated goal of advancing Ampligen toward Phase 3 and potential approval.
