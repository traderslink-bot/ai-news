Summary

AIM ImmunoTech disclosed a timeline for the DURIPANC Phase 2 study of Ampligen (rintatolimod) with AstraZeneca's durvalumab in metastatic pancreatic cancer patients with stable disease after FOLFIRINOX. Milestones include July 2026 enrollment completion, August 2026 full Ampligen dosing completion, December 2026 primary endpoint (Clinical Benefit Rate) evaluation, and June 2027 secondary endpoints (Progression-Free Survival, Overall Survival, immunogenic efficacy, infiltrating immune profile, and quality of life reporting). 18 subjects have been enrolled. The trial is investigator-initiated, open-label, and single-center, conducted with Erasmus MC and AstraZeneca. Reported safety is described as no significant toxicity with high reported quality of life; updates are planned mid-year and year-end.

Positives
  • Enrollment timeline targets set (July 2026) for completion.
  • Dosing completion targeted for August 2026.
  • Primary endpoint evaluation planned for December 2026 (Clinical Benefit Rate).
  • Secondary endpoints planned for June 2027 (PFS, OS, immunogenic efficacy, immune profile, QoL).
  • Reported no significant toxicity and high quality of life in current subjects.
  • Ongoing collaboration with AstraZeneca and Erasmus MC.
  • 18 subjects enrolled to date.
Negatives
  • Study is single-center and investigator-initiated, which may limit generalizability.
  • Small sample size (18 subjects).
  • Results and data are not yet published; milestones are forward-looking.
