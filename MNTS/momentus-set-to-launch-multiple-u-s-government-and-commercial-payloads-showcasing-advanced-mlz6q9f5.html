Summary

Momentus (NASDAQ: MNTS) delivered its Vigoride Orbital Service Vehicle (OSV) to Vandenberg Space Force Base for integration on SpaceX's Transporter‑16 rideshare, targeted no earlier than late March 2026. Vigoride 7 will host 10 payloads demonstrating autonomous rendezvous & proximity operations (RPO), in-space assembly, advanced communications, and next‑generation onboard computing under contracts with DARPA (~$4.2M), SpaceWERX (~$1.9M), NASA (Johnson and Armstrong), AFRL and commercial partners (Portal, Orbit Fab, CisLunar, DPhi, Solstar, Velo3D). The OSV provides >300 kg payload capacity and up to 3 kW peak power; all payloads will be operated from Momentus' San Jose control center. The release includes customary forward-looking statements and notes associated risks and uncertainties.
