Summary

NewGenIVF Group announced a Memorandum of Understanding with Women in Web3 Hong Kong to jointly develop a regional public awareness and education campaign focused on women's health literacy, anchored by the NewGen manifesto and petition. Activities will include talks, panel discussions, and community events, with the campaign leveraging NewGenSup health and longevity products and WiW3HK's network to advance education on women's health, financial inclusion, and the responsible use of emerging technologies.
