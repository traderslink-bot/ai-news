Summary

Upwork Inc. (NASDAQ: UPWK) announced that its board approved a new $300 million share repurchase authorization. Repurchases may be executed on the open market (including 10b5-1 plans), in privately negotiated transactions, via accelerated share repurchase programs, or by other methods at the company’s discretion; the authorization has no expiration but does not obligate any repurchases and may be suspended, terminated, or modified. The announcement follows $136 million deployed in 2025 to repurchase more than 9 million shares and was framed as a capital return amid reported record 2025 results and a plan to accelerate GSV and revenue growth.

Positives
  • Board approved $300 million share repurchase authorization
  • Repurchase authorization has no expiration date
  • Company previously deployed $136 million in 2025 to repurchase >9 million shares
  • Management cites strong balance sheet and record 2025 financial results supporting capital return
Negatives
  • Authorization does not obligate Upwork to repurchase any dollar amount or number of shares
  • Timing and amount of any repurchases are uncertain and dependent on market and business conditions
  • Repurchases may be suspended, terminated, or modified at any time
  • Press release contains forward-looking statements subject to risks; see 2025 Form 10-K risk factors
