Press release: TIAN RUIXIANG Holdings Ltd (NASDAQ: TIRX) confirms it is in advanced, final-stage discussions for a strategic business combination with a well-capitalized, Asia-Pacific InsurTech. No definitive agreement has been reached and the company warned forward-looking statements apply.
