Summary

Tian Ruixiang Holdings Ltd (NASDAQ: TIRX) states it is in advanced and final-stage discussions for a strategic business combination with an Asia-Pacific InsurTech leader. The company also continues to progress its Bitcoin acquisition strategy. Under the contemplated structure, the InsurTech would use Tian Ruixiang as its exclusive, long-term public platform to access U.S. capital markets, while existing TIRX shareholders would retain full public ownership and participate in the upside. No definitive agreement has been reached, there is no privatization, and the company emphasizes its NASDAQ listing and governance. The company expects institutional interest and expanded analyst coverage.
