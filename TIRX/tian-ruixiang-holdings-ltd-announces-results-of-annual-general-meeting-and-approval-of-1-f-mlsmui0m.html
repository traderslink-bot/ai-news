Summary

TIAN RUIXIANG Holdings Ltd. (NASDAQ: TIRX) announced results of its February 17, 2026 annual general meeting: shareholders approved an increase in authorized share capital to US$3,750,000,000 and authorized the board to effect a reverse share split. The board approved a 1-for-50 reverse split of Class A and Class B ordinary shares (every 50 existing shares combined into 1 share). No fractional shares will be issued; fractional amounts will be rounded up. The effective date and new CUSIP will be disclosed later pending completion of the Nasdaq process. A Form 6-K was filed with the SEC reporting these matters.

Positives
  • Shareholders approved increase in authorized share capital to US$3,750,000,000.
  • Board approved implementation of a 1-for-50 reverse share split.
  • Policy for fractional shares specified (rounded up), avoiding issuance of fractional paper.
  • Company states it will disclose effective date and new CUSIP after Nasdaq process is finalized.
Negatives
  • Effective date and new CUSIP not yet disclosed; implementation timing is pending.
  • Reverse split will consolidate the share count (1-for-50), materially reducing number of outstanding shares.
