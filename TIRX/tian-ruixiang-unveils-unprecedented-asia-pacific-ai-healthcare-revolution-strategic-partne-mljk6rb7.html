Summary

Tian Ruixiang Holdings Limited (NASDAQ: TIRX) issued a press release announcing it has entered strategic partnership and acquisition discussions with "Singapore's top three medical technology leaders" to integrate their AI diagnostic platforms into TIRX's insurance-clinic network across Southeast Asia and the broader Asia-Pacific. The company says the proposed transactions — subject to customary closing conditions, regulatory approvals, and shareholder consent — are expected to be finalized within 12 months. TIRX states the integration will enable high-accuracy predictive diagnostics (company claims 95%+), pan-Asia patient data and genomic integration, automation of routine workflows (company claims ~70%), and significant operational cost reductions (company claims >40%). The release contains forward-looking statements and invites investor/media inquiries.
