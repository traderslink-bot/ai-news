Summary

SKYX Platforms Corp. (NASDAQ: SKYX) announced it will launch its patented all‑in‑one ceiling plug & play SKYFAN & Turbo Heater at Walmart. Production is underway, the company plans a broad rollout in Q1 2026 with six color SKUs, and management expects the Walmart channel to drive significant revenue growth in 2026 and advance the company toward cash‑flow positive. SKYX emphasized the combined fan/heater targets a multi‑billion‑dollar annual market, highlighted its intellectual property portfolio (100+ pending/issued patents) and 60 lighting/home décor websites, and signaled plans to expand into other leading retailers. The release contains forward‑looking statements and lists typical commercialization, market acceptance, capital and execution risks.
