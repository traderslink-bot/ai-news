Summary

Nasdaq staff determined XTL Biopharmaceuticals Ltd. is a public shell and that continued listing of its ADSs is not warranted. The company plans to request a hearing before a Nasdaq Hearings Panel to appeal the delisting. A hearing request would stay the suspension of ADSs and delisting pending the panel's decision. The staff noted that The Social Proxy Ltd. has an insolvency filing and liquidation has been ordered, with a trustee appointed. Prior disclosures include failure to meet the minimum stockholders’ equity of $2.5 million (as of Jan 20, 2026) and the ADS bid price rule (as of Dec 20, 2025). If a hearing is not requested by March 4, 2026, ADS trading would be suspended on March 6, 2026 and delisting would follow Nasdaq's Form 25-NSE filing. No guarantee the panel will grant continued listing.
