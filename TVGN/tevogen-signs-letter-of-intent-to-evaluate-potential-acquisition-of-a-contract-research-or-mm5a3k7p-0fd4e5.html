Summary

Tevogen Bio Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: TVGN) announced a signed, non-exclusive, non-binding letter of intent to evaluate a potential acquisition of a global contract research organization. If consummated, the transaction could expand Tevogen's clinical development capabilities and support a revenue-generating healthcare enterprise. The deal is subject to due diligence, negotiation and execution of definitive documentation, required approvals, and closing conditions. Tevogen is also considering other transactions; there is no assurance any transaction will be consummated.
