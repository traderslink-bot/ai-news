Summary

Tevogen Bio Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: TVGN) announced it has entered a signed, non-exclusive, non-binding Letter of Intent to evaluate a potential acquisition of a global Contract Research Organization (CRO). If completed, the transaction could expand Tevogen's clinical development capabilities, introduce revenue-generating service offerings, and allow more internal control of trial execution—potentially improving earnings visibility and capital efficiency. The proposal remains subject to due diligence, negotiation and execution of definitive agreements, required approvals, and customary closing conditions; there is no assurance the transaction will be consummated. The release also reiterates Tevogen's business focus, pipeline, and standard forward-looking risk factors.
