Summary

Tevogen Bio Holdings Inc. (NASDAQ: TVGN) announced it has signed a non-exclusive, non-binding letter of intent to evaluate a potential transaction to acquire Sciometrix Inc., a private healthcare technology company and developer of the Clinicus digital care management platform. If consummated, the deal would give Tevogen a majority voting interest in Sciometrix and is intended to integrate Clinicus' remote patient monitoring and predictive analytics with Tevogen.AI. Management says the acquisition could make Tevogen a revenue-generating healthcare enterprise, accelerate commercialization readiness, support value-based care models, and optimize clinical trials. The transaction is subject to due diligence, negotiation of definitive agreements, required approvals, and customary closing conditions, and may not be completed.
