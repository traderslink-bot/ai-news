Summary

Gaxos.ai Inc. (NASDAQ: GXAI) acquired a 19.99% stake in America First Defense (AFD), a US-based defense technology company. The terms were not disclosed. AFD develops next-generation security platforms for air and ground domains, including a detachable counter-drone system and a biomimetic soft robotics platform for low-detection operations. The investment is positioned to support American-made IP and scalable defense systems. Gaxos.ai previously finalized a BytePlus agreement with ByteDance to secure preferred pricing and early access to video-generation models, aiming to strengthen its Art-Gen platform. GXAI shares fell 5.26% to $1.08 in premarket trading, near a 52-week low of $1.00.
