Summary

Rallybio (NASDAQ: RLYB) and clinical-stage Candid Therapeutics announced a definitive merger agreement under which Rallybio will acquire Candid and the combined company will operate as Candid Therapeutics, Inc., expected to trade on Nasdaq as "CDRX." A concurrent, oversubscribed private financing of over $505 million has been committed by a syndicate of healthcare institutional investors; pro forma cash at close is expected to be approximately $700 million. Pre-transaction Rallybio equityholders are expected to own ~3.65% of the combined company and pre-transaction Candid equityholders (including financing participants) ~96.35% (treasury stock method, assuming Rallybio net cash of $37.5M). The combined company will be led by Dr. Ken Song (CEO). The transaction was unanimously approved by both boards and is expected to close mid-2026, subject to stockholder approvals, effectiveness of an S-4/proxy statement, HSR clearance and other customary conditions. The combined company will advance Candid's T-cell engager (TCE) pipeline, including planned Phase 2 studies of cizutamig in myasthenia gravis and ILD in 2026.
