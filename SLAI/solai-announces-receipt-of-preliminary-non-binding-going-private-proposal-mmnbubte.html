Summary

SOLAI Limited (NYSE: SLAI) announced its Board received a preliminary, non-binding proposal from Chaince Digital Holdings Inc. to acquire all issued and outstanding Class A and Class B ordinary shares (including ADS, each representing 100 Class A ordinary shares) in a cash going-private transaction. The proposed purchase price is US$0.03069 per Ordinary Share, or US$3.069 per ADS, which represents 110% of the Company’s NAV per ADS as of Sept. 30, 2025 (NAV per ADS = US$2.79). The Final Purchase Price would be adjusted to 110% of the most recent quarter-end NAV prior to closing, subject to a price cap of US$3.20 per ADS. The Board has only just received the proposal, no decisions have been made, and there is no assurance a definitive offer or transaction will occur.
