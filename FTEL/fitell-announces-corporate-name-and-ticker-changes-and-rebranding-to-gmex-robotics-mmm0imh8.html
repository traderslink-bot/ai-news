Summary

Press release: Fitell Corporation announced it has legally changed its name to GMEX ROBOTICS CORPORATION effective March 2, 2026 and will change its Nasdaq ticker from FTEL to GMEX, with trading under the new name/ticker expected as early as market open on March 12, 2026. The company's CUSIP remains unchanged and no action is required by shareholders. The firm is repositioning from fitness equipment e-commerce toward designing, manufacturing and commercializing AI-driven consumer and commercial robotics while continuing its fitness and health business as an operational foundation. The company says it is assembling new technical leadership and highlights three strategic pillars: consumer/commercial robotics, AI-driven hardware, and an innovation ecosystem. Forward-looking statement disclosures and caution about risks are included.
