Summary

Deric Eubanks will retire in June after a 23-year tenure with the company and its predecessor. He will step down as CFO of Ashford Inc. and its advised REITs Ashford Hospitality Trust and Braemar Hotels & Resorts on March 31, 2026, and will remain as Senior Managing Director through June 30, 2026 to assist with the transition. Justin Coe will assume the role of Principal Financial Officer. The company and management described the handover as proceeding from a strong foundation.
