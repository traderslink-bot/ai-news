Summary

Ashford Hospitality Trust, Inc. provided updates on strategic hotel transactions, including definitive agreements to sell La Posada de Santa Fe Resort & Spa (157 rooms) for $57.5 million and Hilton St. Petersburg Bayfront (333 rooms) for $96 million, with closings expected in March 2026. The company also closed on two previously disclosed sales (Embassy Suites Houston Near the Galleria and Embassy Suites Austin Arboretum) for $27 million total. Four sales are expected to improve annual cash flow by over $2 million and deliver about $55.5 million in future capital expenditure savings, with proceeds used to retire debt and support deleveraging.
