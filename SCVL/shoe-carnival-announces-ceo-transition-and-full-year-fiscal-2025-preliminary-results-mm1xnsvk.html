Summary

Shoe Carnival (NASDAQ: SCVL) named Cliff Sifford, current Vice Chairman and former CEO, as Interim President and Chief Executive Officer effective February 24, 2026, following the departure and board resignation of Mark Worden; the company will search for a permanent successor. For fiscal year ended January 31, 2026, preliminary results: net sales of $1.135 billion and diluted EPS expected at $1.90 (approximately $0.03 above consensus). The company ended the year with over $130 million in cash, cash equivalents and marketable securities and reported no debt for the 21st consecutive year. Results are preliminary and subject to normal year-end closing and audit adjustments. As of February 25, 2026, Shoe Carnival operated 426 stores in 35 states and Puerto Rico.
