Summary

Press release summarizing Pentagon hypersonics activity and company developments. The Pentagon's Joint Hypersonics Transition Office awarded $68 million across six vendors to accelerate Mach-5+ technologies; the U.S. Army is fielding the Dark Eagle ground-launched hypersonic weapon with deliveries expected to complete in early 2026. Companies highlighted: Starfighters Space (NYSE-A: FJET) — completed wind-tunnel testing for STARLAUNCH 1 and supersonic flight test support work for GE Aerospace; Rocket Lab (NASDAQ: RKLB) — preparing a HASTE hypersonic test mission (Cassowary Vex) and continuing Electron launch cadence; Intuitive Machines (NASDAQ: LUNR) — completed $800M acquisition of Lanteris Space Systems and cites prior Nova-C lunar landings; Kratos Defense & Security Solutions (NASDAQ: KTOS) — received ~ $65M in simulator/training contract awards; Joby Aviation (NYSE: JOBY) — acquiring a >700,000 sq ft Dayton facility to scale production toward four aircraft/month by 2027.
