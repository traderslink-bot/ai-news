Summary

MetaVia Inc. (Nasdaq: MTVA), a clinical-stage biotechnology company focused on transforming cardiometabolic diseases, will participate in a fireside chat at the Life Sciences Virtual Investor Forum on March 12, 2026 at 10:30 am ET. CEO Hyung Heon Kim will discuss the company's obesity and metabolic therapy pipeline, including DA-1726 for obesity and vanoglipel (DA-1241) for MASH. DA-1726 is a novel oxyntomodulin analogue acting as a GLP-1 receptor and glucagon receptor dual agonist; vanoglipel is a GPR119 agonist with preclinical data showing benefits in liver inflammation, lipid metabolism, weight loss and glucose control, and Phase 2a data showing hepatic action and glucose lowering. The event is live online with an archived webcast afterward; attendees can pre-register and schedule one-on-one meetings.
