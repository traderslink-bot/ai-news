Summary

CalciMedica reported 2025 results with cash and expense details. AKI KOURAGE Phase 2 safety review found no evidence of drug-related toxicity, but the trial was discontinued for Stage 2/3 AKI with acute hypoxemic respiratory failure due to a mortality imbalance; the company will discuss potential future AKI development with the FDA in Q2 2026. For acute pancreatitis, FDA engagement on the pivotal Auxora program was positive and design is planned to be finalized in the first half of 2026; Phase 2b CARPO results were published, showing dose-dependent improvements, including a 100% reduction in new-onset severe respiratory failure in hyper-inflamed patients at medium/high doses. CM5480 for PAH has preclinical support and an IND is anticipated for 2027. Financially, cash and short-term investments were $13.0 million at 12/31/2025; R&D expenses were $15.2 million and G&A expenses were $7.9 million for 2025; net loss was $29.6 million.
