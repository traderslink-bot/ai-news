Summary

HeartCore Enterprises, Inc. says its board authorized a share repurchase program to buy up to $2.0 million of common stock. The program follows a corporate restructuring and an improved profitability outlook. Repurchases may be executed via open market transactions, privately negotiated deals, or under a 10b5-1 plan and may be modified, suspended, or discontinued at any time. As of February 24, 2026, preliminary unaudited estimates indicate total net assets exceed market capitalization. The company frames the move as part of a disciplined capital allocation approach alongside growth investments.
