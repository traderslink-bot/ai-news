Summary

Press release: American Rebel Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: AREB) announced an exclusive distribution agreement with Wil Fischer Distributing to expand American Rebel Light Beer's presence across south-central, southeast and northwest Missouri. The Wil Fischer territory reaches roughly 900,000 residents and includes markets such as Springfield, Branson and the Ozarks corridor; the partnership includes planned brand activation at the 9,000-seat Black Oak Amphitheater in spring 2026. American Rebel is also promoting a limited-edition 250th Anniversary "Patriot Pack" (shipping mid-May 2026) and is accepting distributor pre-orders. The company highlights its Distributor-First expansion strategy and notes its product positioning (light all-malt lager with low calories/carbs and ~4.2% ABV). The release discloses corporate actions and risks, including a 1-for-20 reverse stock split completed Feb 2, 2026 and receipt of a Nasdaq delisting notice with an anticipated appeal.

Positives
  • Exclusive distribution agreement with Wil Fischer Distributing covering ~900,000 residents in key Missouri markets (Springfield, Branson, Ozarks, NW Missouri).
  • Planned brand activation at Black Oak Amphitheater (9,000-seat venue) to drive consumer trial and visibility in 2026.
  • Limited-edition 250th Anniversary "Patriot Pack" planned to ship mid-May 2026; pre-orders open for distributors/retailers.
  • Partnership with a long-established distributor (Wil Fischer: 50+ years; ~5 million cases by 2021) with large retail and on-premise reach.
  • Company reports prior rollout to 18 states and emphasizes a Distributor-First growth strategy.
Negatives
  • Company disclosed completion of a 1-for-20 reverse stock split on Feb 2, 2026.
  • Company has received a Nasdaq delisting notice and intends to appeal; delisting risk may affect liquidity and listing status.
  • Forward-looking statement warnings of risks including potential production delays, supply chain disruptions, and inability to fulfill pre-orders or achieve expected retail placements.
