Summary

Press release from American Rebel Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: AREB) describing progress of its American Rebel Light Beer launch and national rollout. The company says it has executed distribution agreements with over 30 top-tier partners across 18 states, expanded e-commerce to 40 states and 1,300+ retailers, and secured retail placements including initial scheduled placement in 62 Total Wine & More locations and Kroger-related 16oz can activity. At NBWA 2025 the company reported meetings with 110+ distributors, 83 qualified follow-ups, 65 high-interest opportunities and 16 verbal on-site commitments; two distributors began formal contract negotiations within 24 hours. The release highlights a limited-edition 250th Anniversary Patriot Pack, product attributes (approx. 110 calories per 12 oz, 4g carbs, 4.2% ABV, all‑malt recipe), and notes a completed 1-for-20 reverse stock split on Feb 2, 2026 and a Nasdaq delisting notice with an upcoming appeal and OTC contingency. The filing includes standard forward-looking statements and risk disclosures.
