Summary

Arteris (NASDAQ: AIP) reported Q4 2025 revenue of $20.1M (up 30% YoY) and full-year 2025 revenue of $70.6M (up 22% YoY). ACV plus royalties reached a record $83.6M (up 28% YoY) and remaining performance obligations (RPO) were $116.8M (up 32% YoY) with ~half expected to be recognized in 2026. Q4 GAAP operating loss was $8.5M (non-GAAP operating loss $2.2M); Q4 net loss was $8.5M ($0.19/sh) and non-GAAP net loss $2.3M ($0.05/sh). FY 2025 GAAP net loss was $34.7M ($0.82/sh); non-GAAP net loss was $14.1M. Variable royalties grew 50% YoY to $6.6M. Non-GAAP free cash flow was positive $3.0M in Q4 and $5.3M for FY 2025. Arteris closed the acquisition of Cycuity in Jan 2026. Guidance: Q1 2026 ACV+royalties $85.0–$89.0M, revenue $20.5–$21.5M; FY 2026 ACV+royalties $100.0–$104.0M, revenue $89.0–$93.0M; FY non-GAAP operating loss $5.0–$9.0M; FY free cash flow $5.0–$9.0M.

Positives
  • Q4 2025 revenue $20.1M, up 30% year-over-year
  • Full-year 2025 revenue $70.6M, up 22% year-over-year
  • Record ACV plus royalties $83.6M, up 28% year-over-year
  • RPO $116.8M, up 32% year-over-year (approx. half expected to convert in 2026)
  • Variable royalties up 50% year-over-year ($6.6M)
  • Non-GAAP free cash flow positive: $3.0M in Q4 and $5.3M for FY 2025
  • Completed acquisition of Cycuity to expand semiconductor security offerings
Negatives
  • GAAP operating loss of $8.5M in Q4 2025 and $33.1M for FY 2025
  • GAAP net loss of $8.5M in Q4 2025 and $34.7M for FY 2025 ($0.82 per share)
  • Stockholders' deficit of $14.6M as of December 31, 2025
