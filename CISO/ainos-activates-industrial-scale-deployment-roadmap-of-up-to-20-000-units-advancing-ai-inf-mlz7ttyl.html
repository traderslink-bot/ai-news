Summary

Ainos, Inc. announced a phased deployment to install up to 20,000 AI Nose units in ASE Technology Holding Co. facilities under a multi-phase framework. Phase 1 includes a confirmed order of 1,400 AI Nose units on a three-year subscription worth approximately $2.1 million, with $350,000 in customer deposits and a target completion by end-Q2 2026. Phase 2 contemplates deployment of 5,000 units; Phase 3 contemplates up to 15,000 units. Deployments also extend to advanced front-end semiconductor wafer fabs for validation, subject to customer review and formal commercial approvals before potential revenue conversion. The rollout centers on the Smell ID platform and a SmellTech-as-a-Service model, with forward-looking statements and risks disclosed.

Positives
  • Confirmed order for 1,400 AI Nose units under a three-year subscription valued at ~ $2.1 million
  • $350,000 in customer deposits received
  • Strategic framework with ASE Technology Holding Co., Ltd. under a Memorandum of Understanding
  • Phased plan to scale to up to 20,000 units across multiple deployment phases
  • Expansion into advanced front-end fabs for validation and potential revenue if approvals are obtained
  • Use of a subscription-based deployment model (SmellTech-as-a-Service)
Negatives
  • Phase progression contingent on milestone validation and contract progression
  • Deployment in front-end fabs subject to customer review and formal commercial approval before revenue is recognized
  • Up to 20,000 units depend on multiple successive phases and approvals; not guaranteed
  • Forward-looking statements risk; outcomes depend on numerous factors
  • No guaranteed timeline beyond Phase 1 completion target (end-Q2 2026)
