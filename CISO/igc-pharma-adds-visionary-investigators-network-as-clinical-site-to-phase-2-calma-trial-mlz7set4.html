Summary

IGC Pharma, Inc. (NYSE: IGC) announces Visionary Investigators Network (VIN) as a clinical research site for its Phase 2 CALMA trial evaluating IGC-AD1 for agitation in Alzheimer's disease dementia. VIN is a practice-integrated neurology network that supports enrollment and protocol adherence. Approximately 70% of planned enrollment is complete; CALMA is a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled Phase 2 study to be conducted at VIN’s Coconut Grove site in Miami. The release includes forward-looking statements and SEC filing references.

Positives
  • VIN added as a clinical site for the CALMA Phase 2 trial.
  • VIN’s practice-integrated model may support enrollment efficiency and patient engagement.
  • Approximately 70% of planned CALMA enrollment is completed.
  • CALMA trial design remains randomized, double-blind, and placebo-controlled.
  • Specific site location (Coconut Grove, Miami) and lead investigator named.
Negatives
  • No efficacy or safety results are reported in the release.
