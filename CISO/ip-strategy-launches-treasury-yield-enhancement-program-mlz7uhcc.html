Summary

IP Strategy Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: IPST) announces a treasury yield-enhancement program using a structured covered-call strategy on 3 million IP tokens, targeting an average monthly yield of 3.96% (59.37% CAGR). In the first four weeks, the program generated $167,324 gross income without selling IP tokens. The company plans to expand the strategy gradually to fund operating costs from recurring treasury income while preserving the majority of treasury assets to support its validator infrastructure business and participation in the Story Protocol. A board-approved share repurchase program of up to 1 million common shares in 2026 is also disclosed. Forward-looking statements come with typical risk disclosures.
