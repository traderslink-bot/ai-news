Summary

rYojbaba Co., Ltd. (NASDAQ: RYOJ) announced participation in the Guardian Girls Aikido project in Los Angeles, marking the program's first implementation in the United States. The initiative is conducted with Guardian Girls USA and UNFPA and follows earlier launches in Bogotá (2024) and expansion to Colombia, Japan, Peru, and Turkey. The Los Angeles session focused on situational awareness, self-confidence, and practical, non-violent self-defense based on Aikido, led by Lia Suzuki. The company frames the effort as part of its ESG and social-impact objectives and indicates potential future collaborations in the U.S. The release includes a standard forward-looking statements disclaimer.
