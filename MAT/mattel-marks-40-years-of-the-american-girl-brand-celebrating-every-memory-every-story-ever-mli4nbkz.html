Summary

Mattel (NASDAQ: MAT) announced the 40th anniversary of its American Girl brand and launched the American Girl Modern Era Collection, reimagining historical characters as 14.5-inch contemporary dolls (pre-sale for AG Rewards Berry members immediately, wider pre-sale Feb. 13, 2026). Mattel also announced two publishing projects: The Making of American Girl (on sale May 12, 2026) and Samantha: The Next Chapter, an adult novel by Fiona Davis (on sale Oct. 13, 2026). The press release highlights the brand's legacy (over 36 million dolls sold, 50+ characters) and says Mattel will roll out additional products, retail experiences and partnerships across the anniversary year.

Positives
  • Launch of Modern Era Collection introduces a new product line that can drive doll sales and renew engagement with legacy characters
  • Publishing projects (archive book and adult novel) create additional revenue and marketing channels beyond toys
  • Brand strength and scale: over 36 million dolls sold and 50+ characters, supporting multigenerational demand
  • Anniversary-focused marketing and new retail/partnership initiatives can boost traffic and cross-sell opportunities
Negatives
  • Press release provides no financial guidance, sales forecasts, or expected revenue impact from the launches
