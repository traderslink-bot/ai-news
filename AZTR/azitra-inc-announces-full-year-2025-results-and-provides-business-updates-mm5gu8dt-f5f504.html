Summary

Azitra reported full-year 2025 results for the year ended December 31, 2025. Net loss was $10.96 million and cash and cash equivalents at December 31, 2025 were $2.07 million. Revenue was nil; R&D expenses were $4.84 million and G&A expenses were $6.13 million. Total operating expenses were $10.97 million. The company completed financings totaling $8.5 million. Pipeline updates include: Phase 1/2 ATR-04 trial dosing of the first patient in 2025 with topline data expected mid-2026 for EGFRi-associated rash; ATR-12 Phase 1b safety data with topline data anticipated in the second half of 2026 for Netherton syndrome; positive preclinical data for ATR-01 targeting ichthyosis vulgaris. FDA Fast Track designation for ATR-04. 2026 milestones include topline data for ATR-12 and ATR-04 and IND-enabling studies for ATR-01.

Positives
  • Dosed first patient in Phase 1/2 ATR-04 trial in Q3 2025.
  • Topline data for the ATR-04 Phase 1/2 first cohort expected around mid-2026.
  • ATR-12 Phase 1b safety data with topline data anticipated in H2 2026.
  • Positive preclinical data for ATR-01 targeting ichthyosis vulgaris announced in 2025.
  • FDA Fast Track designation for ATR-04.
  • Completed financings totaling $8.5 million.
Negatives
  • Net loss of $10.96 million for the year ended December 31, 2025.
  • Cash and cash equivalents of $2.07 million at year-end 2025.
  • Revenue was nil; no product revenue reported for 2025.
  • R&D and G&A expenses combined to $10.97 million for 2025.
  • Net loss per share of $2.25 and weighted average shares 4.87 million.
