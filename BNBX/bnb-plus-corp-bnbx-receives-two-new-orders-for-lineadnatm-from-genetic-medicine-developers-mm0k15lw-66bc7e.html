Summary

BNB Plus Corp. says its LineaRx unit received two new LineaDNA orders from genetic medicine developers, with production and shipment expected to be completed before June 2026. The company notes a prior $1.2 million accelerated LineaDNA order and projects over $1.5 million in LineaDNA shipments in the first half of 2026, including about 8 grams of DNA in the current quarter. Cost reductions cited include roughly $3 million in annual savings in the legacy LineaRx business and over $600,000 in additional annualized savings from a relocated corporate headquarters. The company also reports its digital asset portfolio, with BNB tokens valued at about $10.7 million as of February 23, 2026, representing more than double its market cap.
