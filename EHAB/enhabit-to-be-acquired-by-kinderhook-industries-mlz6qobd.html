Summary

Enhabit, Inc. (NYSE: EHAB) entered into a definitive agreement to be acquired by private equity firm Kinderhook Industries in an all-cash transaction valuing the company at approximately $1.1 billion. Enhabit stockholders will receive $13.80 per share in cash (≈24.4% premium to the Feb 20, 2026 close; ≈33.8% premium to the 60-day VWAP). The transaction was unanimously approved by Enhabit's board, is expected to close in Q2 2026 subject to stockholder and regulatory approvals and customary closing conditions, and will result in Enhabit ceasing to be listed on the NYSE and becoming a private company. Kinderhook has secured committed debt and equity financing. Enhabit will not hold its planned earnings call or issue 2026 guidance; its Q4 and FY2025 release is expected March 4, 2026.
