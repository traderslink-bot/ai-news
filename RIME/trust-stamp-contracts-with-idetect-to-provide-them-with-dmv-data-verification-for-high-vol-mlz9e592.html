Summary

Trust Stamp (IDAI) announces a partnership with IDetect to provide access to the AAMVA Driver's License Data Verification (DLDV) service for real-time verification of driver’s license data. The integration aims to elevate IDetect’s ID scanning by adding data verification against DMV records, with potential to process millions of verifications per month at full scale. The integration will be available across IDetect’s hardware and software solutions. The announcement is dated February 23, 2026, and notes customers including Marriott, Harrah’s Casinos, and the FBI. No financial terms were disclosed.

Positives
  • Access to AAMVA DLDV data verification through Trust Stamp
  • Potential to process millions of verifications per month at full scale
  • Integration across IDetect’s hardware and software solutions
  • Enhances security beyond physical ID verification to data verification
  • Support for high-profile customers and government-related use cases
Negatives
  • No financial terms disclosed
  • Forward-looking projections carry execution risk
  • Reliance on DMV data and AAMVA availability and accuracy
  • Potential privacy and regulatory considerations with data verification
