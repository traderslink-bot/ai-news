Summary

Rocket Companies (NYSE: RKT) and Compass International Holdings (NYSE: COMP) announced a three-year strategic alliance to list Compass' 'Coming Soon' and later 'Private Exclusive' inventory on Redfin, potentially adding more than 500,000 listings to Redfin's platform. The partnership integrates search, agents and Rocket Mortgage financing to streamline the homebuying/selling process, aims to drive more buyer traffic (Redfin expects nearly 2 billion visits in 2026), gives Compass agents access to over 1 million Redfin-generated buyer inquiries, and offers preferred Rocket Mortgage pricing (one-percentage-point rate reduction for the first year or up to $6,000 lender credit). No financial terms beyond mortgage pricing incentives were disclosed.
