Summary

Anixa Biosciences (NASDAQ: ANIX) announced that the Korean Ministry of Intellectual Property issued a Notice of Allowance for a patent titled "Vaccine Adjuvants and Formulations," providing composition-of-matter protection in South Korea for its Cleveland Clinic‑licensed breast cancer vaccine technology targeting α-lactalbumin. The allowance expands the company’s international IP estate alongside U.S. and other patents, supporting potential future regulatory and commercialization efforts. The release reiterates Phase 1 results at Cleveland Clinic showing the vaccine was safe, well tolerated, and elicited immune responses in 74% of participants, and notes Cleveland Clinic is entitled to royalties on the licensed technology.
