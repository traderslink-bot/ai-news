Summary

Veea Inc. (NASDAQ: VEEA) announced TerraFabric, a control plane for automating and coordinating distributed edge environments across multi-vendor, heterogeneous networks. TerraFabric provides fleet orchestration, policy enforcement, and software lifecycle management (integrating prpl LCM) and is available for pilot deployments now as a standalone platform, integrated within VeeaONE, or in hybrid mode. Capabilities highlighted include policy-driven workload placement, controlled software rollouts with health checks and one-click rollback, governed autonomy for AI agents, and demoing at Mobile World Congress 2026. The release notes prpl LCM’s prior scale (managing containerized applications on >12 million AT&T gateways) and includes forward-looking statement cautions; no financial impacts or pricing were disclosed.
