Summary

Veea Inc. announced the planned commercial rollout of Veea SecureConnect by Telcel in Mexico, using 5G-based fixed wireless access with AI-driven cybersecurity and enterprise features. The solution targets multi-site businesses and integrates cloud-managed security with edge computing, SD-WAN, and IoT capabilities. Deployment is demonstrated at Mobile World Congress, with on-site setup claimed to be plug-and-play in under 30 minutes. TerraFabric provides orchestration across distributed edge environments.

Positives

  • 5G-based fixed wireless access with AI-driven cybersecurity and enterprise-grade features
  • Cloud-managed and edge-executed platform delivering NGFW-as-a-Service (NGFWaaS) and SD-WAN with Wi‑Fi 6 mesh
  • Support for PCI DSS compliance through device discovery and secure profiles
  • TerraFabric enables fleet orchestration across multi-vendor edge environments
