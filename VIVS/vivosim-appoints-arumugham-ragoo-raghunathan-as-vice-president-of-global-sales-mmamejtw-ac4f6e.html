VivoSim Labs, Inc. announced the appointment of Dr. Arumugham (Ragoo) Raghunathan, PhD, as Vice President of Global Sales. He will be based in the Greater Boston area and lead commercial expansion, secure strategic partnerships with biopharma companies, CROs/CDMOs, and research institutions, with a focus on VivoSim's liver and GI toxicity solutions for preclinical development.
