Summary

GameSquare Holdings (NASDAQ: GAME) repurchased over 1.5 million shares of common stock in February for $499,137, an average price of approximately $0.33 per share. The company has approximately $2.5 million remaining under its current repurchase authorization. Since October 2025, GameSquare has repurchased a total of 5.05 million shares for $2.5 million (average ~$0.49 per share). The company says it will continue to opportunistically repurchase shares and highlights that in Q3 2025 its operating segments represented ~98% of revenue and ~95% of gross profit.
