Summary

GameSquare Holdings, Inc. repurchased over 1.5 million shares of its common stock during February for $499,137, at an average price of approximately $0.33 per share. The company has about $2.5 million remaining under its current share repurchase authorization. Since October 2025, it has repurchased a total of 5.05 million shares for $2.5 million, at an average price of approximately $0.49 per share. CEO Justin Kenna described the strategy as grounded in the core operating business and a yield-focused treasury approach, allowing opportunistic repurchases despite volatility in crypto and digital asset markets.
