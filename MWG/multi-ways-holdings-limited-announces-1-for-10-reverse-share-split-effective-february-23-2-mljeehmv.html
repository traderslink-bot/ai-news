Summary

Multi Ways Holdings Limited (NYSE: MWG) announced a 1-for-10 reverse split of its Class A and Class B ordinary shares, approved by the board on Jan 30, 2026 and previously approved by shareholders on Nov 26, 2025. The reverse split will be effective for trading on February 23, 2026 on NYSE American under the symbol MWG with a new CUSIP (G6362F116). Each ten issued and outstanding Class A or Class B share will be combined into one share. Par value will increase from US$0.00025 to US$0.0025 per share and the authorized share capital will be adjusted to reflect 800,000,000 Class A, 100,000,000 Class B and 1,000,000,000 preferred shares at the noted par values. After the split the company expects approximately 4,142,000 Class A and 1,000,000 Class B shares issued and outstanding. No fractional shares will be issued; fractional entitlements will be rounded up. VStock Transfer, LLC is the exchange/transfer agent. The company states the reverse split is intended to increase the market price per share to maintain its NYSE American listing.

Positives
  • Reverse split effective February 23, 2026 with post-split trading on NYSE American under MWG (new CUSIP G6362F116).
  • Company expects post-split issued and outstanding shares of approximately 4,142,000 Class A and 1,000,000 Class B.
  • Transfer/ exchange agent named (VStock Transfer, LLC) and fractional shares will be rounded up, avoiding issuance of fractional certificates.
Negatives
  • Company is undertaking a reverse share split specifically to increase the market price per share to maintain its NYSE American listing.
  • Par value per share will increase from US$0.00025 to US$0.0025, and authorized share capital figures are being adjusted.
  • Reverse split reduces the number of outstanding shares (combines every 10 shares into 1), which is typically used when share price is low.
