Summary

GigaMedia Limited announced an amendment to the Aeolus convertible promissory note, extending the due date for the US$7 million principal to May 31, 2026. The note bears 4% annual interest and may be convertible at maturity, prepayment, or certain events at the lower of US$1.25 per share or 80% of the offering price. The amendment is intended to support Aeolus's recent financing activities, and GigaMedia may engage in additional transactions involving Aeolus's securities as permitted by law.

Positives
  • Extension provides financing support for Aeolus
  • Outstanding principal has a defined due date extended to May 31, 2026
  • Conversion terms include potential equity participation at a favorable price (lower of $1.25 per share or 80% of offering price)
Negatives
  • Convertible feature could dilute existing shareholders if the note converts
  • Agreement increases exposure to Aeolus and depends on its ongoing financing activities
