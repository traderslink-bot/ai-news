Summary

Press release (Feb 18, 2026): Brenmiller Energy (NASDAQ:BNRG) announced that Rock Energy Storage (its exclusive distributor in the Northeastern U.S.) and Idaho National Laboratory will present at IDEA Campus 2026 on integrating small modular reactors (SMRs) with Brenmiller's thermal energy storage (TES). The presentation will cover how pairing SMRs with TES (Brenmiller's bGen™ system based on bCube crushed rock architecture) can store excess thermal output, enable flexible thermal dispatch, improve reactor utilization, reduce fossil peaking reliance, and enhance campus/district energy decarbonization and resilience.

  • Rock Energy Storage designated as Brenmiller's exclusive distributor in the Northeastern U.S.
