Summary

IQSTEL Inc. reported preliminary revenue of approximately $317 million for fiscal year 2025. The company says it currently operates at about a $400 million annual revenue run rate (80% Telecom, 20% Fintech). It reports an Adjusted EBITDA run rate of about $2.7 million and projects EBITDA run rate to reach $9–$15 million as revenue scales toward $500–$600 million in 2026. IQSTEL plans two targeted acquisitions in 2026 and is expanding into AI-enabled telecom solutions and cybersecurity services through IQSTEL AI & Digital, with seven-digit annual revenue expected by 2027. The company aims to become a $1 billion tech-driven enterprise by 2027. Additional details include no convertible debt and no warrants outstanding, roughly 4.9 million shares outstanding, $12.23 in assets per share, and $4.66 in stockholders’ equity per share. IQSTEL operates in 21 countries with more than 600 telecom operators and an indirect reach of about 2.7 billion end customers. An 8-K filed March 6, 2026 accompanies the release.
