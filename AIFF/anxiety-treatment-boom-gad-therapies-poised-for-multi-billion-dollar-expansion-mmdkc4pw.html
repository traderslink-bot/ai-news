Summary

Press release (Market News Updates / PR Newswire) highlighting projected growth in the generalized anxiety disorder (GAD) treatment market (estimated ~$1.8B in 2023 to ~$4.26B by 2033) and company updates. Key company-specific disclosure: Helus Pharma reported topline Phase 2 signal-detection results for HLP004 (36 patients randomized 2:1 to active/placebo) showing a statistically significant mean HAM-A reduction of ~10.4 points (p<0.0001) at six weeks versus standard of care, durable effects through six months (pooled: 67% responders, 39% remitters), favorable tolerability with no drug-related serious adverse events, short acute effects (~90 minutes) and typical discharge readiness within ~3 hours. Additional corporate items: Acadia Healthcare reported Q4 2025 revenue of $821.5M and a net loss of $(13.02)/share driven by a $996.2M goodwill impairment; Johnson & Johnson, BrainsWay and Firefly Neuroscience updates are summarized. Disclosure notes the release was distributed on behalf of Helus Pharma and MarketNewsUpdates was paid $4,600 for coverage.
