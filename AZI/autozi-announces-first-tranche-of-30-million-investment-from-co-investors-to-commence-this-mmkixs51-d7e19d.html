Summary

Autozi Internet Technology (Global) Ltd. announced that the first tranche of assets valued at approximately $30 million from co-investors will commence within this week, as part of a commitment to invest approximately $110 million at $1.30 per share to supplement liquidity, accelerate strategic expansion, and optimize the company's capital structure. The commitment follows a prior $7 million investment by the controlling shareholder. The asset injection is intended to enhance liquidity, expand automotive aftermarket service networks, strengthen digital platform capabilities, optimize supply chains, and improve financial flexibility for future actions.
