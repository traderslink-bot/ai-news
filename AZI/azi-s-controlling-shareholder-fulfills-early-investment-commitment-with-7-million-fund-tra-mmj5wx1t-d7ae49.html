Summary

Autozi Internet Technology (Global) Ltd. (Nasdaq: AZI) announced that its controlling shareholder has completed an early investment commitment by transferring $7 million, with funds fully received. The controlling shareholder and co-investors proposed an additional approximately $110 million equity investment at $1.30 per share to bolster liquidity and accelerate strategic expansion, subject to internal procedures and regulatory approvals. Uses include expanding automotive aftermarket service networks, upgrading digital platform capabilities, optimizing supply chain systems, and improving financial flexibility. The announcement notes adherence to SEC and Nasdaq requirements and frames the investment as a signal of long-term confidence in the company's value.
