Summary

Filing Type: Form 8-K (Item 7.01, Regulation FD Disclosure). Reporting entity: Signing Day Sports, Inc. (Registrant). On February 12, 2026, Signing Day Sports furnished a press release (Exhibit 99.1) announcing that Eyal Rozen was named Chief Operating Officer of One Blockchain LLC. The announcement relates to a proposed business combination among Signing Day Sports, BlockchAIn Digital Infrastructure, Inc., One Blockchain, BCDI Merger Sub I Inc., and BCDI Merger Sub II LLC under the Business Combination Agreement (as amended). BlockchAIn has filed a Registration Statement on Form S-4 (declared effective) that contains a preliminary proxy statement/prospectus; definitive materials will be distributed to Signing Day Sports stockholders when available. The filing states the furnished press release and related information are "furnished" not "filed," includes forward-looking statements and risk disclosures, and confirms the managers and officers of One Blockchain do not currently hold interests in Signing Day Sports. No ownership numbers or percentages are disclosed in this Form 8-K.
