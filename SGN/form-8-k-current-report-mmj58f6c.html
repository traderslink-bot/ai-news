Summary

Signing Day Sports, Inc. filed a Form 8-K on March 9, 2026 furnishing a press release (Exhibit 99.1) under Regulation FD that discloses details regarding One Blockchain and BlockchAIn's infrastructure development strategy. The filing references the Business Combination Agreement (dated May 27, 2025, as amended) among Signing Day Sports, BlockchAIn Digital Infrastructure, Inc., One Blockchain LLC, BCDI Merger Sub I Inc. and BCDI Merger Sub II LLC. No ownership numbers or percentages are disclosed in this Form 8-K. The communication is furnished (not filed) and the parties are actively seeking shareholder approval for the proposed business combination.
