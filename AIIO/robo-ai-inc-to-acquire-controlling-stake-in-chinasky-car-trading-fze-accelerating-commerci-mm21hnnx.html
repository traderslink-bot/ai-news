Summary

Robo.ai Inc. (NASDAQ: AIIO) announced a definitive share purchase agreement to acquire a 51% equity interest in Chinasky Car Trading FZE (CCT), an automotive trading company based in Jebel Ali Free Zone, Dubai. CCT reported approximately AED 100 million (≈USD 30 million) in revenue for fiscal 2025 and distributes across more than 20 markets in Central Asia, North Africa and Eastern Europe. Robo.ai intends to use CCT as a global distribution hub to accelerate market entry and commercialization of its intelligent hardware pipeline (Robotaxis, eVTOL, intelligent logistics robots), and its RoBUS commercial vehicle joint venture will leverage CCT's channels. The release discloses strategic objectives and contains forward-looking statements; no purchase price or detailed transaction terms were provided.
