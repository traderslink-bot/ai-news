Summary

Robo.ai Inc. (NASDAQ: AIIO) announced via press release that a subsidiary has secured its first commercial order to deliver 30,000 hours of Embodied AI robot training data to U.S.-based DaBoss.AI Inc. over a 12-month period. The deliverables include multi-modal raw data (RGB-D vision, 6-DoF motion trajectories, and force/tactile feedback) intended for training Level 4+ Embodied AI models. Revenue from the transaction will be consolidated into Robo.ai's financial statements through the joint venture under U.S. GAAP and is expected to contribute cash flow in fiscal 2026. The company positions the order as validation of its Embodied AI Data Port strategy and its recently formed joint venture with DaBoss.AI. The release does not disclose contract price or total revenue.
