Summary

E-Power (NASDAQ: EPOW) announced an MOU with Kehui International Ltd to form a U.S. joint venture, E-Power Grid Inc. USA, focused on microgrids for AI data centers, power equipment, and power automation. Under the MOU E-Power will be majority owner with at least a 55% stake and will provide $1.5 million in cash to fund initial registration and establishment. Kehui will contribute its proprietary "Synchronous Constant Frequency Microgrid" patents and technical support; the patent contribution value and investment ratio will be determined by a mutually recognized third-party appraisal. The JV has exclusive rights to use the contributed patent technology in the United States and Canada, includes a priority procurement clause for Kehui products, and contains performance-based milestones, including a $3 million cumulative sales target within the first three years to retain patent rights. Both parties will form a joint task force within 30 days to finalize registration, appraisals, and capital transfers.

Positives
  • Formation of U.S. JV (E-Power Grid Inc. USA) to pursue microgrid and AIDC opportunities
  • E-Power to hold majority stake (at least 55%)
  • E-Power committed $1.5 million cash for initial JV setup
  • Kehui contributing proprietary "Synchronous Constant Frequency Microgrid" patents and ongoing technical support
  • Exclusive rights for the contributed patent technology in the U.S. and Canada
  • Priority procurement clause to source Kehui products when competitively priced
  • Performance milestones established (including $3M cumulative sales in first three years)
Negatives
  • Agreement is a Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) — an initial/early-stage agreement, not necessarily a definitive binding contract
  • Patent contribution valuation and final investment ratio remain undetermined and subject to third-party appraisal
  • Patent retention is contingent on achieving $3 million in cumulative sales within three years
  • Initial cash commitment is limited to $1.5 million for registration and establishment
