Summary

IN8bio, Inc. (NASDAQ: INAB) announced that CEO William Ho will present at the TD Cowen 46th Annual Health Care Conference on March 2, 2026 at 9:50 a.m. ET in Boston. The conference runs March 2–4, 2026, with management hosting one-on-one investor meetings. A live webcast and replay will be available via the IN8bio website under Events and Presentations. IN8bio is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company developing γδ T cell therapies, with lead programs INB-100 and INB-200/400 and a preclinical γδ T cell engager platform INB-600.
