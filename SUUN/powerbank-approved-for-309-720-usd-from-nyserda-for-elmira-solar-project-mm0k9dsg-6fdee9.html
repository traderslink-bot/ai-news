Summary

PowerBank Corporation (NASDAQ: SUUN) announced that its 2.6 MW Elmira Solar Power Project in New York has been approved to receive up to $309,720 in NYSERDA NY-Sun incentives. The company expects to enroll the project in the Inclusive Community Solar Adder stream, which could provide up to an additional $545,107. The Elmira Project is planned as a community solar project expected to power about 325 homes annually.

Positives

  • Opportunity for up to an additional $545,107 through the Inclusive Community Solar Adder (ICSA) stream
  • 2.6 MW project size; expected to power ~325 homes annually
  • Project to be operated as community solar, expanding solar access
  • PowerBank has a pipeline exceeding 1 GW and over 100 MW of completed projects
