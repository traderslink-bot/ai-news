Summary

Bluerock Homes Trust, Inc. (NYSE American: BHM) announced its Board authorized a new share repurchase plan to buy up to $10.0 million of its Class A common stock. The program will begin March 1, 2026, run for one year through February 28, 2027, and will be conducted via open-market transactions in accordance with Rule 10b-18 (and subject to Rule 10b-5). Repurchases are subject to market conditions, price and volume limits, may be discontinued at any time, and the timing and extent of any purchases are at the Company's discretion.

Positives
  • Board authorized repurchase program targeting up to $10.0 million of Class A common stock
  • Repurchases will be conducted under Rule 10b-18 (provides an established framework for open-market buys)
  • Defined one-year program start and end dates (March 1, 2026 to February 28, 2027)
Negatives
  • Repurchases are subject to market conditions, price and volume limitations and may be discontinued at any time
  • Company did not disclose number of shares to be repurchased or expected impact on share count
  • Forward-looking statements and risk-factor references indicate outcomes are not guaranteed
