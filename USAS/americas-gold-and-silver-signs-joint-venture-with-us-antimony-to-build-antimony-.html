Americas Gold and Silver signs joint venture with US Antimony to build antimony processing facility in Idaho's Silver Valley

US Antimony (USAS) announced a joint venture with Americas Gold and Silver to construct an antimony processing facility in Idaho's Silver Valley, disclosed via an SEC Form 6-K. The deal aims to unlock value from USAS's antimony production and strengthen U.S. critical mineral supply chains; material details such as capex, timeline and financial terms were not disclosed in the filing.

Positive
  • Creates domestic processing capacity for antimony, supporting U.S. critical minerals strategy.
  • Potential to monetize existing antimony resources and improve margins through downstream processing.
  • Partnership with Americas Gold and Silver brings JV partner expertise and potential operational scale.
Negative
  • No financial terms, timeline, permitting status or funding plan disclosed in the 6‑K.
