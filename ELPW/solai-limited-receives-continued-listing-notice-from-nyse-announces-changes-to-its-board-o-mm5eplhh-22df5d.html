Summary

SOLAI Limited disclosed that NYSE notified the company it is not in compliance with continued listing standards due to ADS price below $1.00 for a 30-trading-day period. The company has a six-month cure period to regain compliance. The notice does not immediately affect listing. Separately, Qian Sun resigned as an independent director and Zhan Chen was appointed as an independent director, joining the audit, nominating and corporate governance, and strategy committees.

Positives
  • NYSE continues listing during the six-month cure period to regain compliance
  • The company intends to regain compliance with the minimum price requirement
  • Zhan Chen appointed as independent director and will join key board committees, bringing relevant experience
Negatives
  • NYSE notified SOLAI of non-compliance due to ADS price below $1.00 for a 30-trading-day period
