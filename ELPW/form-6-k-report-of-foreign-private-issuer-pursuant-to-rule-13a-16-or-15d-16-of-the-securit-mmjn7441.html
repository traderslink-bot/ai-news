Summary

Elong Power Holding Limited filed a Form 6-K reporting that between March 2 and March 9, 2026 all previously issued 24,955,000 warrants were fully exercised via a zero exercise price option, resulting in issuance of an aggregate 77,764,364 Class A ordinary shares. As of March 9, 2026 the company has 113,019,749 Class A ordinary shares and 361,090 Class B ordinary shares issued and outstanding.
