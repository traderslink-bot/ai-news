Summary

Elong Power Holding Limited closed an underwritten public offering of 21,700,000 Units at US$0.3231 per Unit, for gross proceeds of approximately US$7.0 million. Each Unit consists of one Class A ordinary share and one Common Warrant exercisable for one Class A ordinary share for three years; warrants are exercisable immediately and have an exercise price initially equal to US$0.3231, with price adjustments on March 3, 2026 and March 6, 2026 to 70% and 50% of the initial price, and a zero-cash exercise option. The underwriter, Maxim Group LLC, has a 45-day option to purchase up to 3,255,000 additional Units and/or 3,255,000 additional Common Warrants, which was partially exercised on February 27, 2026 for 3,255,000 Common Warrants. Proceeds will be used for general corporate purposes and working capital. The offering was conducted under an effective registration statement with the SEC declared on February 25, 2026; a final prospectus is available on the SEC website.
