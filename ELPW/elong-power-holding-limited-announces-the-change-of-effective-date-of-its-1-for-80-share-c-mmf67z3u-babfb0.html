Summary

Elong Power Holding Limited announced a 1-for-80 reverse stock split to maintain Nasdaq listing compliance. The reverse split takes effect at market open on March 12, 2026. Post-split, outstanding common shares will total about 0.79 million from about 63 million; Class A and Class B shares will consolidate at 80-to-1 with no fractional shares issued; shareholders’ ownership percentages remain unchanged; the ticker remains ELPW and a new CUSIP G3016G129 will apply for Class A shares. The move is intended to satisfy Nasdaq Rule 5810(c)(3)(A)(iii).

Positives
  • Maintains Nasdaq bid price compliance by aiming to keep price above $0.10.
  • No action required by shareholders.
  • No fractional shares will be issued; fractional shares are eliminated in favor of whole shares.
  • Outstanding shares reduced to approximately 0.79 million after the reverse split.
  • New CUSIP for Class A shares: G3016G129.
  • Same ticker (ELPW) will continue trading after the split.
