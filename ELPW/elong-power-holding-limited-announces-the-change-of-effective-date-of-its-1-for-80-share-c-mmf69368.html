Summary

Elong Power Holding Limited (Nasdaq: ELPW) issued a press release announcing a change in the effective date for a previously approved 1-for-80 share consolidation (reverse split). The Reverse Split will take effect at the open of Nasdaq on March 12, 2026. The board approved the 1-for-80 ratio on March 5, 2026 following shareholder authorization on January 6, 2026 permitting consolidations up to an aggregate 4000:1 over two years. The consolidation will reduce issued and outstanding common shares from approximately 63 million to approximately 0.79 million and will result in a new CUSIP (G3016G129) while shares will continue trading under the symbol ELPW. The stated objective is to maintain compliance with Nasdaq Listing Rule 5810(c)(3)(A)(iii) (minimum $0.10 closing bid). No action is required for shareholders holding shares through brokers. The company states no fractional shares will be issued and shareholders will receive one full share in lieu of fractional interests; authorized shares will be proportionally reduced.
