Summary

Elong Power Holding Limited announced a reverse stock split of its issued and outstanding Class A and Class B ordinary shares at a ratio of 1-for-80, effective at the open of Nasdaq on March 10, 2026. The consolidation reduces total outstanding common shares from about 63 million to about 0.79 million and will trade under the same ticker ELPW with a new CUSIP (G3016G129). It is intended to maintain Nasdaq Listing Rule 5810(c)(3)(A)(iii) compliance; shareholders need not take any action, and no fractional shares will be issued.

  • No action required by shareholders; no fractional shares issued
