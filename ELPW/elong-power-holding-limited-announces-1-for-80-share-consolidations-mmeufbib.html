Summary

This PRNewswire press release announces Elong Power Holding Limited's 1-for-80 reverse share consolidation of Class A and Class B ordinary shares, effective at the open of Nasdaq on March 10, 2026. The board approved the reverse split on March 5, 2026 under authority granted by shareholders on January 6, 2026 (cumulative consolidation up to 4000:1, authorization valid for two years). The action is intended to enable compliance with Nasdaq Listing Rule 5810(c)(3)(A)(iii) (minimum closing bid > $0.10). Post-consolidation the company’s issued and outstanding common shares will change from approximately 63 million to approximately 0.79 million; authorized shares will be proportionally reduced. Trading will continue under the symbol ELPW with a new CUSIP (G3016G129). Par value of each share will change from $0.00016 to $0.0128. No fractional shares will be issued; the release states shareholders will receive one full share in lieu of any fractional share. No action is required by shareholders holding shares through brokerage accounts.
