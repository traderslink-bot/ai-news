Summary

Reborn Coffee Inc. (NASDAQ: REBN) announced a distribution partnership with Sysco Corporation to support nationwide franchise expansion. The arrangement will use Sysco's distribution network to improve fulfillment reliability, streamline purchasing, and standardize distribution for current and future franchise locations. The deal is expected to enhance quality assurance resources, food safety protocols, local sourcing support, and operational tools, supporting franchise readiness and allowing stores to focus more on customer experience and revenue generation. Sysco operates 340 distribution centers across more than 10 countries and reported over $78 billion in sales in fiscal year 2024.
