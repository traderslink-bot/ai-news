Summary

Research Solutions (NASDAQ: RSSS) launched Scite MCP, a Model Context Protocol integration that connects Scite's database and Smart Citations to AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Microsoft Copilot, Cursor, Claude Code and other MCP-enabled apps). The integration gives connected AI tools access to over 250 million indexed articles, book chapters, preprints and datasets, and delivers citation context (supporting, mentioning, or contrasting) to produce evidence-backed, verifiable answers. Scite MCP is available to paid Scite subscribers and currently provides access to Open Access content while discussions with publishers are underway to expand paywalled coverage.
