Summary

Ondas Inc. (Nasdaq: ONDS) announced a merger agreement to acquire Mistral Inc., a U.S.-based defense prime contractor. Under the deal Mistral will merge with an Ondas subsidiary while retaining contract administration responsibilities, providing Ondas with direct prime-contractor access to U.S. Army and USSOCOM contract vehicles, U.S.-based manufacturing and integration infrastructure, and established federal/state contracting capabilities. Mistral is a prime contractor on over $1 billion of IDIQ and other DoD contracts. Ondas said the acquisition advances its strategy to expand participation in multi-year DoD contract vehicles; terms will be disclosed in an 8-K to be filed later. Ondas will discuss Mistral on its March 25, 2026 earnings call.
