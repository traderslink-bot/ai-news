Summary

Ondas Inc. (Nasdaq:ONDS) has entered into a merger agreement with Mistral Inc., a U.S.-based defense prime contractor. Under the agreement, Mistral will merge with a subsidiary of Ondas while maintaining its current contract administration responsibilities. The merger provides Ondas with direct prime contractor access to U.S. Army and Special Operations contract vehicles and adds U.S.-based manufacturing, integration, and federal contracting infrastructure. Mistral brings decades of defense and public safety experience and has acted as a prime contractor on over a billion dollars of IDIQ and other U.S. DOD contracts. The transaction aligns with Ondas' defense expansion strategy and Ondas will discuss outlook on its earnings call on March 25, 2026.
