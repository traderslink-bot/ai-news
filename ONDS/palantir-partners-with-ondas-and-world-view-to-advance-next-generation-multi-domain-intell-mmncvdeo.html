Summary

Press release announcing a strategic partnership among Palantir Technologies (NASDAQ: PLTR), Ondas Inc. (NASDAQ: ONDS) and World View to integrate Palantir's Artificial Intelligence Platform (AIP) with Ondas' autonomous aerial/ground systems and World View's Stratollite stratospheric platforms. The collaboration targets development of three programs—Warp Speed (production/mission readiness/operational data foundation), AI Flight Director (AI-enabled mission operations), and SkyWeaver (on-vehicle edge computing)—to enable persistent multi-domain ISR, unified command-and-control, and scaled stratospheric/aerial/land operations. Work to optimize World View systems has begun and integration across Ondas' portfolio is planned as early as Q4 2026. No financial terms or contract values were disclosed.
