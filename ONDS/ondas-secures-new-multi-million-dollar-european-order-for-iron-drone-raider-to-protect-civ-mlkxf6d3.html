Summary

Ondas Inc. (NASDAQ: ONDS) announced that its subsidiary Airobotics received a new multi-million dollar order from a major European customer in a NATO country for the Iron Drone Raider counter-UAS system. The award follows an operational deployment of the system at a major European international airport tied to a separate contract from the same customer and complements prior orders (totaling $16.8 million) to protect two major European airports. Company highlights Iron Drone Raider’s net-based kinetic interception as a civil-compliant alternative to RF jamming or ballistic interceptors and positions Ondas, together with Sentrycs and Insight Intelligent Sensors, to offer an integrated detection-to-interception solution for civil infrastructure protection.

Positives
  • New multi-million dollar order for Iron Drone Raider from a major European NATO-country customer
  • Operational deployment at a major European international airport already completed
  • Prior related orders amounting to $16.8 million to protect two major European airports

  • Positions Ondas to deliver an integrated end-to-end counter-UAS solution (detection, ID, electronic mitigation, kinetic interception)
