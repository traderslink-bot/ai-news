Summary

Sagtec Global (NASDAQ: SAGT) announces a strategic evolution to an Agentic AI Orchestration Layer, transitioning to autonomous agent workflows and production-ready orchestration to support global enterprise SaaS. Key metrics include a target FY2026 AI revenue of $12–$15 million under a Usage + Subscription model, an internal test showing ~40% reduction in operational latency, and US market expansion plans in retail, mobility, and food service automation. Full audited results due in April 2026 with prior-year preliminary revenue growth of 64% and net profit growth of 75%. Previously announced a US$4.0 million Smart AI mobility contract. 12,000+ business clients served.
