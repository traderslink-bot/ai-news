Summary

Fiscal year 2025 ended December 31, 2025. Net revenue rose 16.52% to $18.41 million. Gross profit was $4.29 million with a 23.33% gross margin. Net loss attributable to INLIF was $5.45 million, or a $0.33 basic/diluted loss per share. New energy sector-focused products contributed 12.98% of total revenue. Operating expenses were $10.11 million, up 209.63%, with share-based compensation about $5.14 million. Cash and cash equivalents totaled $6.72 million at year-end, up from $2.47 million. Net cash from financing activities was $6.87 million. Production volume declined to 2,763 units from 3,026. Revenue by category included $9.07 million from manipulator arms and services, $2.27 million from accessories, $4.64 million from raw materials and scraps, and $2.39 million from new energy sector-focused products.

Positives

  • Gross margin remained above 20% at 23.33%
