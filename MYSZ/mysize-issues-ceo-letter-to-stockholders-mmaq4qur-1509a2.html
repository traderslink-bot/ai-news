Summary

Nasdaq notified MySize that its closing bid price has been below $1.00 for 30 consecutive trading days, giving the company 180 calendar days (until August 31, 2026) to regain compliance. The company states the notice does not impact operations, customers, or financial position and plans to regain compliance through execution and by growing higher-margin activities. 2025 revenue was approximately $10 million across the platform; Orgad had roughly 40% gross margins in 2025; 10peaks, formed September 2025, generated about $150,000 in 2025 revenue with ~65% gross margins and could potentially reach ~$3 million in annual revenue. The broader platform includes Naiz Fit (AI sizing) and Percentil (secondhand marketplace). The letter also reiterates a strategy to grow revenue, shift toward higher-margin activities, and improve operating leverage, and it notes forward-looking statements and preliminary 2025 results disclosure.

Positives
  • Diversified platform with Naiz Fit, Percentil, Orgad, and 10peaks.
  • Orgad posted about 40% gross margins in 2025, providing a stable revenue base.
  • 10peaks generated approximately $150,000 in 2025 revenue with ~65% gross margins and potential for higher-margin growth.
  • 2025 revenue reported at approximately $10 million across the platform.
  • Strategic focus on growing revenue and increasing share of higher-margin activities to improve operating leverage.
  • Stock remains listed on Nasdaq under the symbol MYSZ.
Negatives
  • Nasdaq notice due to closing bid price below $1.00 for 30 consecutive trading days.
  • Regain compliance required within 180 days (by August 31, 2026); potential delisting if not regained or if second compliance period is not available.
  • Forward-looking statements and estimated results are not audited and may differ from actual results.
