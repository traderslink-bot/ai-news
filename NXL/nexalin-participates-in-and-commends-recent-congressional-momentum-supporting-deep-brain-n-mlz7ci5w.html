Summary

Nexalin Technology, Inc. (NXL) announces its participation in and commendation of bipartisan Congressional momentum to expand deep brain neurostimulation research for veterans and neurological disorders. The release highlights legislative activity around H.R. 7091 and related appropriations that could expand veteran access to emerging neuromodulation therapies, aligns Nexalin's DIFS technology with UC San Diego and the San Diego VA research, and notes Nexalin America's government engagement efforts. Ongoing clinical study of DIFS at UC San Diego with veterans from the San Diego VA is mentioned. The company cites lobbying disclosures and includes a CEO quote. No financial guidance is provided.
