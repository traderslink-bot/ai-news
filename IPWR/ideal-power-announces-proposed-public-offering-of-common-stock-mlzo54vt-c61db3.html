Summary

Ideal Power announces an underwritten public offering of its common stock. Titan Partners, a division of American Capital Partners, is the sole bookrunner. The offering includes a 30-day over-allotment option to purchase up to 15% of the shares. Proceeds will be used to advance commercialization of B-TRAN and for general corporate purposes. The offering is being made under an effective Form S-3 shelf registration (No. 333-292492). A preliminary prospectus supplement and base prospectus have been filed with the SEC. Completion depends on market conditions; no guarantee of the offering. Documents are available on the SEC site.

Positives
  • Shelf registration under Form S-3 is already effective, enabling quicker future offerings
  • Sole bookrunner designated (Titan Partners) supports credible execution
  • Proceeds earmarked for commercialization of B-TRAN and working capital
  • Preliminary prospectus and base prospectus filed with the SEC and available for due diligence
Negatives
  • Offering is subject to market conditions with no assurance of completion
  • Possible dilution to existing shareholders due to new share issuance
  • Reliance on future market demand for the company’s stock and B-TRAN technology
  • Over-allotment option could increase the number of shares offered
