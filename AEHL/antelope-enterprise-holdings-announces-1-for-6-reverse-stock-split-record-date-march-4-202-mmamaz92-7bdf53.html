Summary

Antelope Enterprise Holdings Limited announced a reverse stock split of its class A ordinary shares at a ratio of 1-for-6. The reverse split becomes effective at 4:01 p.m. ET on March 4, 2026, and ordinary shares will begin trading on a split-adjusted basis on March 5, 2026 under Nasdaq symbol BIYA. Pre-split outstanding shares were 7,344,694; post-split outstanding shares will be approximately 1,224,116. No fractional shares will be issued. Shareholders with shares held in book-entry form or through brokers do not need to take action. The new CUSIP is G041JN148.
