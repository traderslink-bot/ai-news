Summary

Antelope Enterprise Holdings Limited (NASDAQ: AEHL) announced completion of an initial $1 million Bitcoin purchase as the launch of its "Genius Plan," a long-term digital asset allocation and capital recycling framework. The company said it will pursue phased accumulation, establish independent cost anchors for each tranche, and provide daily updates of holdings via its official accounts on X, Futu, and Tiger Trade. AEHL emphasized the initiative is long-term, intends to continue increasing investment as market conditions allow, and reiterated compliance with applicable regulations.
