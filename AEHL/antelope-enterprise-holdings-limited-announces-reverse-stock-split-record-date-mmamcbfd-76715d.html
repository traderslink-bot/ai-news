Summary

Antelope Enterprise Holdings Limited announced a 1-for-6 reverse stock split of its class A ordinary shares. The reverse split becomes effective at 4:01 p.m. ET on March 4, 2026, with trading on a split-adjusted basis starting March 5, 2026. The company will trade on Nasdaq Capital Market under the symbol BIYA with new CUSIP G041JN148. Pre-split outstanding shares were 7,344,694; post-split outstanding shares will be approximately 1,224,116. No fractional shares will be issued. The reverse split affects all holders uniformly.
