Summary

UroGen Pharma (NASDAQ: URGN) issued a press release reporting post-hoc analyses from the Phase 3 ENVISION trial (NCT05243550) showing ZUSDURI (mitomycin) for intravesical solution achieved high complete response (CR) rates at 3 months across EORTC recurrence-score groups in recurrent low-grade intermediate-risk non-muscle invasive bladder cancer (LG-IR-NMIBC). In 240 treated patients in the analysis, 3-month CR rates were 83.9% (EORTC 1–4), 81.2% (5–9) and 60.0% (10–17); overall 3-month CR was 79.6% and Kaplan–Meier event-free probability at 24 months was 72.2%. Among responders, 24-month event-free probabilities by group were 67.4%, 73.7% and 66.7%; median duration of response was not reached. A poster with these results will be presented at ASCO-GU 2026. The release notes the post-hoc nature and smaller subgroup sizes and reiterates approved use and safety information for ZUSDURI.
