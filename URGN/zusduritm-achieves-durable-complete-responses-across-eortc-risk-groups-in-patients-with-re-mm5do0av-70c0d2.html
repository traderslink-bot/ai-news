Summary

Post-hoc analysis from Phase 3 ENVISION shows ZUSDURI (mitomycin) intravesical solution achieves durable complete responses across European Organization for Research and Treatment of Cancer recurrence score groups in recurrent low-grade intermediate-risk non-muscle invasive bladder cancer. At 3 months, complete response rates were 83.9% (EORTC 1–4), 81.2% (EORTC 5–9) and 60.0% (EORTC 10–17); the majority remained recurrence-free at 24 months. Overall CR rate was 79.6% at 3 months with 24-month event-free survival around 67–73% across groups. A poster detailing the ENVISION EORTC recurrence score analysis will be presented at ASCO-GU 2026. ENVISION is a Phase 3 trial (NCT05243550); patients will be followed for up to five years.

Positives
  • Durable complete responses across all EORTC recurrence score groups
  • High 3-month CR rates across risk groups
  • Majority of responders recurrence-free at 24 months
  • Phase 3 ENVISION trial supports a non-surgical treatment option for recurrent LG-IR-NMIBC
  • Data being presented at ASCO-GU 2026
Negatives
  • Post-hoc analysis with small subgroup sizes
  • Durability data limited to up to 24 months in this report; five-year follow-up ongoing
