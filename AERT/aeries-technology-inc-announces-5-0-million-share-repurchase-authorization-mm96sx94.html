Summary

Aeries Technology, Inc. (NASDAQ: AERT) announced that its Board authorized a share repurchase program of up to $5.0 million of the Company's outstanding Class A ordinary shares over a 12-month period. Repurchases may be made via open market purchases, private negotiated transactions, accelerated share repurchase programs or other legally permissible means. The program is discretionary, may be suspended, modified or discontinued, and does not obligate the Company to repurchase any specific amount of shares. The Board stated the authorization reflects confidence in the Company’s strategy and long-term growth opportunities.
