Summary

Newton Golf Company (NASDAQ: NWTG) will attend the 38th Annual ROTH Conference in Dana Point, California, March 22-24, 2026. CEO Dr. Greg Campbell will participate in one-on-one meetings with analysts and investors, joined by CFO Jeff Clayborne and COO. The company is expanding its product line, brand awareness, and global presence following 2025 revenue growth, including leading shaft sales at Club Champion. Newton Golf has expanded direct-to-consumer presence in Japan and entered an exclusive distribution partnership with VOICE CADDIE in South Korea. Newton will sponsor a ROTH golf tournament on March 22 to support the Challenged Athletes Foundation and will showcase its Motion Driver and Fast Motion Driver shafts during the event.
