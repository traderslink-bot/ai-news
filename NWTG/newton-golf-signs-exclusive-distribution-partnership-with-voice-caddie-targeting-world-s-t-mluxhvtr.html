Summary

Newton Golf (NASDAQ: NWTG) entered an exclusive distribution agreement with VC Inc. (VOICE CADDIE) granting wholesale and retail distribution rights for Newton products in South Korea. VOICE CADDIE will handle promotion, marketing and sales, leveraging its leading market position, large real-world golf database and LPGA partnership. The deal aims to expand Newton's presence in a high-spend, technology-driven market and covers branded governance, performance expectations, and compliance with Newton's marketing and IP policies. Newton expects the partnership to broaden access to its Motion™ and Fast Motion™ driver shafts and complements its recent Japan e-commerce launch.
