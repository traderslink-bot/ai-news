Summary

Klotho Neurosciences announces development of two genomics-based diagnostic tests to be used in its clinical trials and as a service in longevity research. One is a quantitative DNA methylation assay for alpha-Klotho using cell-free DNA; the other, called the Klotho Clock, measures DNA and mRNA sequences of Klotho and eight longevity-associated genes. A prototype is expected in 2026, which could enable earlier revenue and support clock improvements. Dr. Bret Barnes will oversee the diagnostic program; the company emphasizes integration with AI clocks and multiomics capabilities.
