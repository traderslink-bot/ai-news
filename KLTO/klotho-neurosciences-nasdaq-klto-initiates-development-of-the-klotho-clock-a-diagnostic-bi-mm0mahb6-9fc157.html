Summary

Klotho Neurosciences announces development of two genomics-based diagnostic tests for biological aging: a quantitative DNA methylation assay of the anti-aging gene alpha-Klotho using cell-free DNA, and the \"Klotho Clock\" that measures DNA and mRNA sequences of Klotho and eight longevity-associated genes. The tests will be used in clinical trials and offered as a service. Dr. Bret Barnes has been enlisted to oversee the diagnostic program. A prototype is expected in 2026. The company indicates potential for immediate revenue upon product launch and intends to build a database to improve the clock, with integration into AI-based clocks and multiomics capabilities (CNV and SNV).
