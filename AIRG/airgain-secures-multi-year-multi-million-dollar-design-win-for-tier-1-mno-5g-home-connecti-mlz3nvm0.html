Summary

Airgain (NASDAQ: AIRG) announced a multi‑year, multi‑million‑dollar embedded antenna design win with a major North American mobile network operator for a next‑generation 5G home connectivity platform. The program covers antenna systems for a 5G fixed wireless access (FWA) router and a companion in‑home Wi‑Fi extender; pre‑production units are expected to ship this quarter with mass production anticipated later in the year. The win builds on a previously disclosed Wi‑Fi 7 design win and is presented as expanding Airgain's incumbent position with Tier‑1 North American MNOs and supporting growth in consumer broadband and embedded antenna services.
