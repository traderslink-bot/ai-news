Summary

Vision Marine demonstrated premium electric watersports performance across its Nautical Ventures dealer network following the Discover Boating Miami International Boat Show (Feb 11–15, 2026). Nautical Ventures Watersports Division generated gross revenue within approximately 5% of the prior year despite lower unit volume, attributed to higher average transaction values and a shift toward premium electric and performance-oriented products. The Electric Division portfolio, launched September 2025, has been integrated into Nautical Ventures’ Florida showrooms and displays, positioning premium electric mobility alongside Vision Marine’s E‑Motion high-voltage propulsion system. Vision Marine describes the Electric Division as evolving into an ecosystem that aligns propulsion-equipped boats, premium electric watersports platforms, and retail infrastructure under a single framework.
