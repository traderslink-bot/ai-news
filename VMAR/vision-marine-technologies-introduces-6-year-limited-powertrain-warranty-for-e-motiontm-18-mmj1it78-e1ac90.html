Summary

Vision Marine Technologies announced a six-year limited powertrain warranty for its E-Motion™ 180E high-voltage electric outboard system for recreational boating. The company claims it is the first in North America to offer a standard six-year warranty on high-voltage outboards above 150 horsepower for recreational use, extending one year beyond typical offerings. The E-Motion™ 180E has been integrated across more than 24 boat configurations and 12 OEM brands, supported by Vision Marine's vertically integrated retail and service network, including Nautical Ventures in Florida.
