Summary

VisionWave Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: VWAV) focuses on AI-powered sensing, autonomy, counter-UAS, and RF imaging. In 2025, VisionWave filed an 8-K announcing a consulting agreement to design a digital asset treasury reserve with Crypto Treasury Management Group, with potential capital formation up to $300 million and investments in Bitcoin and Solana. The company has a $50 million equity line to scale deployment, a live-fire radar demo in Abu Dhabi with a major defense partner, and ongoing discussions in India on armored fleet upgrades and counter-UAS integration. VisionWave entered a joint venture with AIPHEX to commercialize defense and space technologies, filed a trademark for Evolved Intelligence, and advanced its Radio Wave Finder patent. The IP portfolio includes two granted patents and plans for four additional patent applications. The article situates VisionWave among defense tech peers such as Palantir, AeroVironment, Anduril, and Kratos, in a sector expected to grow with expanding AI, autonomy, and sensing capabilities.
