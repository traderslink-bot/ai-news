Summary

Press release (Feb 18, 2026, Kfar Saba, Israel): ParaZero Technologies Ltd. (NASDAQ: PRZO) announced that in an internal live test its DefendAir net-launching counter-UAS system successfully intercepted a fast-moving drone traveling at 70 mph. The company says this is the first net-based interception at that speed for ParaZero and demonstrated real-time, on-the-move protection in a simulated combat scenario. DefendAir is described as a non-explosive, low-collateral solution available in handheld, stationary point-protection, and drone-mounted configurations. The release reiterates forward-looking statement disclaimers and references the company’s SEC filings for risk factors.
