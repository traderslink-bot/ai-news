Summary

ParaZero Technologies Ltd. (NASDAQ: PRZO) announced receipt of an additional order from a second branch of an Israeli defense entity for its DefendAir counter‑UAS solution. The order includes specialized net-launchers, interception pods (handheld, stationary and drone‑mounted), and a training package (simulation and live‑fire). The announcement cites a prior deployment earlier this year and demonstrations with claimed 100% interception rates against high‑speed threats. The release does not disclose contract value, delivery schedule, or expected revenue impact. Forward‑looking statements are included.
