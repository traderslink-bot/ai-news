Summary

Barnwell Industries, Inc. (BRN) reported in its Q1 FY2026 Form 10-Q that the Twining oil field in Alberta delivered reliable production during winter operations, with approximately 35,000 barrels of oil, 10,000 barrels of NGLs, and 250,000 thousand cubic feet of natural gas in the quarter, totaling 86,667 barrels of oil equivalents (about 950 BOE per day). The company noted minimal operational disruptions and highlighted Alberta's stability as a source of North American supply; full results are in the filed Form 10-Q with the SEC.
