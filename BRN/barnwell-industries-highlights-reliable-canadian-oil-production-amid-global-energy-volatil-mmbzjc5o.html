Summary

Press release from Barnwell Industries (NYSE American: BRN) highlighting operational results disclosed in its recently filed Q1 FY2026 Form 10-Q. Barnwell reported approximately 35,000 barrels of oil, 10,000 barrels of NGLs, and 250,000 thousand cubic feet of natural gas from its Canadian properties in the quarter, totaling 86,667 BOE (about 950 BOE/day). Production came from the Twining oil field in Alberta with minimal winter-related disruptions. The company emphasized the strategic positioning of Western Canadian supply for North American markets and noted continued focus on disciplined capital allocation and operational oversight. The release references the filed 10-Q for additional financial and operational details and contains standard forward-looking statement disclaimers.
