Summary

Profusa, Inc. (NASDAQ: PFSA) announced it will withdraw its Form S-1 registration statement originally filed February 13, 2026, and pursue a smaller, more focused equity offering to support near-term Europe Lumee Oxygen product revenue in Q2 2026 and to fund the Mayo Clinic collaboration to accelerate Lumee Oxygen in the U.S. The company said a previously contemplated $15 million offering is not required to meet near-term revenue projections. No securities were sold in connection with the original offering. A post-effective amendment will be filed with the SEC. The company cited commercial and operational progress, a strengthened balance sheet, and anticipated Europe market launch as rationale; planned U.S. market entry for Lumee Oxygen with Mayo Clinic remains on track for late 2026.

Positives
  • Recent commercial and operational progress

  • Mayo Clinic collaboration to accelerate Lumee Oxygen in the U.S. and develop new continuous oxygen monitoring products
Negatives
  • Withdrawal of the S-1 registration statement
  • Plan to pursue a smaller equity offering rather than the previously contemplated $15 million offering
