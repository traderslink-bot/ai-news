Summary

Profusa, Inc. (NASDAQ: PFSA) announced it will not proceed with the previously filed Registration Statement on Form S-1 (No. 333-293451) for a proposed $15 million offering and intends to file a post-effective amendment with the SEC to withdraw the S-1. No securities were sold under the Registration Statement. The company said it will pursue a smaller, more focused offering to support near-term projected Europe Lumee Oxygen product revenue beginning in Q2 2026 and to fund its collaboration with the Mayo Clinic to accelerate U.S. commercialization and development of continuous oxygen monitoring products. Profusa cited recent changes in projected 2026 revenue and its Mayo collaboration as reasons for revising near-term capital needs.
