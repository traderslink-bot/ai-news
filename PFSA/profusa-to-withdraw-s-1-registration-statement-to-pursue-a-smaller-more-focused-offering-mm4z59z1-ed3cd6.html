Summary

Profusa, Inc. (NASDAQ: PFSA) announced it will not proceed with its public offering and intends to file a post-effective amendment to its Form S-1 originally filed February 13, 2026. The company will pursue a smaller, more focused offering to support near-term projected Europe Lumee Oxygen product revenue starting in Q2 2026 and investment in its Mayo collaboration to accelerate Lumee Oxygen in the U.S. and develop and commercialize new continuous oxygen monitoring products. No securities were sold in connection with the offering described in the Registration Statement. The decision reflects commercial and operational progress, a strengthened balance sheet, and anticipated early Q2 2026 Europe market launch and late-2026 U.S. market entry with Mayo Clinic.

  • Anticipated Europe Lumee Oxygen market launch in Q2 2026
  • Mayo Clinic collaboration to accelerate Lumee Oxygen in the U.S.
  • Strengthened balance sheet due to operational progress
  • No securities were sold in connection with the initial offering
