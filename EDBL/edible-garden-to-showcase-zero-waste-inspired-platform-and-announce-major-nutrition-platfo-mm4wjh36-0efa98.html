Summary

Edible Garden AG Incorporated will exhibit at Natural Products Expo West 2026 (Booth N1546) at the Anaheim Convention Center from March 3–6, 2026. The company will highlight its vertically integrated growth platform and announce a significant strategic initiative expanding its Farm to Formula model and reinforcing its Zero-Waste Inspired operating framework to support growth in fresh produce, clean-label nutrition, and value-added offerings.

  • Company will showcase its vertically integrated growth platform and announce a strategic initiative to advance corporate development.
  • Expansion of the Farm to Formula model and capacity to support growing retailer and consumer demand.
  • Zero-Waste Inspired operating framework aimed at scalable growth and long-term value creation.
