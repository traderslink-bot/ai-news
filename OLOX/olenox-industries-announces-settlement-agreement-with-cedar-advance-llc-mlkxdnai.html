Summary

Olenox Industries Inc. (NASDAQ: OLOX) announced a mutual settlement with Cedar Advance LLC resolving amounts owed under their Standard Merchant Cash Advance Agreements. Fully executed Feb. 10, 2026, the Company agreed to issue restricted shares of common stock at market price to Cedar in satisfaction of the obligations and both parties provided mutual releases of claims. Full terms are disclosed in a Form 8-K filed Feb. 13, 2026.

Positives
  • Outstanding obligations to Cedar Advance LLC are resolved through the settlement.
  • Mutual release of claims provides a full and final resolution of liabilities under the agreements.
  • Settlement avoids further dispute or litigation related to the merchant cash advance agreements.
Negatives
  • Settlement is being satisfied by issuance of restricted common shares, which may dilute existing shareholders.
  • Press release does not disclose the specific dollar amount or share quantity involved in the settlement.
  • Key financial/details of the settlement must be reviewed in the referenced Form 8-K for full disclosure.
