Summary

Olenox Industries Inc. (NASDAQ: OLOX) announced the start of its 2026 drilling program, planning to drill a total of 10 wells: one by the end of the current calendar quarter and three wells in each of the second, third, and fourth quarters. The company completed a pipeline survey and is preparing paperwork to recommission and relicense its 162-mile pipeline, with full operation expected by the end of the third quarter of 2026. Olenox is evaluating wells connected to the pipeline for potential acquisitions and aims to reach 1,000 barrels of oil equivalent per day by year-end. CEO Michael McLaren said the company intends to bring wells online on a weekly basis through workovers and lease enhancements.
