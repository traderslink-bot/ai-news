Summary

Press release (GlobeNewswire) dated March 3, 2026: Olenox Industries Inc. (NASDAQ: OLOX) announced a 2026 drilling program that includes shooting seismic data immediately and a 10-well schedule — one well by the end of the current quarter and three wells in each of Q2, Q3 and Q4 2026. The company completed a pipeline survey and is preparing paperwork to recommission and relicense its 162-mile pipeline, targeting full functionality by end of Q3 2026. Olenox is evaluating wells attached to the pipeline as potential acquisitions and expects to continue workovers and lease enhancements to bring wells online weekly. Management’s 2026 production target is 1,000 BOE/day by year-end. The release contains forward-looking statements and lists risks including the ability to execute the drilling schedule, recommission the pipeline, and maintain NASDAQ listing compliance.
