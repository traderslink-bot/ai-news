Summary

Olenox Industries Inc. (NASDAQ: OLOX) announced its 2026 drilling program is underway with plans to drill 10 wells: one in the current quarter and three in each of the next three quarters. Seismic data collection is to begin this month. The 162-mile pipeline has finished its survey and preparations to recommission and relicense, with full functionality expected by the end of Q3 2026. The company is evaluating wells connected to the pipeline for potential acquisitions and aims for 1,000 boe per day by year-end. The company previously operated as Safe & Green Holdings Corp (SGBX).
