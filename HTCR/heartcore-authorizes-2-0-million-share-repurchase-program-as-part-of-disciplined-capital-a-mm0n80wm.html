Summary

HeartCore Enterprises, Inc. (NASDAQ: HTCR) announced its Board has authorized a share repurchase program to buy up to $2.0 million of its common stock. The company said its preliminary, unaudited estimates indicate total net assets exceeded market capitalization as of Feb. 24, 2026, and framed the repurchase as part of a disciplined capital allocation strategy following recent restructuring and an improved profitability outlook. Repurchases may be executed via open market, privately negotiated transactions, or Rule 10b5-1 plans, and will depend on market conditions, capital availability and other factors; the program may be modified, suspended or discontinued.
