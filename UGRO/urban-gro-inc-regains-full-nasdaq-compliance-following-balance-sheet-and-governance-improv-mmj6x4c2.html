Summary

urban-gro, Inc. (Nasdaq: UGRO) announced it has received written notification from Nasdaq that it has regained compliance with previously cited listing deficiencies. Nasdaq determined the company now meets periodic reporting requirements after filing a delinquent Form 10-K and subsequent Form 10-Qs, satisfies the minimum stockholders' equity requirement following completed transactions, meets the $1.00 minimum bid price after effecting a reverse stock split, and has met the annual meeting requirement. Nasdaq will keep the company on a Discretionary Panel Monitor for one year to oversee continued compliance.
