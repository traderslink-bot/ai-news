Summary

urban-gro, Inc. announced that Nasdaq notified the company it has regained compliance with periodic reporting, minimum stockholders' equity, minimum bid price, and annual meeting requirements. The company will remain on Nasdaq's Discretionary Panel Monitor for one year to address potential future compliance concerns. This follows urban-gro's recent combination with Flash Sports & Media and its ongoing business operations in sports, media, and experiential marketing.

  • Regained annual meeting requirement (Rule 5620(a))
