Summary

Akari Therapeutics, plc (NASDAQ: AKTX) announced the appointment of Olga Anczukow, Ph.D., a leading authority in RNA biology and cancer research, to its Scientific Advisory Board. The company states her expertise will guide research strategy as it advances its PH1 spliceosome-modulating ADC payload and AKTX-101 (targeting Trop2) toward IND-enabling studies with a planned first-in-human trial by late 2026/early 2027. AKTX-101 has shown preclinical activity and potential synergy with checkpoint inhibitors, and the company is also developing AKTX-102 targeting CEACAM5.
