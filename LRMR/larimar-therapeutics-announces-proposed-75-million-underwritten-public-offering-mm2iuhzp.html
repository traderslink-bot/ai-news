Summary

Larimar Therapeutics (NASDAQ: LRMR) announced an underwritten public offering of $75.0 million of common stock and, for certain investors who prefer, pre-funded warrants to purchase common stock. The company also expects to grant underwriters a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional $11.25 million of the securities. All offered securities will be sold by Larimar. J.P. Morgan and Guggenheim Securities are joint bookrunning managers. Larimar intends to use net proceeds to support development of nomlabofusp and for working capital and general corporate purposes. The offering will be made pursuant to Larimar’s shelf registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-279275) declared effective May 24, 2024; a preliminary prospectus supplement will be filed with the SEC. Completion, terms and timing are subject to market and other conditions.
