Summary

SEC filing: Prospectus Supplement on Form 424B5 by Larimar Therapeutics, Inc. (Nasdaq: LRMR) offering 20,000,000 shares of common stock at $5.00 per share (gross $100.0M). Underwriting discount $0.30/share; net proceeds to company ~ $93.5M (approx. $107.6M if underwriters exercise option in full). Underwriters have a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 3,000,000 shares. Expected delivery of shares around Feb 27, 2026. Pro forma shares outstanding immediately after the offering: 100,590,392 (103,590,392 if option exercised). Pre-offering outstanding basis cited as ~80,590,392 shares (pro forma adjustments noted). Company intends to use proceeds to support development and potential commercialization of nomlabofusp and for working capital. Deerfield Management Company, L.P. (the company’s largest stockholder) and certain directors have indicated non-binding interest in purchasing shares. Recent disclosed items: unaudited cash, cash equivalents and marketable securities approximately $136.9M as of Dec 31, 2025; FDA Breakthrough Therapy designation for nomlabofusp (Feb 2026); company targets a BLA submission (accelerated approval pathway) for nomlabofusp in June 2026; FDA START pilot engagement and agreement to consider skin FXN concentration as a potential surrogate endpoint. The prospectus supplement discloses safety developments (anaphylaxis deemed an adverse drug reaction), protocol amendments (premedication and test-dose regimen), significant risk factors (including substantial doubt about ability to continue as a going concern absent additional financing and potential dilution), and detailed equity incentive and convertible securities information.
