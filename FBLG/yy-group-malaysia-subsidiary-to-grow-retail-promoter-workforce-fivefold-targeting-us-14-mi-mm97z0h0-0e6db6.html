Summary

YY Group Holding Limited's subsidiary YY Circle Malaysia plans to increase its retail promoter workforce from 120 to 600, aiming for US$14 million in revenue in 2026. The expansion is projected to contribute a 15% to 20% revenue increase for YY Circle Malaysia and will be managed through the YY Circle platform. YY Group trades on NASDAQ under ticker YYGH.
