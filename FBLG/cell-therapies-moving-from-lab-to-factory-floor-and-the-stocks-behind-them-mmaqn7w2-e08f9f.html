Overview of the global cell therapy market growth and current manufacturing milestones for several companies, including Avaí Bio's Master Cell Bank production with JV Klothonova; FibroBiologics' osteoporosis patent; Fate Therapeutics' FT819 outpatient progress; Mesoblast's Ryoncil survival data and Phase 3 plans; Longeveron's Phase 2b frailty results. Positives
  • Avaí Bio and Austrianova initiated manufacturing of a Master Cell Bank for α-Klotho–expressing cells to support scalable production.
  • The Klothonova joint venture focuses on sustainable production of α-Klotho–related therapies using encapsulation.
  • FibroBiologics received a U.S. patent covering fibroblast cell therapy for osteoporosis, expanding its IP portfolio.
  • Fate Therapeutics reported outpatient discharge for FT819 CAR T-cell therapy in its trials, potentially expanding patient access and reducing hospitalization needs.
  • Mesoblast presented data showing Ryoncil achieved comparable day-100 survival in adults and children with steroid-refractory aGvHD, supporting Phase 3 enrollment in adults.
  • Longeveron Phase 2b results showed dose-dependent improvements in the six-minute walk distance in age-related frailty.
