Summary

The United States Patent No. 12,544,407 B2 was issued on February 10, 2026 covering methods of treating osteoporosis and other bone diseases through administration of fibroblast cells. The patent contemplates systemic or local administration of fibroblasts, including modified fibroblasts, to modulate bone remodeling by inhibiting osteoclast activity and/or promoting osteoblast activity, with claims directed to fibroblasts expressing recombinant CXCR4 or hTERT, dedifferentiated fibroblasts generated via OCT4/NANOG/SOX2, and fibroblasts with specific marker profiles. The issuance strengthens FibroBiologics' IP portfolio in regenerative medicine and bone-related disorders; the company notes it holds 270+ patents issued and pending.
