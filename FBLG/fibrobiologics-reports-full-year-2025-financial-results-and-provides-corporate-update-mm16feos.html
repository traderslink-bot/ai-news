Summary

Press release from FibroBiologics (NASDAQ: FBLG) reporting full-year 2025 results and a corporate update. Key operational updates: secured public and private HREC approvals in Australia to enroll up to 120 patients at as many as 10 sites for a Phase 1/2 trial of CYWC628 in diabetic foot ulcers (DFU) with first patient dosing planned in H1 2026; filed an IND with the FDA for CYPS317 for psoriasis; plans IND submission for a multiple sclerosis candidate (CYMS101) in H1 2026; expanded patent filings including a USPTO filing for orthopedic/musculoskeletal applications. Financing and balance sheet: raised $7.2 million through direct offerings, repaid all outstanding debt, and reported cash and cash equivalents of approximately $4.9 million at December 31, 2025. Financials: R&D expense $7.4M (2025) vs $4.5M (2024); G&A $9.2M (2025) unchanged year-over-year; net loss $18.6M (2025) vs $11.2M (2024). Upcoming milestones include cGMP manufacturing of CYWC628 in Q1 2026, site enrollment and dosing in H1 2026, interim results in H1 2026 and final DFU results by end of 2026. The company received a Nasdaq Hearings Panel extension to regain compliance.
