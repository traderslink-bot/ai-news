Summary

Ainos, Inc. issued a letter from Chairman, President and CEO Eddy Tsai to shareholders detailing a phased deployment of up to 20,000 AI Nose systems, starting with an initial order of 1,400 systems and a three-year service arrangement totaling about $2.1 million in recurring revenue. The plan envisions up to 20,000 systems and annual order values approaching $10 million if fully executed, with deployment across healthcare and industrial markets. The AI Nose platform integrates edge hardware, subscription analytics, continuous AI model optimization, and multi-site scalability, and is undergoing validation in advanced semiconductor front-end facilities to support broader industrial adoption. The letter notes forward-looking statements and related risks.

Positives
  • Initial order of 1,400 AI Nose systems with about $2.1 million in three-year recurring revenue.
  • Phased deployment framework could scale to up to 20,000 systems with potential annual order values near $10 million.
  • AI Nose platform is designed for scalability across healthcare and industrial markets.
  • Validation in advanced semiconductor front-end facilities may enable broader industrial integration.
Negatives
  • Company may incur net losses in the foreseeable future.
  • Need to raise additional capital to continue product development.
  • Profitability and future operating results are uncertain.
  • Advancement depends on staged validation and contractual conversions; risks include delays and regulatory approvals.
