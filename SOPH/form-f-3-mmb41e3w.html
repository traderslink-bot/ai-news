Registration statement on Form F-3 filed by SOPHiA GENETICS SA registering for resale up to 75,000 ordinary shares issuable upon exercise of a warrant certificate held by Perceptive Credit Holdings IV, LP (the selling shareholder). Filing type: Form F-3 (registration statement / prospectus). Registrant: SOPHiA GENETICS SA (Switzerland). Selling shareholder / reporting entity: Perceptive Credit Holdings IV, LP. Securities: up to 75,000 ordinary shares (Second Amendment warrant shares) issuable at $5.1829 per share on exercise; warrant exercisable immediately and expires January 23, 2036. The prospectus notes the selling shareholder currently (as of the prospectus date) beneficially holds warrants covering a total of 475,000 ordinary shares (including Tranche A, Tranche B and Second Amendment warrants) and is offering 75,000 of those shares under this registration; after sale the selling shareholder would beneficially own 400,000 shares/warrants. Based on SOPHiA GENETICS' reported registered outstanding ordinary shares of 89,321,220 (as of Dec 31, 2025), the selling shareholder's 475,000 share-equivalents represent ~0.53% of outstanding shares, the 75,000 offered represent ~0.084%, and the post-sale 400,000 represent ~0.45%. The company will not receive proceeds from resale by the selling shareholder, but may receive up to $388,717.50 if the Second Amendment warrants are exercised in full; proceeds would be used for working capital and general corporate purposes. The registration permits resale of the shares by the selling shareholder; the filing indicates a resale/secondary offering intent (passive resale) rather than an issuer primary sale.
