Summary

Cloudastructure, Inc. (NASDAQ: CSAI) announced commercial deployment of its first solar-powered security enclosure across multiple critical infrastructure sites in Maryland, Ohio, and Illinois. The off-grid enclosure — featuring a 12-foot solar mast, dual high-output solar panels, integrated battery storage, dome cameras, and real-time audio deterrence — is powered by Cloudastructure's cloud-based AI video surveillance platform and provides continuous real-time monitoring, automated threat detection, and live remote guarding without grid power. Initial deployments secure large-scale commercial solar fields and industrial rooftops for enterprise energy operators and Fortune 500 customers. Company highlights expansion of total addressable market into off-grid critical infrastructure, recurring SaaS revenue potential, hardware-agnostic platform scalability, and multi-state commercial rollouts. The release includes forward-looking statements and associated risk disclosures.
