Summary

Rapid7 (NASDAQ: RPD) issued a press release announcing winners of its 2026 Partner of the Year Awards, recognizing 28 partner organizations across 12 categories and four geographic regions (North America, Latin America, EMEA, APJ). Key named partners include CDW (North America Partner of the Year), SHI, Carahsoft, Softcat, and regional winners such as Netconn, Saepio, and Orro. The release emphasizes the role of partners in driving go-to-market execution, customer retention, and adoption of Rapid7’s AI-powered Command Platform and managed detection and response services; no financial impact or partner-driven revenue metrics were disclosed.

Positives
  • Public recognition of a broad partner ecosystem across four regions (North America, Latin America, EMEA, APJ)
  • Awards include major channel partners (e.g., CDW, SHI, Carahsoft, Softcat) that can drive enterprise sales and retention
  • Reinforces go-to-market and channel strategy, which can support recurring revenue and customer expansion
  • Highlights Rapid7’s positioning as an AI-powered MDR and unified security platform with over 11,500 customers
Negatives
  • No quantitative disclosure of partner-driven revenue, bookings, or pipeline impact
  • Press release is primarily promotional and does not provide guidance or operational or financial detail
  • Awards alone may have limited near-term material impact on financial results
