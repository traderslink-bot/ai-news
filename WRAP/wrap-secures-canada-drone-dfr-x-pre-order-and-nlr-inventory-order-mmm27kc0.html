Summary

Wrap Technologies, Inc. (NASDAQ: WRAP) announced that Canadian distributor Professional Security Products Corporation (PSP Corp) placed an inventory purchase order including BolaWrap devices, WrapVision body-worn cameras, and a pre-order for Wrap's DFR-X drone platform. PSP will hold inventory in Canada to accelerate procurement, demos, and evaluations for Canadian law enforcement and reseller partners. The move is presented as supporting expansion of Wrap's non-lethal response (NLR) ecosystem in Canada; no financial terms or order quantities were disclosed.
