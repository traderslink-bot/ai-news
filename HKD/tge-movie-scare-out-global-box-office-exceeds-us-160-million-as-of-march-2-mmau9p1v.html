Summary

Press release from AMTD Group/AMTD IDEA Group (NYSE: AMTD; SGX: HKB), AMTD Digital Inc. (NYSE: HKD) and The Generation Essentials Group (TGE; NYSE & LSE: TGE) reporting that TGE's film "Scare Out" has generated over US$160 million in global box office within two weeks (as of March 2, 2026). The film has amassed ~12.3 billion views across social media (6.2B Douyin, 5.6B Weibo), has been released in 13 countries/regions including the U.S., U.K., Canada, Australia, Singapore and Hong Kong/Macau, and TGE's films have a cumulative worldwide box office of approximately US$720 million to date. The release reiterates TGE's goal to exceed US$1 billion cumulative box office and includes a Safe Harbor statement regarding forward-looking statements.
