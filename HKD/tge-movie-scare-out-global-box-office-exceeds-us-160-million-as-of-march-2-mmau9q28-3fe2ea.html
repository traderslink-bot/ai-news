Summary

TGE's film Scare Out has grossed over US$160 million worldwide in two weeks. The release generated approximately 12.3 billion views across major social media platforms, including about 6.2 billion on Douyin and 5.6 billion on Weibo. The film has been released in 13 countries and regions outside mainland China. Cumulative box office for films produced by TGE is approximately US$720 million, with a target to exceed US$1 billion. The announcement comes from AMTD Group, AMTD IDEA Group, AMTD Digital and The Generation Essentials Group.
