Summary

PTL Limited announced a 1-for-80 share consolidation of Class A and Class B ordinary shares to regain Nasdaq compliance. The market effective date is February 27, 2026. Class A will trade under the same ticker PTLE with a new CUSIP G7377S127. Pre-consolidation outstanding: 491,237,500 Class A and 11,250,000 Class B. Post-consolidation outstanding: 6,140,469 Class A and 140,625 Class B. No fractional shares will be issued; fractional shares will be rounded up to the next whole share. The consolidation was approved by the board on February 11, 2026 and by shareholders on June 16, 2025. Purpose: to regain Nasdaq Marketplace Rule 5550(a)(2) compliance and maintain listing.

Positives
  • Regains Nasdaq compliance and maintains listing on the Nasdaq Capital Market.
  • No fractional shares will be issued; rounding is up to the next whole share, per policy.
  • New CUSIP assigned (G7377S127) and continued trading under PTLE.
  • Board and shareholder approvals completed prior to the market effective date.
  • Significant reduction in outstanding shares post-consolidation (potential listing metric improvement).
Negatives
  • Large reverse stock split (1-for-80) reduces outstanding shares dramatically, which may impact liquidity.
  • Rounding up fractional shares increases total outstanding shares beyond a perfect 1-for-80 ratio.
  • No guarantee that Nasdaq compliance will be maintained beyond the stated objective.
