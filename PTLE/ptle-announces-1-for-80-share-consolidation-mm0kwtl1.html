Summary

PTL Limited (NASDAQ: PTLE) announced a 1-for-80 share consolidation of its Class A and Class B ordinary shares, effective at the open of trading on February 27, 2026 (market effective date). Pre-consolidation outstanding shares are 491,237,500 Class A and 11,250,000 Class B. Post-consolidation outstanding shares will be 6,140,469 Class A and 140,625 Class B. The Company will continue to trade under the symbol PTLE on the Nasdaq Capital Market with a new CUSIP (G7377S127). Fractional shares will not be issued; any fractional share resulting from the consolidation will be rounded up to the next whole share. The board approved the consolidation on February 11, 2026 and shareholders approved it on June 16, 2025. The stated objective is to regain compliance with Nasdaq Marketplace Rule 5550(a)(2) and maintain the Company’s Nasdaq listing.
