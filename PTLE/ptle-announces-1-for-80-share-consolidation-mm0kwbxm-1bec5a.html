Summary

PTL Limited (PTLE) approved a 1-for-80 share consolidation of its Class A and Class B ordinary shares. The market effective date is February 27, 2026, with post-consolidation trading on Nasdaq Capital Market under the same ticker PTLE and a new CUSIP G7377S127. Before consolidation, there were 491,237,500 Class A shares and 11,250,000 Class B shares outstanding; after consolidation, outstanding will be 6,140,469 Class A shares and 140,625 Class B shares. No fractional shares will be issued; any fractional result will be rounded up to the next whole share. The consolidation was approved by the board on February 11, 2026 and by shareholders on June 16, 2025, and aims to regain Nasdaq Marketplace Rule 5550(a)(2) compliance and maintain listing. The company operates as a bunkering facilitator providing marine fuel logistics services in the Asia Pacific region.

Positives
  • Aims to regain Nasdaq Capital Market compliance under Rule 5550(a)(2) and maintain listing
  • Trading will continue under the same ticker PTLE with a new CUSIP (G7377S127)
  • No fractional shares will be issued; fractional shares will be rounded up to the next whole share
  • Board and shareholder approvals have been obtained (board February 11, 2026; shareholders June 16, 2025)
Negatives
  • Significant reduction in outstanding shares (from 491,237,500 Class A and 11,250,000 Class B to 6,140,469 Class A and 140,625 Class B), which could impact liquidity and market dynamics
  • Post-consolidation share count materially different from pre-consolidation levels may affect per-share metrics and investor perception
  • Reliance on the action to restore or maintain Nasdaq compliance; no guarantee that listing status will be preserved if conditions change
