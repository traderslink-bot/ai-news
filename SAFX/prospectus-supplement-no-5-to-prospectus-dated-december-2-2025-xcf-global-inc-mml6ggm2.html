Summary

SEC filing (Form 424B3 prospectus supplement to Form S-1, Registration No. 333-291020) filed March 10, 2026 that updates XCF Global’s prospectus with information contained in a Form 8-K dated March 10, 2026. The Form 8-K reports results of a March 6, 2026 Special Meeting where shareholders approved the issuance of 19.99% or more of the Company’s common stock (as of January 26, 2026) to a single investor pursuant to a private placement (Nasdaq Rules 5635(d) and 5635(b)). As of the record date there were 232,673,544 shares outstanding and 162,336,821 shares (69.77%) were present or represented by proxy. Voting on Proposal 1: For 159,944,874; Against 2,337,496; Abstain 54,451. The prospectus supplement references up to 72,463,768 shares of Class A common stock. Under a binding term sheet, EEME Energy SPV I LLC previously purchased 38,000,000 XCF shares for $3,800,000 and has committed to acquire an additional 62,000,000 shares (total committed 100,000,000 shares) for $6,200,000 to be funded in two tranches (week of March 7 and week of March 31, 2026); shareholder approval removes a prior 19.99% share cap that had limited EEME’s purchases. The filing also furnishes a press release (Exhibit 99.1) disclosing that XCF has begun plant conversion work (New Rise Renewables Reno) including procurement of a hydrotreating catalyst (Axens’ Vegan technology) to produce ASTM D7566-compliant SAF. The filing notes the contemplated business combination among XCF, DevvStream Corp., Southern Energy Renewables, Inc., and EEME and states the parties expect to file a Form S-4 (proxy statement/prospectus) in connection with the proposed transaction. The filing includes forward-looking risk disclosures, including possible failure to complete the transaction, need for additional capital, and Nasdaq continued listing risk (minimum bid price).
