Summary

Capstone Holding Corp., NASDAQ: CAPS, announced multiple new homebuilder engagements across the U.S. and Canada representing more than 300,000 square feet of annualized demand, delivering immediate revenue and EBITDA contributions and accelerating the transition to positive EBITDA and free cash flow. Projects include Lennar, Jayman Homes (Alberta), Marrano Homes (Buffalo, NY), Adams Homes in the Southeast, a large multifamily development in Madison, Wisconsin, and other customers; several programs are already in production with shipments underway. Capstone leverages its Instone platform to optimize supply chains across 38 states and Canada and anticipates publishing an Earnings Power Presentation in Q1 2026.
