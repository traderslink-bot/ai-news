Summary

Greenlane Holdings reports holdings of approximately 70.4 million BERA as of February 27, 2026. Approximately 50 million BERA have been deployed into validator infrastructure. Between December 4, 2025 and February 27, 2026, Greenlane purchased about 9 million BERA at prices ranging from $0.40 to $0.93. In January 2026, Greenlane announced plans to deploy up to 50 million BERA across validators, including ~20 million across two validators and up to 30 million via a partnership with Infrared Finance; as of February 27, 2026, about 50 million units were deployed. Staking activity includes substantial deployment into PoL staking and validator participation, with an approximate 25% annualized PoL staking rate as of February 27, 2026. Jason Hitchcock was appointed Chief Executive Officer on February 18, 2026 to advance the company’s digital asset treasury strategy.
