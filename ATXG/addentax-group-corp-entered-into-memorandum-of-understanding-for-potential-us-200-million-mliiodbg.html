Summary

Addentax Group Corp. (Nasdaq: ATXG) announced non-binding MOUs dated Feb 9, 2026 with two institutional investors backed by a Middle Eastern royal family to explore a potential strategic equity investment of up to US$200 million (up to US$100 million per investor). Each investor intends to acquire newly issued common shares at a proposed fixed price of US$1.50 per share; definitive agreements are targeted to be negotiated and executed within six months and the transaction is subject to due diligence, corporate, regulatory and stock exchange approvals. The parties are also discussing strategic collaboration to advance ATXG's AI-driven financial technology and cryptocurrency financial services, with potential uses of proceeds including scaling platforms, expanding compliant crypto services, Hong Kong/APAC market penetration, and R&D investment. The company cautions there is no assurance the transaction will be completed.

Positives
  • Potential up to US$200 million equity commitment to materially strengthen balance sheet
  • Proposed fixed share price of US$1.50 provides pricing clarity in preliminary talks
  • Investors reportedly bring industry resources, regulatory insights and global market experience
  • Planned strategic collaboration could accelerate ATXG's AI and cryptocurrency product development and APAC expansion
Negatives
  • MOUs are non-binding and completion is subject to due diligence, definitive agreements and multiple approvals
  • No assurance the investment or strategic collaboration will be consummated
  • New issuance of shares would dilute existing shareholders if transaction proceeds
  • Transaction terms, timing and regulatory approvals remain uncertain (definitive agreements to be negotiated within six months)
