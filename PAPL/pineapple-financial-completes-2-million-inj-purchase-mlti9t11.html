Summary

Pineapple Financial Inc. (NYSE: PAPL) announced a $2.0 million open-market purchase of 560,647 Injective (INJ) tokens at a weighted average price of $3.567 per token as part of its ongoing digital asset treasury strategy. The company reports $20.79 million in capital reserves available for future INJ purchases and states its combined treasury is currently valued at $41.89 million versus an approximate market capitalization of $18 million. Pineapple provides a public Digital Asset Treasury Dashboard for real-time holdings and purchase history data.

Positives
  • Completed $2.0M acquisition increases the company’s INJ token holdings (560,647 INJ).
  • Company reports $20.79M in capital reserves available for further open-market purchases.
  • Public real-time Digital Asset Treasury Dashboard increases transparency of holdings and purchases.
Negatives
  • Concentrated exposure to a single digital asset (INJ) subjects the treasury to INJ price volatility.
  • Company discloses potential for impairment charges if INJ market price falls below carrying value.
  • Forward-looking statements caution that expectations may not be realized and results could differ materially.
