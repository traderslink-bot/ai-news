Summary

Pineapple Financial Inc. announced an operational reset to lower costs and accelerate growth using artificial intelligence, targeting more than $2.5 million in annual operating expense reductions as part of its 2026 Core Mortgage Platform strategy. The plan combines permanent cost reductions, AI-driven automation, and a technology-enabled recruitment initiative, with headcount reduced by more than 60% and monthly cash burn cut by over 50%. An AI-enabled recruitment platform aims to engage more than 40,000 mortgage agents nationwide.

Positives
  • Targeting more than $2.5 million in annual operating expense reductions.
  • Approximately $1.33 million in annual savings achieved to date.
  • Headcount reduction of more than 60% lowers operating costs.
  • Monthly cash burn reduced by over 50%.
  • AI-driven automation across core operations expected to improve efficiency.
  • AI-enabled recruitment platform to expand the mortgage agent network (targeting >40,000 agents).
  • Plan supports higher EBITDA margins and near-term cash-flow breakeven under the 2026 Core Mortgage Platform strategy.
Negatives
  • Large workforce reductions may impact operations and morale.
  • Execution risk associated with AI deployment and automation.
  • Uncertain impact on revenue growth and profitability in the near term.
