Summary

Pineapple Financial Inc. announced an operational reset to lower costs and accelerate growth using artificial intelligence, aligning with its 2026 Core Mortgage Platform strategy. The restructuring aims to reduce annual operating expenses by more than $2.5 million, with about $1.33 million in annual savings already achieved and expected to be reflected in the run rate by March 31, 2026. The plan includes a workforce realignment reducing headcount by more than 60% and cuts across software, marketing, and professional services, which has cut monthly cash burn by more than 50%. AI integration across core operations aims to replace manual workflows, improve processing speed and accuracy, and provide round-the-clock engagement. An AI-enabled recruitment platform will target more than 40,000 mortgage agents nationwide. Shares were up 0.85% in premarket trading to $0.65.

Positives
  • Annual operating expenses expected to be reduced by more than $2.5 million through restructuring.
  • Approximately $1.33 million in annual savings already achieved, anticipated to be reflected in run rate by March 31, 2026.
  • AI integration across core operations aims to lower costs, improve processing speed and accuracy, and provide 24/7 engagement.
  • AI-enabled recruitment platform to expand the mortgage agent network, targeting more than 40,000 agents nationwide.
  • Plan aims for higher EBITDA margins and near-term cash-flow breakeven with scalable operations.
Negatives
  • Workforce realignment reduces headcount by more than 60%, which may impact operations and morale.
  • Significant cuts to software, marketing, and professional services could affect capabilities if not offset by productivity gains.
