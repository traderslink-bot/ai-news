Summary

Press release from Nauticus Robotics, Inc. (NASDAQ: KITT) announcing senior leadership appointments and commercialization initiatives to scale autonomy software and Aquanaut vehicle production. Appointments include Dr. Kjerstin "Kj" Easton as Vice President of Software, Chuck Claunch as Chief Software Architect & Principal Software Engineer, Jason Close as Vice President of Growth & Go‑To‑Market (transitioned into the role), and continuation of Steve Walsh as Vice President of Sales. The company states Master Investment Group committed to support building additional Aquanaut vehicles to expand manufacturing capacity and enable increased deployments across commercial energy and defense markets. Nauticus highlights focus on scaling its Nauticus ToolKITT autonomy platform and driving product-led revenue growth; no financial terms or specific production/timing details were disclosed.
