Summary

Bridger Aerospace (NASDAQ: BAER, BAERW) announced a five-year multiple-award Indefinite Delivery Indefinite Quantity (IDIQ) contract to provide on-call fixed-wing transportation services in Alaska for the U.S. Department of the Interior and other federal agencies. The contract runs April 1, 2026 through March 2031. The award value is estimated at $18.6 million (not a guarantee of future revenue). Management said the award is expected to increase utilization of Bridger's light fixed-wing fleet.
