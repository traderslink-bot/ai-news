Summary

Bridger Aerospace Group Holdings, Inc. announced a five-year multiple-award IDIQ contract for on-call fixed-wing transportation services in Alaska to support the U.S. Department of the Interior and other federal agencies. The contract runs from April 1, 2026, to March 2031, with an award value of $18.6 million (estimate). The deal could increase fleet utilization, but revenue is not guaranteed as the amount is an estimate and depends on future demand.
