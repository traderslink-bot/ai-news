Summary

Glancy Prongay Wolke & Rotter LLP announced it is investigating Kyndryl Holdings, Inc. (NYSE: KD) for possible violations of federal securities laws after Kyndryl disclosed on February 9, 2026 that its CFO and General Counsel departed effective immediately, the company is reviewing cash-management disclosures and internal controls, and it received voluntary document requests from the SEC’s Division of Enforcement. Kyndryl said it anticipates reporting material weaknesses in its internal control over financial reporting. On that news KD shares fell $12.90 (54.9%) to close at $10.59. The law firm is soliciting investors who lost money to contact them and notes potential SEC whistleblower rewards.
