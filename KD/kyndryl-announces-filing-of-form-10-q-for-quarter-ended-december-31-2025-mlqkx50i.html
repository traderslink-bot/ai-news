Summary

Kyndryl (NYSE: KD) issued a press release stating it filed its Quarterly Report on Form 10-Q for the quarter ended December 31, 2025, and filed amendments to its Form 10-K for fiscal year ended March 31, 2025 and to Forms 10-Q for the quarters ended June 30, 2025 and September 30, 2025. The company reported no restatement of current or previously reported financial statements. Filings disclosed material weaknesses in internal control over financial reporting related to disclosure processes, including certain cash management practices (deferring vendor payments quarter to quarter). Kyndryl disclosed a remediation plan and said its financial position remains strong.
