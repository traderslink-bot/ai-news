Summary

Bimergen Energy announced the acquisition of eight late-stage 9.9 MW distributed generation battery energy storage projects totaling 79.2 MW in ERCOT South from Aggreko's IPP Solutions, financed through a joint venture with RelyEZ and using RelyEZ lithium-based batteries. Five projects are expected to achieve ISD in late 2026 and three in early 2027. Construction contracts will be awarded soon as the portfolio moves toward build and commercial operation.
