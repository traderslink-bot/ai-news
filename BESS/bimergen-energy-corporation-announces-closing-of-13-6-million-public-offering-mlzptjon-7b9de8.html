Summary

Bimergen Energy Corporation announced the closing of a public offering of common stock or pre-funded warrants at $4.00 per share, for gross proceeds of $13.6 million. The underwriters have a 45-day option to purchase up to 200,000 additional shares/warrants and 200,000 warrants for over-allotments. The offering included 200,000 warrants in connection with partial exercise of the over-allotment option. Proceeds are intended for BESS project development and working capital. ThinkEquity acted as sole book-running manager. The offering is being conducted through a registration statement on Form S-1 (File No. 333-280668) effective January 29, 2026, and is being made only by a prospectus.
