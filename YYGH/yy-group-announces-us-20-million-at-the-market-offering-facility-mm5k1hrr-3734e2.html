Summary

YY Group Holding Limited entered into an At-The-Market Sales Agreement with Spartan Capital Securities LLC and Wilson-Davis & Co., Inc. to offer and sell up to US$20,000,000 of its Class A Ordinary Shares from time to time at market prices, through Nasdaq Capital Market or other trading venues. The company may also conduct private negotiated or block transactions. The offering is discretionary and can be suspended or terminated. Proceeds may be used to repay approximately US$0.4 million of outstanding debt and for general corporate purposes, including diversification, development initiatives and capital expenditures. The offering is pursuant to a shelf registration on Form F-3 (File No. 333-286705) filed April 23, 2025 and effective April 30, 2025; a prospectus supplement will be filed. Lead agent is Spartan Capital; co-agent Wilson-Davis & Co. The company is Singapore-headquartered and Nasdaq-listed, providing flexible workforce solutions and IFM across regions.
