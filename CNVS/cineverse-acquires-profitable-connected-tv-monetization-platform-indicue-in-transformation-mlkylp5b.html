Summary

Press release: Cineverse Corp. (NASDAQ: CNVS) announced the acquisition of IndiCue, Inc., a profitable connected TV (CTV) advertising monetization platform, to integrate its ad tech into Cineverse's Matchpoint platform. IndiCue is expected to generate approximately $38.0 million of revenue and $9.6 million of EBITDA in calendar year 2026 (~25% EBITDA margin) and has ~40 live clients with ~75 additional publishers onboarding. Cineverse expects fiscal 2027 revenue of $115–$120 million with technology platforms >50% of revenue and adjusted EBITDA of $10–$20 million (FY2027 commencing April 1, 2026). Transaction consideration totals up to $40.0 million (including $22.0 million base and up to $18.0 million in performance-based earnouts). Cineverse raised $13.0 million in convertible notes from existing long-term shareholders to support closing and working capital. IndiCue founders and senior leadership join Cineverse under multi-year employment/retention agreements.
