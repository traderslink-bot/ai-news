Summary

Cineverse Corp. (Nasdaq: CNVS) announced a proposed underwritten public offering of its Class A common stock. The Benchmark Company, LLC will act as sole underwriter, and Cineverse intends to grant a 30-day option to the underwriter to purchase up to an additional 15% of the shares. The offering is subject to market conditions; a shelf registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-273098) became effective January 25, 2024, and a preliminary prospectus supplement will be filed with the SEC.

Positives
  • Company has an effective S-3 shelf registration (File No. 333-273098) enabling an offering
  • Sole underwriter named (The Benchmark Company, LLC) providing a clear distribution agent
  • Underwriter option to purchase up to an additional 15% of shares provides execution flexibility
Negatives
  • Offering is subject to market conditions with no assurance it will be completed
  • Potential dilution to existing shareholders if offering is executed
  • Size and terms of the offering have not been determined yet
