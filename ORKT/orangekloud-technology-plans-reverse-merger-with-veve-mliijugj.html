Summary

Orangekloud Technology Inc. (NASDAQ: ORKT) announced a non-binding Letter of Intent to negotiate a reverse merger acquisition of Orbis Technology Limited's flagship consumer brand and marketplace, VeVe. The proposed transaction contemplates Orangekloud issuing shares privately to acquire the entire issued share capital and undertaking of Orbis. After the transaction, Orbis co-founders/shareholders would collectively hold a majority of Orangekloud's issued shares and existing Orangekloud shareholders would hold a minority stake; the company expects to retain its dual-class share structure. Parties aim to finalize terms and move to a definitive implementation agreement on or around February 28, 2026. The release reiterates forward-looking statement cautions.

Positives
  • Announcement targets acquisition of VeVe, a consumer-facing marketplace and IP infrastructure platform (Orbis's flagship brand).
  • Transaction intended to add digital IP ingestion, rights management, marketplace infrastructure and secondary-market monetisation capabilities to Orangekloud.
  • Company plans to proceed toward a definitive implementation agreement with a targeted timeline (on or around Feb 28, 2026).
Negatives
  • Agreement is a non-binding Letter of Intent; no definitive agreement or closed transaction yet.
  • Proposed transaction would result in Orbis founders/shareholders collectively owning a majority and existing Orangekloud shareholders becoming minority holders (significant ownership dilution/shift).
  • Forward-looking statements and risks noted; completion depends on final terms and other customary conditions.
