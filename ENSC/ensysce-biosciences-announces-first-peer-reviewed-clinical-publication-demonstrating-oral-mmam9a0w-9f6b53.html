Summary

Ensysce Biosciences published Phase 1 clinical data describing PF614-MPAR, an oxycodone prodrug with MPAR overdose protection, in the Journal of Opioid Management. Data show PF614-MPAR maintains therapeutic opioid exposure under normal dosing and minimizes increases in opioid delivery with supratherapeutic dosing. MPAR technology is described as providing overdose protection and had FDA Breakthrough Therapy Designation in January 2024. The company notes ongoing clinical development including a second trial, continued support from the National Institute on Drug Abuse, and plans to expand MPAR to other therapies such as amphetamines and methadone. The publication references reductions in overdose risk and a potential new safety paradigm for opioid analgesia.
