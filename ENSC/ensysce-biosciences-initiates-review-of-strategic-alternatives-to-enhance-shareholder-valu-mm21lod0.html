Summary

Ensysce Biosciences (NASDAQ: ENSC) announced that its Board has initiated a formal review of a broad range of strategic alternatives to enhance shareholder value. Potential options include strategic partnerships, licensing, capital formation, business combinations, asset sales or other corporate transactions. The company emphasized there is no assurance the review will result in any transaction and no timetable has been set. Ensysce remains focused on advancing its TAAP™ and MPAR® platforms for safer opioid therapies. The release includes standard forward-looking statements and notes clinical-stage programs are not approved and subject to development and financing risks.
