Summary

Ensysce Biosciences announced the publication of the first peer‑reviewed clinical manuscript describing MPAR (Multi-Pill Abuse Resistance) overdose protection with PF614-MPAR, an oxycodone prodrug. The Phase 1 study shows PF614-MPAR maintains therapeutic opioid exposure under normal dosing while limiting opioid release with supratherapeutic doses. The work supports a potential safety paradigm in opioid analgesia. MPAR had received FDA Breakthrough Therapy Designation in January 2024. The company expects ongoing second clinical trial and is pursuing MPAR across other categories, with NIH/NIDA support for the research.
