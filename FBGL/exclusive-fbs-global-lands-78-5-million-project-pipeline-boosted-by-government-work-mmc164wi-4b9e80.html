Summary

FBS Global Limited (NASDAQ: FBGL), a Singapore-based green building contractor and interior fit-out specialist, announced a $78.5 million project pipeline covering eight public and commercial infrastructure contracts, including about $34.6 million allocated to government infrastructure projects where the company acts as a subcontractor. The scope includes specialized installations and retrofit/fit-out work across government and private-sector projects. CEO Kelvin Ang said the pipeline demonstrates the company’s technical capabilities and disciplined execution, supporting long-term growth. The company also regained Nasdaq minimum bid price compliance, closing at or above $1 for 10 consecutive trading days between February 12 and 26. In premarket trading, FBGL was down about 3.54% to around $0.75.
