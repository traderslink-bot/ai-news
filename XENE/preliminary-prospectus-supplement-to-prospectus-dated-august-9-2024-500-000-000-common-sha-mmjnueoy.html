Summary

SEC filing (Form 424B5) by Xenon Pharmaceuticals Inc. (Nasdaq: XENE). Xenon filed a preliminary prospectus supplement for an underwritten offering of $500.0 million of common shares (with a 30-day underwriter option for an additional $75.0 million). Common shares trade on Nasdaq under ticker XENE (last reported sale $41.94 on March 6, 2026). The filing discloses 80,010,790 common shares outstanding as of December 31, 2025 and lists outstanding equity instruments (stock options, RSUs/PSUs, pre-funded warrants and shares reserved under equity plans and the ATM program). Use of proceeds: advance clinical development, pre-commercial/commercial activities, discovery/pre-clinical work and general corporate purposes. Joint book-running managers include J.P. Morgan, Jefferies, TD Cowen, Stifel, RBC Capital Markets and William Blair; Baird is lead manager. The prospectus supplement incorporates recent corporate developments: Xenon announced positive topline Phase 3 X-TOLE2 results for azetukalner in focal onset seizures (met primary endpoint: MPC in monthly focal seizure frequency at Week 12 for 25 mg and 15 mg vs placebo; placebo-adjusted MPC for 25 mg = -42.7%; responder (≥50%) rate for 25 mg = 54.8%), reported safety/tolerability consistent with prior studies, and plans to submit an NDA to FDA for focal onset seizures in Q3 2026. Company states cash, cash equivalents and marketable securities plus $130.0 million raised under ATM since 12/31/2025 expected to fund operations into H2 2027. The filing repeatedly discloses offering risks, dilution, and typical lock-up and underwriting arrangements.
