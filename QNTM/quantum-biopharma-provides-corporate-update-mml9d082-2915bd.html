Summary

Quantum BioPharma Ltd. announced it will report 2025 financial and operational results after market close on March 24, 2026. It disclosed a non-brokered private placement of up to 4,000 convertible debenture units at CAD 1,000 per unit, with each unit including a secured CAD 1,000 debenture and 250 warrants exercisable for 250 Class B shares. Debentures mature in 24 months, bear 1.25% interest per month, and are convertible at CAD 4.00 per share; warrants are exercisable at CAD 5.00 for five years. Proceeds are for development and working capital. The company also plans a debt settlement with creditors and insiders by issuing shares at the conversion price, a related-party transaction under MI 61-101. Securities are not registered in the United States. Background: the company focuses on neurodegenerative and metabolic disorders, with Lucid-MS under Lucid Psycheceuticals and royalty arrangements with Celly Nutrition.

  • Lead asset Lucid-MS under Lucid Psycheceuticals with patent protection in development
  • Significant tax loss carryforward (~CAD 130 million) to offset future profits
  • Royalty structure with Celly Nutrition (7% of sales to CAD 250 million, then 3% thereafter) and 25.71% stake retained in Celly Nutrition
