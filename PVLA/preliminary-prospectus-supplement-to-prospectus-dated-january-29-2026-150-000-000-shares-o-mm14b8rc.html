Summary

Form 424B5 preliminary prospectus supplement filed by Palvella Therapeutics, Inc. for an underwritten public offering of up to $150.0 million of common stock (or, in lieu of shares to certain investors, pre-funded warrants exercisable at $0.001 per share). The offering includes a 30-day underwriter option to purchase up to an additional $22.5 million of common stock. Pre-funded warrants do not expire, are exercisable immediately subject to ownership limits (default 4.99%, optional election to 9.99%), and are not expected to be listed. Palvella's common stock trades on Nasdaq under PVLA (last reported sale $87.84 on Feb 23, 2026 per the filing). The filing discloses pro forma capitalization inputs (11,088,329 shares outstanding as of Sept 30, 2025), outstanding option and warrant pools (see details below), and estimated unaudited cash, cash equivalents and marketable securities of approximately $55.9 million as of Dec 31, 2025. The prospectus supplement also summarizes recent clinical developments (positive Phase 3 SELVA topline results for QTORIN rapamycin and positive Phase 2 TOIVA results), intended use of proceeds (development of QTORIN programs and general corporate purposes), underwriting arrangements and lock-up provisions, and risk factors (including dilution and limited liquidity for pre-funded warrants).
