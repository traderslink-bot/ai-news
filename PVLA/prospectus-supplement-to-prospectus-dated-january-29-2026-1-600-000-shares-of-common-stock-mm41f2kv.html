Summary

This is a Form 424(b)(5) prospectus supplement filed by Palvella Therapeutics, Inc. (Nasdaq: PVLA) for a registered underwritten offering of 1,600,000 shares of common stock at $125.00 per share (gross proceeds $200.0M). The underwriters have a 30-day option to purchase up to an additional 240,000 shares. Net proceeds to Palvella are estimated at approximately $187.3M (or $215.5M if option exercised in full) before expenses. The company intends to use proceeds to fund development of its QTORIN platform programs (including QTORIN 3.9% rapamycin and QTORIN pitavastatin), working capital and general corporate purposes. The filing summarizes recent clinical updates: positive topline Phase 3 SELVA results for QTORIN rapamycin in microcystic lymphatic malformations (SELVA: ITT N=49, primary endpoint mLM-IGA mean change +2.13, p<0.001, key secondary and all additional secondary endpoints p<0.001) and positive Phase 2 TOIVA results for cutaneous venous malformations (TOIVA: improvements on multiple clinician- and patient-reported endpoints at Week 12). Preliminary unaudited cash, cash equivalents and marketable securities were estimated at $55.9M as of December 31, 2025. Post-offering outstanding shares are projected at 12,688,329 (12,928,329 if option exercised). The prospectus discloses outstanding options, convertible preferred conversions and pre-funded warrants that may further dilute shareholders and provides dilution calculations (pro forma as-adjusted net tangible book value $16.16/share, immediate dilution to new investors $108.84/share). Certain directors indicated non-binding interest in purchasing in the offering.
