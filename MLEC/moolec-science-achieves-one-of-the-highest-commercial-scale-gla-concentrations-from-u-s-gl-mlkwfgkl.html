Summary

Moolec Science (NASDAQ: MLEC) announced that crushing of its 2025 U.S. GLASO1 safflower campaign confirmed approximately 45% gamma-linolenic acid (GLA) concentration at commercial scale. The campaign covered ~1,100 acres with average yields of ~2,200 lbs/acre (up from ~1,400 lbs/acre in 2024, a 57% YoY increase), and the company's report states the product is compatible with existing U.S. agricultural and processing infrastructure. Moolec describes the results as validation of the GLASO1 platform's commercial-scale performance and positions the company to pursue disciplined growth and conversion of this operational achievement into recurring revenue. The release contains forward-looking statements and standard risk disclosures.

Positives
