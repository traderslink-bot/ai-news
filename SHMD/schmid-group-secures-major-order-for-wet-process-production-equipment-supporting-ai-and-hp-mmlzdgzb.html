Summary

SCHMID Group (NASDAQ: SHMD) announced a lower two-digit million-USD purchase order from a major Chinese high-end PCB manufacturer for multiple wet-process systems to equip a new HDI multilayer production line. The systems (InfinityLine H+ and InfinityLine V+) are designed for high-layer-count HDI boards used in AI server boards and high-performance computing platforms. Equipment will be installed in the customer's next-generation production facility and targets growing demand from hyperscale, data center and AI infrastructure markets. The company did not disclose customer identity, precise order value, or delivery timeline.
