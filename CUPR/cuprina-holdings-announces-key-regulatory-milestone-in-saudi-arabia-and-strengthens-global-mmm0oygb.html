Summary

Press release: Cuprina Holdings (NASDAQ: CUPR) announced that its 49%-owned associate, Cuprina MENA Co. Ltd., received product classification from the Saudi Food and Drug Authority (SFDA) for MEDIFLY as a Medical Device–Drug combination with primary mode of action regulated under the medical device framework, enabling finalization of requirements for commercial distribution in Saudi Arabia and the MENA region. The company also appointed Dr. Enming Yong to its Medical and Scientific Advisory Board to provide scientific governance and clinical oversight, and reported recent clinical engagement activities in Hong Kong and Singapore and its inaugural advisory board meeting in March 2026.
