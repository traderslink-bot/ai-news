Summary

New Pacific Metals Corp. signed a Framework Agreement for Cooperation and Coordination with the Carangas community (TIOC Carangas) regarding the Carangas silver-gold project in Oruro, Bolivia. The agreement establishes a framework of understanding with commitments by the company to respect indigenous land rights, environmental compliance, social development, local sourcing, and a community development fund; and by the Carangas community to participate in consultations with Bolivian authorities and to support exploration, permitting, construction, and production. With the framework in place, the company aims to complete prior consultation, convert exploration licenses to mining permits, and begin a feasibility study, including a 2026 drilling campaign of over 30,000 metres. The project is expected to provide economic and social benefits to Bolivia, the Oruro Department, TIOC Carangas, and shareholders.

Positives
  • Framework agreement signed establishing a formal cooperation framework for the Carangas Project
  • Commitment to respect property rights and Bolivian laws, including environmental compliance
  • Commitment to social and economic development initiatives and a community development fund
  • Plan to source services and supplies locally where possible
  • Carangas community commitments to participate in consultations and support exploration, permitting, and production
  • Intention to advance toward converting exploration licenses to mining permits and to commence a feasibility study
  • 2026 drilling campaign planned to exceed 30,000 metres
Negatives
  • Achievement of project milestones depends on Bolivian regulatory approvals and permits
  • Requires completion of prior consultation with AJAM and other government bodies and potential legislative approvals for permit conversion
  • Subject to typical regulatory, environmental, political, and community relation risks inherent to mining projects
  • Forward-looking elements depend on successful execution of regulatory processes and social licenses
