Summary

ReTo Eco-Solutions, Inc. (Nasdaq: RETO) announced it closed the acquisition of a 51% equity interest in Seven Arrows Supply Chain Limited (BVI) through a share exchange dated February 27, 2026. ReTo issued 8,670,000 Class A shares to the seller at $1.00 per share (total consideration $8,670,000). Seven Arrows, via ReTo's wholly owned subsidiary Hainan Seven Arrows Supply Chain Co., Ltd., operates craft beer supply chain management and distribution in China. ReTo plans to deploy its smart craft beer vending machines through Seven Arrows' network, expecting to expand beverage sales, accelerate machine rollout, and achieve procurement, logistics and channel synergies. The release includes customary forward-looking risk disclosures related to integration, synergies, market and regulatory uncertainties.
