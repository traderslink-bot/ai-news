Summary

ReTo Eco-Solutions, Inc. has closed on acquiring 51% equity interest in Seven Arrows Supply Chain Limited, a British Virgin Islands exempted company engaged in craft beer supply chain management and distribution in China. The acquisition was completed via a share exchange dated February 27, 2026, with ReTo issuing 8,670,000 Class A shares at $1.00 per share for a total value of $8,670,000. Through its subsidiary Hainan Seven Arrows Supply Chain Co., Ltd., Seven Arrows will deploy ReTo's proprietary smart craft beer vending machines across Chinese cities, leveraging Seven Arrows' distribution network. The deal aims to expand ReTo's footprint in the intelligent beer vending equipment sector, support cash flow growth from expanded craft beer and other beverage sales, and create operational synergies in procurement, logistics, and channel access. CEO Xinyang Li described the closing as a milestone in focusing on high-growth, cash-generating businesses and noted that Seven Arrows' offline network complements the vending machine business.

Positives
  • Expands footprint in the intelligent beer vending equipment sector.
  • Deployment of proprietary smart craft beer vending machines through Seven Arrows' distribution network across China.
  • Potential cash flow growth from expanded craft beer and other beverage sales.
  • Accelerated rollout of ReTo's proprietary vending machines.
  • Enhanced market reach and synergies across procurement, logistics, and channel access.
  • Seven Arrows provides an established offline network and supply chain capabilities.
Negatives

  • Potential impact of market conditions and regulatory developments on expected cash flow.
  • Operational challenges in implementing the rollout of vending machines.
