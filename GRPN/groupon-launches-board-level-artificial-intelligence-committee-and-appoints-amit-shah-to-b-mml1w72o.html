Summary

Groupon (NASDAQ: GRPN) announced on March 10, 2026 the appointment of Amit Shah to its Board of Directors effective the same day and the formation of a Board-level Artificial Intelligence Committee, which Shah will chair. The Board now has six members; Shah's director term expires at Groupon's 2026 annual meeting, when he will be nominated for election. The AI Committee will provide board oversight of AI strategy with mandates covering capital allocation, responsible AI practices, technical resilience (model risk, cybersecurity, vendor concentration), and organizational readiness.
