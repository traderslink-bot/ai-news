Summary

BIO-key International, Inc. (BKYI) and RunLevel announced a multi-year strategic agreement to support SIMO, operator of Mozambique's national electronic payments network, with PortalGuard IAM, PIN:You tokenless authentication, and Single Sign-On integrated into SIMO's environment to improve governance, cybersecurity resilience, and secure access for participating institutions. The deployment expands BIO-key's footprint to 11th banking/financial sector customer globally. The project follows a May 2025 joint IAM deployment for a major national bank and aligns with Mozambique's push to modernize its digital financial services amid a population of about 36.6 million.
