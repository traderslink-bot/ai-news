Summary

BIO-key International (NASDAQ: BKYI) announced a multi-year agreement with Sociedade Interbancária de Moçambique (SIMO) to modernize identity and access management across Mozambique's national payments network. Working with regional integrator RunLevel, BIO-key will deploy its PortalGuard IAM platform, PIN:You tokenless authentication, and Single Sign-On to improve governance, cybersecurity resilience, and secure access for institutions on SIMO's SIMOrede network. The deal builds on a prior joint May 2025 deployment for a major national bank and represents BIO-key's 11th banking/financial-sector customer. No financial terms were disclosed. (Press release dated Feb. 24, 2026; Maputo and Holmdel.)
