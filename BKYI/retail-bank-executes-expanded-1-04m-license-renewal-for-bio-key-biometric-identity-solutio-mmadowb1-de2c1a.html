Summary

BIO-key International, Inc. announced a $1.04 million license renewal and expansion for its biometric identity solution with a foreign retail bank client that has used BIO-key since 2015. The renewal value increased 30% versus the prior year, with deployment expanding to more than 30 million enrolled clients and roughly 10,000 bank employees using the fingerprint technology for branch operations and authentication. The system supports about 300 million fingerprints for in-branch identification and KYC verification against national identity systems. BIO-key cited a year-end cash position of $2.7 million and stated it expects to fund growth internally from anticipated cash inflows. Forward-looking statements are included with standard risk disclosures.
