Summary

Livechain, Inc. (OTCX: LICH), a subsidiary of Vyome Holdings, Inc. (NASDAQ: HIND), announced a debt purchase agreement to acquire Humanyze via all-stock consideration valued at approximately $325,000, taking control of Humanyze's assets including IP and trade secrets. The transaction initiates a default and asset transfer to assume operations. Humanyze, originated from MIT Media Lab, provides AI-enabled HR analytics. Vyome is the majority shareholder of Livechain. Livechain plans to pursue AI HR assets and aims for potential uplisting to a national exchange by 2026. Forward-looking statements are disclosed and cautioned.

Positives
  • All-stock transaction preserves cash while transferring Humanyze assets and IP to Livechain
  • Livechain gains direct control of Humanyze's assets and ongoing operations
  • Potential pathway toward uplisting to a national exchange by 2026
  • Vyome remains a majority shareholder, potentially benefiting from increased Livechain value
Negatives
  • Deal value is small (approximately $325,000), which may limit perceived scale and impact
  • Transaction involves a default and asset transfer, introducing execution and integration risk
  • Forward-looking uplisting prospects depend on market, regulatory conditions and company performance
  • Integration of Humanyze assets into Livechain may present operational challenges
