Actelis Networks, Inc. (NASDAQ: ASNS) announced a significant order from a Japanese governmental entity, placed through its Japanese channel partner, to supply dozens of its MetaLight cyber-hardened Ethernet-over-copper units for modernization of civil and defense-adjacent critical infrastructure across Japan (transportation, utilities, public safety). The MetaLight systems provide fiber-grade connectivity over existing copper wiring, enabling rapid deployment without new fiber installation. The company did not disclose dollar value or specific unit count beyond 'dozens.'
