Summary

VisionWave (NASDAQ: VWAV) announced it executed a binding definitive agreement to acquire a 51% controlling interest in C.M. Composite Materials Ltd., an Israeli certified aerospace-grade composite manufacturer that supplies structural components used in systems publicly known as Iron Dome and Barak 8. The transaction is subject to customary closing conditions and regulatory approvals and is expected to close in Q1 2026; consideration for the 51% interest will consist of 250,000 shares of VisionWave common stock (per an 8-K). C.M.'s preliminary, unaudited FY2025 internal results (IFRS) show ~NIS 52M (~$17.3M) revenue and ~NIS 9M (~$3.0M) net income before tax. The deal is presented as complementary to VisionWave’s previously announced $10 million QSpeed™ development Statement of Work; management cites potential production and efficiency synergies but cautions QSpeed™ is under development and integration benefits are unproven.
