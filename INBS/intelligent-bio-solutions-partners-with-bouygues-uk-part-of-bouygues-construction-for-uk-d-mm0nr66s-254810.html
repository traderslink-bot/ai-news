Summary

Intelligent Bio Solutions Inc. (NASDAQ: INBS) announces a partnership with Bouygues UK (subsidiary of Bouygues Construction) to deploy INBS's fingerprint drug screening technology across Bouygues UK's UK operations. The initial rollout covers 13 project sites. The in-house solution is expected to replace third-party testing, supported by ROI analysis indicating cost savings. Bouygues Construction reported €10.3 billion turnover in 2024. The fingerprint system tests for recent use of opiates, cocaine, methamphetamine, and cannabis, with sample collection in seconds and results in under ten minutes. The companies describe the relationship as a collaborative partnership rather than a traditional supplier–customer arrangement. The release contains forward-looking statements and associated risks.
