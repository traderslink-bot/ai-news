Summary

Intelligent Bio Solutions Inc. (NASDAQ: INBS) announced a partnership with Bouygues UK, a subsidiary of Bouygues Construction, to deploy INBS's fingerprint drug screening technology across 13 Bouygues UK project sites in the United Kingdom. The deal shifts from third-party testing to an in-house solution, citing hygiene, efficiency, and cost savings supported by aROI analysis. Bouygues UK described the arrangement as a collaborative partnership rather than a traditional supplier relationship. The rollout aims to improve workplace safety and testing efficiency, with rapid results (under ten minutes) emphasized by the company. The release contains standard forward-looking statements and associated risk disclosures.

Positives
  • In-house fingerprint drug testing rollout across Bouygues UK's UK operations
  • ROI analysis shows substantial cost savings versus prior third-party testing
  • Potential for expansion within Bouygues construction group based on initial deployment
Negatives
