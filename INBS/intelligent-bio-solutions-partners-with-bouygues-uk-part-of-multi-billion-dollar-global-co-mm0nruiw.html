Summary

Intelligent Bio Solutions Inc. (NASDAQ: INBS) announced a partnership with Bouygues UK (a subsidiary of Bouygues Construction) to deploy INBS's fingerprint-based drug screening technology across 13 Bouygues UK project sites. Bouygues Construction reported €10.3 billion in turnover for 2024 and ~35,600 employees. Bouygues UK is moving from third-party testing to an in-house solution after an ROI analysis that the companies say shows substantial cost savings; INBS highlights hygiene, employee experience, time efficiency and results in under ten minutes. No contract value or definitive expansion commitments were disclosed.
