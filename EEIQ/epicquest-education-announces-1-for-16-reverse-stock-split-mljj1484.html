Summary

EpicQuest Education Group International Limited (NASDAQ: EEIQ) announced a 1-for-16 reverse stock split of its ordinary shares effective February 17, 2026. The company will amend its charter to reduce authorized ordinary shares from 970,000,000 to 60,625,000 and authorized preferred shares from 10,000,000 to 625,000, and will increase par value per share from $0.0016 to $0.0256. Outstanding ordinary shares will be reduced from approximately 23,671,667 to approximately 1,479,479. The shares will continue to trade on the Nasdaq Capital Market under the ticker EEIQ and will have a new CUSIP G3104J142. No preferred shares are outstanding. No fractional shares will be issued; fractional interests will be paid in cash based on the closing post-split price on the first trading day after the split. The company describes this action in the context of its international growth strategy for its educational institutions.

Positives

  • Ordinary shares will continue to trade on the Nasdaq Capital Market under ticker EEIQ
  • New CUSIP assigned: G3104J142

Negatives

  • Some shareholders will receive cash instead of fractional shares following the reverse split
