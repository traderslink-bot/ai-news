Summary

EpicQuest Education Group International Limited (NASDAQ: EEIQ) announced that Davis University entered an Agency Agreement with Veritas Global Uni (Pvt) Ltd. under which Veritas will act as a non-exclusive recruitment, enrollment and administrative agent for Davis' online Masters of Science in Management and Graduate Foundational Program. The recruitment focus is global with emphasis on China, Hong Kong, Singapore, the Middle East and Southeast Asia (Sri Lanka excluded). Davis remains the sole academic authority and degree-granting institution. EpicQuest says the arrangement supports its internationalization strategy to grow international student enrollment; no financial terms were disclosed.

Positives
  • Adds an international recruitment channel targeting high-growth regions (China, Hong Kong, Singapore, Middle East, Southeast Asia).
  • Veritas will handle recruiting, enrollment and administrative services, potentially increasing student intake efficiency.
  • Davis University remains sole academic authority and degree-granting institution, preserving academic control.
  • EpicQuest highlights this as part of a broader internationalization strategy that could expand enrollment and program reach.
Negatives
  • Agreement is non-exclusive, so Veritas is not the sole recruiter for the programs.
  • Sri Lanka is excluded from the Agreement (separate MOU exists for Sri Lanka).
  • No financial terms, targets, or enrollment commitments were disclosed.
  • The release contains forward-looking statements subject to risks and uncertainties (no assurance of outcomes).
