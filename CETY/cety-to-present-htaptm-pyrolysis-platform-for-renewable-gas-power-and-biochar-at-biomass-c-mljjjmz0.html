Summary

Clean Energy Technologies, Inc. (NASDAQ: CETY) announced it will exhibit at the 19th Annual International Biomass Conference & Expo (March 31–April 2, 2026) in Nashville at Booth #233. The company will present its HTAP™ advanced pyrolysis platform that converts organic and biomass feedstocks into renewable gas, hydrogen-ready syngas, electricity, and biochar. CETY frames the appearance as part of its strategy to commercialize distributed biomass conversion solutions, accelerate project development discussions (including references to Vermont Renewable Gas projects), and pursue partnerships with municipalities, industrial sites, and emerging energy-intensive customers.
