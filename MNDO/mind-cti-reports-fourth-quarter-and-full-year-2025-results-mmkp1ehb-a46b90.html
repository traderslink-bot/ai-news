Summary

MIND C.T.I. LTD. (MNDO) announced Q4 2025 results and full-year 2025 results. Q4 2025 revenue was $4.9 million with net income of $1.0 million and operating income of $0.8 million. Full-year 2025 revenue was $19.4 million with net income of $2.6 million and operating income of $2.1 million. Cash flow from operating activities was $1.2 million in Q4 and $4.0 million for the full year, with cash on hand of about $13.6 million at year-end. The company announced a share repurchase program and venue for its 2026 AGM on May 6, 2026. Management cited challenging market conditions, expected headwinds into 2026, and plans to invest in technology transformation and AI capabilities.

Positives
  • Q4 2025 operating income of $0.8 million (17% of revenue)
  • Q4 2025 cash flow from operating activities of $1.2 million
  • Full-year 2025 net income of $2.6 million ($0.13 per share)
  • Full-year 2025 cash flow from operating activities of $4.0 million
  • Cash position of approximately $13.6 million as of December 31, 2025
  • Follow-on orders from long-time existing customers (license upgrades, expanded scope)
  • Share repurchase program approved
Negatives
  • Q4 2025 revenue of $4.9 million, down from $5.2 million in Q4 2024
  • Full-year 2025 revenue of $19.4 million, down from $21.4 million in 2024
  • Full-year 2025 operating income of $2.1 million (11% of revenue), down from $4.4 million (20%) in 2024
  • Full-year 2025 net income of $2.6 million ($0.13 per share), down from $4.6 million ($0.23 per share) in 2024
  • Market conditions remained challenging with churn and lower spending levels; headwinds expected to weigh on revenues into 2026
  • Pricing pressure from AI-driven solutions affecting spending patterns
