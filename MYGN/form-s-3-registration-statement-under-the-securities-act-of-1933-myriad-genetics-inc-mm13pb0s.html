Summary

Registration statement on Form S-3 filed by Myriad Genetics, Inc. to register up to $200,000,000 aggregate offering of common stock, preferred stock, depositary shares, debt securities and/or warrants on a shelf basis. The prospectus covers general terms; specific terms, prices, underwriters and offering amounts will be provided in future prospectus supplements. Use of proceeds is for general corporate purposes (including operations, R&D, possible debt repayment and acquisitions). This Form S-3 replaces a prior Form S-3ASR because the registrant was no longer a well-known seasoned issuer after filing its 2025 Form 10-K. The filing incorporates the 2025 Annual Report and discloses material risks; offering is subject to supplements and SEC effectiveness.
