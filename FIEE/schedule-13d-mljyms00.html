Summary

This is a Schedule 13D filed by Elements Corporate Services Ltd and Wong Man Ching reporting beneficial ownership of 3,196,343 shares of FiEE, Inc., representing 39.9% of the class (based on 8,010,635 shares deemed outstanding). The stake comprises 3,119,830 common shares and 54,652 shares of Series A Convertible Preferred Stock (convertible into 76,513 common shares at a 1.4 ratio). Securities were acquired from David Lazar pursuant to a Securities Purchase Agreement (including earlier conversions and a warrant exercise); the filing states the Securities were purchased with Elements' working capital (aggregate purchase price reported as $4,130,534.20) and a final closing consideration of $2,891,372.90 for certain securities. The reporting persons state the shares are held for general investment purposes (no present plans to effect specified transactions) but reserve the right to formulate future plans or proposals.

Positives
  • Reporting persons (Elements Corporate Services Ltd and Wong Man Ching) now beneficially own 3,196,343 shares (39.9% of the class)
  • Detailed transaction history provided (Securities Purchase Agreement, note conversion, warrant exercise, final closing)
  • Source of funds disclosed (Elements' working capital) and purchase prices reported
Negatives
  • Series A Convertible Preferred Stock conversion is capped so preferred cannot convert into more than 19.99% of shares outstanding immediately prior to original issuance
  • Although the filing states no present plans to effect specified transactions, the reporting persons expressly reserve the right to pursue future plans or proposals (potential for future changes in control or holdings)
