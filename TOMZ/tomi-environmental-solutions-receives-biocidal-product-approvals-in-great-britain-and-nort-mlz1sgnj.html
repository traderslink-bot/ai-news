Summary

TOMI Environmental Solutions (NASDAQ: TOMZ) announced that the U.K. Health and Safety Executive (HSE) authorized its Binary Ionization Technology (BIT) solution and the SteraMist iHP brand for use in Great Britain under Regulation 528/2012 GB, and a separate approval was granted for Northern Ireland under Regulation (EU) No. 528/2012. The BIT solution is approved for room fogging and handheld direct spray applications across healthcare, industrial, commercial, hospitality and institutional sectors, with established bactericidal, yeasticidal and virucidal efficacy claims (standard contact times ranging from 5 to 15 minutes depending on application and test). TOMI highlights potential further expansion pending expected EU Biocidal Products Regulation approval and reiterates product composition (low-percentage hydrogen peroxide active ingredient).
