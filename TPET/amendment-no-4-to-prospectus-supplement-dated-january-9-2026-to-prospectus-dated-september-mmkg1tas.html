Summary

Trio Petroleum Corp. filed Amendment No. 4 to its prospectus supplement on Form 424B5 to update its at-the-market (ATM) offering capacity under the ATM Sales Agreement with Ladenburg Thalmann & Co. Inc. The company had an original ATM capacity of up to $17,377,000 and, as of this filing, has sold $17,375,884 of common stock (18,139,045 shares) pursuant to the Amended ATM Prospectus Supplement. Due to the General Instruction I.B.6 limitations tied to its public float, Trio reports a public float of $57,050,838 (based on 29,256,840 shares held by non-affiliates and a $1.95 closing price on March 3, 2026) and now may offer and sell additional shares having an aggregate offering price of up to $1,641,000. The common stock is listed on the NYSE American under the ticker TPET; the filing notes a last reported sale price of $1.70 on March 9, 2026. Sales under the amended ATM may be made as at-the-market transactions on NYSE American or other trading markets, through market makers, or in negotiated transactions.
