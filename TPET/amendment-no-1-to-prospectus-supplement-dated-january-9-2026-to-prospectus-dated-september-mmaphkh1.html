Summary

Trio Petroleum Corp. filed a Form 424B5 (Amendment No. 1 to Prospectus Supplement) to update its at-the-market (ATM) offering agreement with Ladenburg Thalmann & Co. Inc. The filing reports that Trio had sold $3,599,884.47 of common stock (7,344,372 shares) under the ATM Agreement. Due to the company's current public float ($20,677,627 based on 18,462,167 shares held by non-affiliates and a $1.12 closing price on March 2, 2026), General Instruction I.B.6 limits future sales to no more than one-third of public float in any 12-month period while public float is under $75 million. After accounting for prior sales, the amendment decreases the remaining offering capacity to up to $3,292,000 (aggregate offering price) available for future at-the-market sales through Ladenburg. The filing confirms sales may be made on NYSE American (ticker TPET) or other market venues and that the sales agent will use commercially reasonable efforts but is not required to sell any specific amount.
