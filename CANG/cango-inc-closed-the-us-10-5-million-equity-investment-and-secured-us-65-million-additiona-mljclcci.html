Summary

Press release: Cango Inc. (NYSE: CANG) closed a previously announced US$10.5 million equity investment from Enduring Wealth Capital Limited (EWCL), issuing 7 million Class B ordinary shares at US$1.50 each (20 votes per share). EWCL's beneficial ownership rose to ~4.71% of total outstanding shares and its voting power to ~49.71%. The company also entered definitive agreements for aggregate additional equity investments of US$65 million from entities wholly owned by Chairman Xin Jin and director Chang-Wei Chiu: Fortune Peak Limited (29,975,137 Class A shares for US$39,567,181) and Armada Network Limited (19,267,287 Class A shares for US$25,432,819) at US$1.32 per Class A share. Upon closing (subject to customary conditions and regulatory approvals, expected February 2026) Mr. Chiu would hold ~11.99% of total shares (6.71% voting power) and Mr. Jin ~4.70% of total shares (2.63% voting power). Proceeds are intended to support Cango's expansion into AI and computing infrastructure and to strengthen the balance sheet.

Positives
  • Closed US$10.5M investment from EWCL, providing immediate capital
  • Definitive agreements in place for an additional US$65M in equity commitments
  • Proceeds earmarked to support expansion into AI and computing infrastructure and to strengthen the balance sheet
  • Transaction prices disclosed: US$1.50 per Class B share to EWCL and US$1.32 per Class A share to insider entities
Negatives
  • Closings of the US$65M insider investments are subject to customary conditions and regulatory approvals
  • Issuance of large numbers of Class A and Class B shares will dilute existing shareholders
  • Significant concentration of voting power with EWCL (~49.71% voting power)
  • Additional equity investments are from entities owned by the Chairman and a director (related-party transactions)
