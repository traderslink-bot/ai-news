Summary

ESS Tech, Inc. (NYSE: GWH) announced a collaboration framework with Salt River Project (SRP) and Google for Project New Horizon, a 5 MW / 50 MWh pilot deploying ESS's iron flow Energy Base long-duration energy storage at SRP's Copper Crossing Energy and Research Center in Florence, Arizona. The pilot, awarded through SRP's competitive solicitation, is intended to evaluate non-lithium LDES operational performance with EPRI providing independent oversight; Google will cost-share a portion of project payments. Design is underway, manufacturing is expected to begin in 2026, and delivery is targeted for December 2027. ESS positions the pilot as a commercial validation milestone for its next-generation utility-scale Energy Base platform and a potential pathway to follow-on opportunities with utilities and large energy users.
