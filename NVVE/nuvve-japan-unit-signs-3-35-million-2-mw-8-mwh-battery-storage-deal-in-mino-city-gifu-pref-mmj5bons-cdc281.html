Summary

NUVVE JAPAN K.K. signed a definitive sales agreement for a 2-MW, 8-MWh battery energy storage system in Mino City, Gifu Prefecture, with a contracted value of about $3.35 million. Operations are expected to begin in Q4 2026. The project will use the Nuvve GIVe platform, participate in Japan's Supply and Demand Adjustment Market and the Japan Electric Power Exchange, and includes an upfront deposit of about $928,000. It is the subsidiary's first grid-scale storage sale in Japan and supports Nuvve's expansion into Japan's growing energy storage market; the company also has a separate collaboration with OMNIA Global on future European projects.
