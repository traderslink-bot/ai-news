Summary

Nuvve Holding Corp. (NASDAQ: NVVE) and OMNIA Global announced a collaboration to develop more than 1 gigawatt of battery energy storage projects over the next two years, starting with a 50 MW / 75 MWh CE-approved system in Sweden that is expected to begin operations in Q2 2026. Nuvve will own and manage the facility and provide market access services to optimize revenue and grid participation, expanding its presence in Europe’s grid-scale energy storage market. The project aligns with Nuvve’s strategy to deploy scalable, revenue-generating energy storage globally and follows Sweden’s strong renewable deployment and market support for storage. The stock rose 2.4% in premarket trading to $1.28 and was trading near its 52-week low.
