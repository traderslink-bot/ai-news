Summary

22nd Century Group, Inc. reports continued early sales momentum for VLN low-nicotine cigarettes in the U.S. In Q4 2025, about 8,800 cartons were distributed across roughly 1,700 new retail outlets. The company plans to reach more than 5,000 distribution points in 2026 by adding new retail partners. VLN is FDA-authorized to reduce nicotine, with MRTP-backed claims, and the company notes regulatory momentum and international interest in nicotine reduction products. Three VLN brand presentations are in market: VLN, Pinnacle VLN, and Smoker Friendly VLN.
