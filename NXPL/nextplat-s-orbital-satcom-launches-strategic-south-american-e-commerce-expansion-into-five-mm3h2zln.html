Summary

NextPlat Corp (NASDAQ: NXPL, NXPLW) announced that its Orbital Satcom unit launched online storefronts on Mercado Libre in five Latin American markets (Mexico, Brazil, Argentina, Chile, Colombia) to sell satellite and wireless connectivity products operating on Globalstar, Iridium and Inmarsat networks. The company says the move targets large underserved connectivity needs across a region of ~440 million people, with additional South American sites planned for Q2 2026. The release is a company press announcement and contains forward-looking statement disclaimers; no financial projections or revenue impacts were disclosed.
