Summary

Press release: NextPlat (NASDAQ: NXPL, NXPLW) says it expects approximately $55 million in 2025 revenue and reports sequential improvement in key metrics for Q4 2025 versus Q3 2025. The company expects Q4 2025 340B revenue to increase more than 90% sequentially and total prescription volume to rise ~7% sequentially. Consolidated gross margin is expected to be roughly flat Q4/Q3 but management forecasts significant margin expansion to ~20%-25% in Q1 2026 as it shifts toward higher-margin contracted services. NextPlat reports approximately $13 million cash, ~$15 million working capital and expects zero unsecured debt as of Dec 31, 2025. The company is implementing cost reductions (facility closures, headcount and consultant reductions, logistics efficiencies), anticipates reduced operating losses by end of Q2 2026, and plans to file its Form 10-K for year-ended Dec 31, 2025 on or before March 31, 2026. Results are preliminary and subject to the year-end audit.
