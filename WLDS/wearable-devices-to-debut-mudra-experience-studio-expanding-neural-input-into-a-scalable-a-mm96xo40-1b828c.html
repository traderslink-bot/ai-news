Summary

Wearable Devices Ltd. will participate in MWC Barcelona 2026 to debut the Mudra Experience Studio, a platform for neural input experiences for XR and AI applications, and to showcase Mudra Link, a neural wristband. The event runs March 2–5, 2026 at Hall 5, Booth 5E61. The Mudra ecosystem enables gesture control across Android, iOS, and PC, with prior 2025 native compatibility for Rokid, RayNeo, and Viture smart glasses. The company trades under the symbols WLDS and WLDSW on Nasdaq.
