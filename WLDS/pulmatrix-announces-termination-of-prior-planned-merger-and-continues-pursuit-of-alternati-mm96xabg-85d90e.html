Summary

Pulmatrix, Inc. (Nasdaq: PULM) announced that Cullgen Inc. notified Pulmatrix on February 28, 2026 that it was terminating the Merger Agreement. Pulmatrix said it will continue to pursue alternative merger opportunities. The previously announced merger with Cullgen was entered November 13, 2024 (amended in 2025) and was conditioned on regulatory approvals, including from the China Securities Regulatory Commission, which had experienced delays. Pulmatrix indicated it is exploring a reverse merger and noted increased industry activity in merger transactions. Pulmatrix’s pipeline centres on its iSPERSE™ technology with assets PUR3100 (acute migraine, Phase-related IND), PUR1800 (AECOPD), and PUR1900 (itraconazole inhalation).

Positives

  • Recent increase in transaction activity within the biotech/merger space
  • Pipeline assets with IND status and international development progress
Negatives
  • Termination of the Cullgen merger creates uncertainty about a near-term strategic transaction
  • Regulatory approvals from CSRC were not obtained, contributing to termination
