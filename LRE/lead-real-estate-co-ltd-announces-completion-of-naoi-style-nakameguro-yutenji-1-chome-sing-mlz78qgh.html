Summary

Lead Real Estate Co., Ltd (LRE) reports the completion of its Naoi Style Nakameguro Yutenji 1-Chome single-family home in Meguro, Tokyo. The three-story wooden residence sits on ~120.17 sqm (~1,293 sq ft) of land with ~182.73 sqm (~1,967 sq ft) total floor area and a built-in two-car garage. Features include solar panels with battery storage, a mist sauna, underfloor heating, ECO-ONE hybrid water heating, a SECOM security system, electric locking, a delivery box, and curated furnishings. Completion certificate received (No. JE25BC1-13-0473); construction began February 2025 and marketing activities have begun. The property is located near Nakameguro and Yutenji stations. CEO Eiji Nagahara commented on continued execution in the luxury single-family segment and disciplined development.
