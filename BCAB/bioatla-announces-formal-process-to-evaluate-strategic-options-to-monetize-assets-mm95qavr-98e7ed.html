Summary

BioAtla, Inc. (NASDAQ: BCAB) board initiated a formal process to evaluate strategic options to monetize assets, including potential sale of preclinical and clinical assets, licensing transactions, strategic partnerships or other corporate transactions. The company is also implementing a restructuring plan to significantly reduce operating expenses, including a workforce reduction of about 70% and cost containment measures; essential employees will be retained. Tungsten Advisors has been engaged as exclusive strategic financial advisor. There is no guarantee a transaction will result, and updates will be provided only if the board approves a specific action or determines disclosure is appropriate.
