Summary

Klaviyo (NYSE: KVYO) announced its board approved a share repurchase program authorizing up to $500 million of its Series A Common Stock and that the company will imminently enter into a $100 million accelerated share repurchase (ASR). Management highlighted strong 2025 growth, expanding profitability, a strong balance sheet and consistent cash generation, and said the company will continue investing in AI-driven innovation and platform expansion while returning capital when appropriate.
