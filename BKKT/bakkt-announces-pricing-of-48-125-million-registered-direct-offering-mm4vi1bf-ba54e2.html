Summary

Bakkt priced a registered direct offering of 3,024,799 shares of Class A common stock and up to 2,475,201 pre-funded warrants at $8.75 per share (and $8.7499 per warrant), with gross proceeds expected to be $48.125 million. The offering is to a single institutional investor and is expected to close on or around March 2, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. Proceeds are targeted for working capital, general corporate purposes and strategic initiatives. Cohen & Company Capital Markets is the sole placement agent. The offering is being conducted under a shelf registration statement, with a final prospectus supplement to be filed. Sales are restricted to jurisdictions where lawful.
