Summary

Bakkt, Inc. (NYSE: BKKT) priced a registered direct offering of 3,024,799 shares of Class A common stock and pre-funded warrants to purchase up to 2,475,201 shares at $8.75 per share and $8.7499 per pre-funded warrant (reflecting a $0.0001 exercise price). The offering to a single institutional investor is expected to close on or about March 2, 2026, subject to customary conditions. Gross proceeds are expected to be $48.125 million before fees and expenses. Net proceeds are intended for working capital, general corporate purposes and strategic initiatives. Cohen & Company Capital Markets is sole placement agent. The offering is being made pursuant to a shelf registration statement on Form S-3 (File No. 333-288361).
