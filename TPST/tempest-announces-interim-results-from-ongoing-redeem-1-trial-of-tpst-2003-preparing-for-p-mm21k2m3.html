Summary

Tempest Therapeutics (NASDAQ: TPST) reported interim Phase 1/2a REDEEM-1 results for TPST-2003, a CD19/BCMA parallel dual-targeting CAR-T developed by partner Novatim. As of Jan 31, 2026, 36 rrMM patients have been treated across two studies (24 in a prior IIT, 12 in REDEEM-1). All six efficacy-evaluable patients in REDEEM-1 achieved complete response (CR); across both studies 25/25 evaluable patients with measurable disease had an overall response rate (ORR) of 100%. Prior IIT data (19 evaluable) showed ORR 100% and CR 89.5%, with median PFS 23.1 months (including patients with extramedullary disease). Safety: no Grade >=3 CRS or ICANS observed as of data cutoffs; one low-grade ICANS at highest dose. Tempest plans to submit a U.S. IND and, subject to clearance, pursue a U.S. registrational study in 2026 and will present full data later in 2026. Tempest retains ex-China/India/Turkey/Russia rights.
