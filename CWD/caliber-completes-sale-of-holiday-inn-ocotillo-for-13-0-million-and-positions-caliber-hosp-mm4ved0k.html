Summary

Caliber (NASDAQ: CWD) announced the sale of the Holiday Inn Ocotillo in the Phoenix–Chandler submarket for $13.0 million. The asset was owned by Caliber Hospitality Trust, Inc. (CHT), Caliber's private Up-C vehicle focused on branded, cash-flowing hotels. Caliber says it will recycle proceeds, raise new equity through its capital markets platform and institutional partners, and pursue expansion of CHT in 2026–2027. CHT’s strategy emphasizes tax-efficient contribution transactions, value-add acquisitions, debt restructuring, renovations, and institutional asset management; growth of the Trust is expected to generate asset management fees, performance incentives and potential balance-sheet participation for Caliber. The release notes sector headwinds in 2024–2025, potential liquidity pathways for CHT (including a non-traded REIT and a long-term public listing), and includes standard forward-looking statement disclosures.
