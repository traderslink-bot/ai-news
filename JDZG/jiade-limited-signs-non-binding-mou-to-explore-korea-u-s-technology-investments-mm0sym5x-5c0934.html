Summary

JIADE LIMITED (NASDAQ: JDZG) announced a non-binding memorandum of understanding with Chinalink Education Group to explore cross-border investment opportunities in high-growth technology sectors, focusing on Korea–U.S. collaboration to identify and invest in Korean technology enterprises. The MOU contemplates a strategic capital pool of up to US$5 million and investments in AI-related sectors, including robotics and AI-powered education platforms, subject to definitive agreements.

Positives
  • Non-binding agreement to explore cross-border technology investments with Chinalink Education Group
  • Potential strategic capital pool of up to US$5 million
  • Focus on AI-integrated industries, including robotics and AI-enabled education platforms
  • Exploration of investments in AI-powered education platforms and cross-border education infrastructure
  • Korea–U.S.–focused collaboration aiming to identify internationally expandable technology opportunities
Negatives
