Summary

JIADE LIMITED (NASDAQ: JDZG) entered a Securities Purchase Agreement to sell 12,000,000 Class A ordinary shares (or pre-funded warrants in lieu) at $0.25 per share in a registered direct offering, producing expected gross proceeds of approximately $3.0 million. Pre-funded warrants have an exercise price of $0.0001. Investors have an option to purchase up to an additional 48,000,000 shares (or pre-funded warrants) on or before the 30th calendar day after the initial closing if they deliver written notices. The initial closing is expected on or about February 19, 2026, subject to customary conditions. The shares/warrants are being sold under a Form F-3 shelf registration (File No. 333-292574) declared effective January 13, 2026. Univest Securities, LLC is sole placement agent. A prospectus supplement will be filed with the SEC.

Positives
  • Raises approximately $3.0 million in gross proceeds at the initial closing
  • Offered under an SEC-declared effective Form F-3 shelf registration (File No. 333-292574)
  • Univest Securities, LLC engaged as sole placement agent
  • Use of pre-funded warrants allows purchasers to avoid ownership limits while providing near-immediate equity exposure (exercise price $0.0001)
Negatives
  • Issuance of 12,000,000 shares at $0.25 will dilute existing shareholders
  • Investors have option to purchase up to an additional 48,000,000 shares within 30 days, which could substantially increase dilution
  • Offering price of $0.25 per share may exert downward pressure on market price
