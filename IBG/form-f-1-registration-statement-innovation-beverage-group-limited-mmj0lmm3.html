Summary

SEC filing: Form F-1 registration statement by Innovation Beverage Group Limited registering up to $15.0 million of securities through a best-efforts offering of up to 4,166,667 Ordinary Units (each unit = one Ordinary Share, one Series A Warrant and one Series B Warrant) or Pre-Funded Units. Assumed public offering price is $3.60 per unit (closing price Feb 27, 2026); Series A and B Warrants exercise price $3.60, five-year term; Pre-Funded Warrants exercise price $0.00001. The filing discloses up to 4,166,667 shares underlying each warrant series, Ordinary Shares outstanding immediately before the offering of 1,068,881 shares and pro forma shares outstanding after the offering of 5,235,548 shares (assuming maximum sale, no pre-funded units, no warrant exercises). Estimated net proceeds if maximum sold: approx. $12.87 million after fees; $2.5 million of net proceeds are intended to be lent to Blockfuel to enable Blockfuel to exercise a call right (IBG expects to obtain ~51% of Blockfuel), with remaining proceeds for capex, sales & marketing, working capital. Placement agent: Aegis Capital Corp.; fees: 8% cash fee + 1% non-accountable expense allowance + reimbursement of up to $125,000. Ordinary Shares listed on Nasdaq under ticker IBG. Filing notes emerging growth and foreign private issuer status and risk factors including that this is a best-efforts offering with no minimum.
