Summary

Innovation Beverage Group Ltd (NASDAQ: IBG) provided a corporate update on its proposed merger with BlockFuel Energy Inc. The companies remain on track to close the merger in Q1 2026, subject to approvals. Operationally, 10 wells are back in production and seven more are expected to be returned to production by month-end; BlockFuel expects first oil and gas sales in February 2026 with initial revenues anticipated before March 31, 2026. The combined strategy targets a vertically integrated business monetizing hydrocarbons via traditional sales and onsite gas-to-power digital infrastructure (including modular Bitcoin mining), which management believes can lower effective energy costs and create incremental cash flow per well. BlockFuel signed a Letter of Intent to acquire additional nearby producing fields adding ~4,000 contiguous acres. All milestones remain subject to customary closing conditions, financing, commodity, regulatory and operational risks.

Positives
  • Merger with BlockFuel Energy targeted to close in Q1 2026 (subject to approvals).
  • Production restart underway: 10 wells active with 7 more expected back by month-end.
  • First oil and gas sales expected in February 2026; initial revenues anticipated before end of Q1 2026.
  • Dual revenue model: conventional hydrocarbon sales plus potential onsite gas-to-power and bitcoin mining to enhance margins.
  • LOI to acquire ~4,000 contiguous acres to expand producing asset base and scale operations.
Negatives
  • Merger and milestones are forward-looking and subject to customary approvals and closing conditions.
  • Execution risks including financing, regulatory approvals (including for digital assets), operational setbacks and commodity price volatility.
  • Planned digital mining deployment and cost advantages are projections and not guaranteed.
