Summary

LegalZoom (NASDAQ: LZ) announced a press release that it has launched the "LegalZoom Connector," integrating its attorney-services into Anthropic's Claude AI ecosystem. The Connector routes Claude users to LegalZoom’s network of independent attorneys when Claude flags high-risk clauses or complex compliance issues, packaging Claude’s analysis and conversation context for the attorney. LegalZoom frames this as a human-in-the-loop model offering ongoing legal advice for a semi-annual fee (claimed to cost less than a typical law-firm hour), removing hourly billing and retainers. The release provides no specific pricing, launch metrics, or quantified financial impact.
