Summary

Atomera Incorporated (NASDAQ: ATOM) announced a registered direct offering of 5,000,000 shares of common stock at $5.00 per share for expected gross proceeds of approximately $25 million before placement agent fees and offering expenses. The offering is expected to close on or about February 24, 2026, subject to customary closing conditions. Craig-Hallum is acting as sole placement agent. The shares are being offered pursuant to Atomera's Form S-3 shelf registration (File No. 333-287603). Atomera intends to use net proceeds for working capital and general corporate purposes; a prospectus supplement will be filed with the SEC.
