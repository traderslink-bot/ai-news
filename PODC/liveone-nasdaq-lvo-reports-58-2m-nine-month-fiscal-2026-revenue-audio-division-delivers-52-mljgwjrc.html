Summary

Press release reporting LiveOne's Q3 Fiscal 2026 and nine-month results: Q3 revenue $20.3M and Adjusted EBITDA $1.6M; Audio Division Q3 revenue $18.6M and Adjusted EBITDA $2.6M. Nine-month revenue $58.2M; nine-month Adjusted EBITDA was negative $1.2M and nine-month net loss $13.7M. Management cites AI-driven efficiencies that cut quarterly operating expenses 52% year-over-year and reduced headcount from ~350 to 88. Company expanded share repurchases (approximately $6M remaining under current authorization) and acquired 771,000 PodcastOne shares during fiscal 2026 (186,636 in Q3 at $2.17 avg). Strategic highlights include Fortune 250 partner revenue at a >$27M annual run rate, >1.3M Tesla ad-supported users, ARPU increase ~60% and improved Premium conversions. Audio Division preliminary Fiscal 2027 guidance: revenue $85–$95M+ and Adjusted EBITDA $8–$10M+. Conference call scheduled Feb 12, 2026.
