Summary

Press release: PodcastOne reported record Q3 Fiscal 2026 revenue of $15.9M (up 25% YoY) and Adjusted EBITDA of $2.8M (up 516% YoY). For the nine months ended Dec 31, 2025, revenue was $46.0M (up 21% YoY) and Adjusted EBITDA was $4.5M (up 421% YoY). Cash balance increased 217% year-over-year to $3.4M. Company raised full Fiscal 2026 guidance to revenue $58–$60M and Adjusted EBITDA $5–$6M, and provided preliminary Fiscal 2027 guidance of revenue $68–$75M and Adjusted EBITDA $6–$10M. Operational highlights include adding 25 podcasts YTD, maintaining a Top 10 Podtrac publisher ranking, expanded Amazon (Art19) partnership to $20M+ annual run rate, and a Fortune 250 partner at $27M+ annual run rate. GAAP net loss for the three months was $0.154M and $2.183M year-to-date; company provides Adjusted EBITDA reconciliations.
