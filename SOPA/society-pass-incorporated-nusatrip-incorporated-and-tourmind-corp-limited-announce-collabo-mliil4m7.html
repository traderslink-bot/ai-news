Summary

Press release (Feb 11, 2026): Society Pass (NASDAQ: SOPA) and its majority-owned subsidiary NusaTrip (NASDAQ: NUTR) announced a strategic collaboration with Tourmind Corp Limited to jointly develop technology solutions for travel wholesalers and the global B2B travel sector. The partnership will combine NusaTrip's travel inventory and network (500+ airlines, 650,000 hotels) with Tourmind's technology expertise to build infrastructure and tools—including AI-enabled distribution enhancements—initially for select partners with broader rollout planned later. No financial terms, timelines or revenue targets were disclosed.
