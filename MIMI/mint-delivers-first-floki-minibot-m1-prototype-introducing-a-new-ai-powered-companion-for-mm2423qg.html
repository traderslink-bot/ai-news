Summary

Press release (Feb 25, 2026): Mint Incorporation Limited (NASDAQ: MIMI) announced delivery of the first prototype of the FLOKI Minibot M1, an AI-powered companion robot co-developed by Mint's Axonex Intelligence Limited and Rice Robotics. The prototype was showcased at the British Education Training and Technology Show. Axonex and Rice Robotics have an agreement to deliver up to 800 FLOKI Minibot M1 units by the end of Q1 2026. Mint and certain investors have agreed in principle to increase proposed investment for a strategic joint venture with Rice Robotics from HK$10 million to HK$15 million, subject to definitive documentation and customary closing conditions. The product targets consumer B2C markets in the Asia Pacific with planned applications in education, hospitality and entertainment as part of Mint's strategic shift into AI and robotics.
