Summary

Mint Incorporation Limited (NASDAQ: MIMI) announced that its wholly-owned subsidiary Axonex Intelligence Limited (AXONEX) has entered a joint venture with Synergy Technology Group Limited (STG) to form Axonex Automation Limited in Hong Kong. AXONEX will own 80% and STG 20% of the JV. AXONEX has committed up to HK$20,000,000 of capital to be provided in four tranches over an expected 12–24 month period subject to achievement of defined business and technical milestones; STG will license its Zhishan No-Code Digital Twin platform and related drone inspection technologies to the JV. The JV will target commercialization in infrastructure inspection, utilities, smart cities and industrial operations in Hong Kong and selected overseas markets.
