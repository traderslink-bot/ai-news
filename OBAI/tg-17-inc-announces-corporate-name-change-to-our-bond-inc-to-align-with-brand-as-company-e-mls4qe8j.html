Summary

TG-17, Inc. (NASDAQ: OBAI) announced a corporate name change to Our Bond, Inc. to align its corporate identity with its consumer and enterprise brand as it expands sales, marketing and public awareness efforts. The company says operations, strategy, management and mission remain unchanged; the ticker symbol and CUSIP for its common stock will remain the same and no shareholder action is required. The release highlights that Bond has invested more than $100 million in its AI-powered preventative personal security platform, handled over 1.4 million security service requests (including 10,000+ emergencies), and operates in 28 countries with enterprise and municipal customers.

  • Significant investment in platform: >$100 million

  • Global footprint: operations in 28 countries and existing enterprise customers
