Summary

Hyperscale Data, Inc. (NYSE American: GPUS) announced an at-the-market offering program to sell shares of its 13% Series D Cumulative Redeemable Perpetual Preferred Stock for aggregate gross proceeds up to approximately $35.4 million. Shares will be sold from time to time through Wilson-Davis & Co., Inc. as sales agent, including ordinary broker transactions on the NYSE American. The company intends to use a majority of net proceeds, if any, to acquire Bitcoin and potentially precious metals (gold, silver and/or copper), with a smaller portion for working capital and general corporate purposes (which may include repayment, refinancing, redemption or repurchase of indebtedness or capital stock). The Preferred Stock is being offered under the company’s shelf registration statement (File No. 333-291595), effective December 11, 2025; offerings will be made by prospectus/prospectus supplement available on EDGAR.

Positives
  • Up to $35.4 million of potential gross proceeds available via ATM program
  • Proceeds targeted primarily to acquire Bitcoin and potentially precious metals (asset purchases)
  • Offering is covered by an effective shelf registration (File No. 333-291595) and will be sold through a named sales agent (Wilson-Davis & Co.)
Negatives
  • Issuance of preferred shares may dilute existing equity holders' economic and voting interests
  • Management has broad discretion over timing and application of net proceeds (uses not fully specified)
  • Forward-looking statements and intended uses subject to risks and uncertainties; no assurance proceeds will be used as described
