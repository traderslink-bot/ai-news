Summary

Faruqi & Faruqi, LLP is investigating potential securities claims against Vistagen Therapeutics, Inc. (VTGN) and reminds investors of a March 16, 2026 deadline to seek lead plaintiff status in a federal securities class action alleging false or misleading statements and/or failure to disclose material adverse facts related to the Phase 3 PALISADE-3 study of fasedienol for the acute treatment of social anxiety disorder. The complaint alleges violations of federal securities laws. On December 17, 2025, PALISADE-3 topline results indicated the study failed to meet its primary efficacy endpoint (no statistically significant improvement on SUDS scores vs placebo). The stock closed at $0.86, down 80.27% for the day. The court-appointed lead plaintiff is the investor with the largest financial interest in the relief sought by the class. Investors may join the suit or remain absent class members. The firm invites information from whistleblowers, former employees, shareholders and others.
