Summary

Press release: Waystar reported Q4 2025 revenue of $303.5M (up 24% YoY), GAAP net income of $20.0M and non-GAAP net income of $70.7M; Q4 adjusted EBITDA was $129.1M (43% margin). FY 2025 revenue was $1,099.3M (up 17% YoY), GAAP net income $112.1M, non-GAAP net income $262.9M, and adjusted EBITDA $462.1M (42% margin). Key operating metrics: net revenue retention 112%, 1,391 clients >$100k LTM revenue, FY subscription revenue $558.4M (+22% YoY) and volume-based revenue $534.8M (+11% YoY). 2026 guidance: total revenue $1.274B–$1.294B, adjusted EBITDA $530M–$540M, non-GAAP net income $317M–$335M, diluted non-GAAP EPS $1.59–$1.68. Company highlights integration of Iodine acquisition and AI product focus. The release notes cash flow, balance sheet and non-GAAP reconciliations and references a related Form 8-K filed Feb 17, 2026.

Positives
  • Q4 revenue $303.5M, +24% YoY; FY revenue $1,099.3M, +17% YoY
  • Strong adjusted EBITDA margins: Q4 43%, FY 42%; FY adjusted EBITDA $462.1M
  • Positive GAAP net income in FY 2025 of $112.1M and Q4 GAAP net income $20.0M
  • High net revenue retention (NRR) of 112% and growth in clients contributing >$100k (1,391, +16% YoY)
  • Subscription revenue growth: Q4 +38% YoY, FY +22% YoY
  • Provided 2026 guidance showing revenue and profitability expansion
Negatives
  • Net debt of $1,395.0M as of Dec 31, 2025 and adjusted net leverage ratio ~3.0x
  • Significant interest expense (FY interest expense $77.5M) and elevated long-term debt balances
  • Cash and cash equivalents decreased to $61.4M from $182.1M year-over-year
  • Large cash use for acquisitions: $629.5M of acquisitions, net of cash acquired, in FY 2025
  • Diluted weighted-average shares outstanding increased (diluted 184.8M for FY 2025)
